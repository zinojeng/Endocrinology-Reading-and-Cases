## 🩺 臨床情境 Clinical Scenario

本章附有三則臨床案例（Clinical Case Vignettes），分別呈現轉銜期最常見的三種決策難題。以下先呈現案例情境，詳細解析與正解見「個案解析與臨床推理」章節。

### Case 1
An 18-year-old woman was diagnosed with a craniopharyngioma at age 14 years, and she underwent surgical resection, with resultant panhypopituitarism. She has been on a replacement regimen with levothyroxine, hydrocortisone, and GH. An oral contraceptive was recently commenced for estrogen and progesterone replacement. Over the past 12 months, she has only grown 1 cm, and her height now is 62 in (157.5 cm). A recent X-ray of her hand and wrist shows almost complete epiphyseal closure. She ran out of GH and has been off therapy for 3 months. Her most recent IGF-1 SDS is –2.4. She inquires if she still needs to continue GH treatment, as she is "tired" of the daily GH injections.

> 一名 18 歲女性於 14 歲時診斷為 craniopharyngioma，接受手術切除，術後產生 panhypopituitarism。她一直使用 levothyroxine、hydrocortisone 與 GH 進行荷爾蒙補充。近期因 estrogen 與 progesterone 補充需求開始服用口服避孕藥。過去 12 個月身高僅增加 1 cm，目前身高為 62 吋（157.5 cm）。近期手腕 X 光顯示 epiphyseal（生長板）幾近完全閉合。她的 GH 已用完並停藥 3 個月，最近一次 IGF-1 SDS 為 –2.4。她詢問是否仍需繼續 GH 治療，因為她已「厭倦」每日的 GH 注射。

### Case 2
A 19-year-old man is seen in the clinic for evaluation of headaches, decreased libido, and worsening fatigue. MRI reveals a 12-mm Rathke cleft cyst. On further questioning, he recalls a period 2 years ago when he experienced increased thirst and urinary frequency that has since resolved. On physical examination, he has a sallow complexion, minimal facial hair, and a normal-sized phallus and testes.

> 一名 19 歲男性因頭痛、性慾下降及日益加重的疲倦前來就診。MRI 顯示一顆 12 mm 的 Rathke cleft cyst。進一步詢問，他回想起 2 年前曾有一段時間出現口渴增加與頻尿，之後自行緩解。理學檢查發現膚色蒼黃（sallow complexion）、面部毛髮稀少，陰莖與睪丸大小正常。

### Case 3
A 20-year-old man who underwent surgical removal of a nonfunctioning pituitary macroadenoma 3 years ago has developed hypopituitarism. He currently takes levothyroxine, hydrocortisone, and injectable testosterone cypionate. He transitioned to adult services 1 year ago. He has attended today's clinic visit alone and is fully independent. He has started to question his treatments because he finds it difficult to take his medications regularly. He has a normal libido and erectile function and is considering fertility in the near future. On physical examination, he is well virilized with 18 cc testes bilaterally with normal consistency.

> 一名 20 歲男性，3 年前接受 nonfunctioning pituitary macroadenoma 手術切除，之後產生 hypopituitarism。目前使用 levothyroxine、hydrocortisone 與注射型 testosterone cypionate。他於 1 年前轉銜至成人照護服務。今日獨自就診且完全獨立生活。他因難以規律服藥而開始質疑自己的治療。他的性慾與勃起功能正常，並考慮在近期生育。理學檢查顯示男性化良好，雙側睪丸各 18 cc、質地正常。

## 🔬 背景與重要性 Background & Significance

### 轉銜的定義與複雜性 Significance of the Clinical Problem
Transition refers to the structured process that ensures continuity of health care for adolescents with complex chronic medical conditions as they move from pediatric to adult health services. The complexity of this process is well recognized, including the lack of standardized diagnostic criteria, high treatment costs, and limited experience among clinicians managing rare pituitary tumors.

> 轉銜（transition）是指確保患有複雜慢性疾病的青少年，在從兒科轉移至成人照護服務時能維持照護連續性的一套結構化流程。此流程的複雜性已被廣泛認識，包括缺乏標準化診斷準則、治療費用高昂，以及臨床醫師在處理罕見 pituitary tumors 上的經驗有限。

Managing patients in transition requires periodic testing to reassess hormonal status and ongoing monitoring of signs and symptoms to guide hormonal dosage adjustments. Individualized treatment planning is essential, as some adult patients might benefit more from hormone replacement than others, whereas others, such as those with idiopathic GH deficiency (GHD), may experience recovery of endogenous GH secretion by the time adult height is achieved and therefore can experience adverse effects if treated with GH when not indicated.¹

> 處置轉銜期患者需要定期檢測以重新評估荷爾蒙狀態，並持續監測症狀與徵象以調整荷爾蒙劑量。個別化的治療計畫至關重要，因為部分成人患者比其他人更能從荷爾蒙補充中獲益；而另一些患者，例如 idiopathic GH deficiency (GHD) 者，可能在達到成人身高時已恢復內源性 GH 分泌，若在無適應症下仍給予 GH，反而可能產生不良反應。¹

Transition age marks the period between the end of puberty and the attainment of peak bone mass.² It is a time of physical changes, including growth and sexual maturation, as well as pronounced cognitive and emotional development. Young people begin to navigate adult relationships, employment, tertiary education, independent living, and a social life, which may include alcohol and recreational drugs.

> 轉銜年齡（transition age）標誌著從青春期結束到達到 peak bone mass（尖峰骨量）之間的時期。² 這是身體變化的時期，包括生長與性成熟，以及顯著的認知與情緒發展。年輕人開始面對成人關係、就業、高等教育、獨立生活與社交生活，其中可能包含飲酒與娛樂性藥物。這對健康的人已屬複雜階段，對慢性病患者更是艱難。

### 兒童與青少年腦下垂體腫瘤的特性
Pituitary tumors are rare in children and adolescents, especially before puberty, and are generally more aggressive, treatment-resistant, or part of a genetic syndrome. These tumors are characterized in the same way as in adults: nonfunctioning (eg, craniopharyngioma and nonfunctioning pituitary adenomas [NFPAs]) or functioning (eg, prolactinomas, Cushing disease, somatotropinomas, and, rarely, TSHomas). Typical endocrine sequelae in pituitary tumor survivors include persistence or recurrence of functioning pituitary tumors and/or hypopituitarism.³

> Pituitary tumors 在兒童與青少年中相當罕見，尤其在青春期之前，且通常更具侵襲性、對治療有抗性，或屬於某種遺傳症候群的一部分。這些腫瘤的分類與成人相同：nonfunctioning（如 craniopharyngioma 與 nonfunctioning pituitary adenomas [NFPAs]）或 functioning（如 prolactinomas、Cushing disease、somatotropinomas，以及罕見的 TSHomas）。腦下垂體腫瘤存活者常見的內分泌後遺症包括 functioning 腫瘤的持續存在或復發，以及/或 hypopituitarism。³

During the transition period, special attention should be directed to growth, puberty, fertility, and lifestyle. Currently, no specific studies exist on optimizing care when transitioning the care of pituitary tumor survivors. Existing transition processes are adapted from models used for other chronic childhood-onset diseases (eg, diabetes and cystic fibrosis).⁴⁻⁶ Given the variability in patient readiness, cognitive function, comorbidities, health care access, insurance coverage, and availability of local expertise, transitioning the care of pituitary tumor survivors requires an individualized, multidisciplinary approach. Close collaboration between pediatric and adult endocrinologists is essential to ensure a seamless, effective transition of clinical care.⁷

> 在轉銜期間，應特別關注生長、青春期發育、生育力與生活型態。目前尚無針對腦下垂體腫瘤存活者照護最佳化的專門研究。現行的轉銜流程多是改編自其他兒童期發病慢性疾病（如 diabetes 與 cystic fibrosis）所使用的模式。⁴⁻⁶ 由於病人準備度、認知功能、共病、就醫可近性、保險給付與在地專業資源的差異甚大，腦下垂體腫瘤存活者的照護轉銜需要個別化、多專科的方式。兒科與成人內分泌科醫師間的密切合作，是確保臨床照護能無縫且有效轉銜的關鍵。⁷

### 實務落差 Practice Gaps
- Despite the availability of clinical guidelines for several chronic childhood-onset diseases, there remains uncertainty and variation in the approach to transitioning the care of pituitary tumor survivors to adult endocrine care services.
  - 儘管多種兒童期發病慢性疾病已有臨床指引提供轉銜架構，腦下垂體腫瘤存活者轉銜至成人內分泌照護的做法仍存在不確定性與差異。
- Transitioning pediatric patients with varying levels of readiness, cognitive abilities, coexisting comorbidities, and health care access creates significant challenges.
  - 病人在準備度、認知能力、共病與就醫可近性上的差異，為成功轉銜帶來重大挑戰。
- Lack of standardized diagnostic criteria, cost of treatment, and unfamiliarity or inexperience of clinicians treating rare childhood-onset pituitary disorders compound the challenges.
  - 缺乏標準化診斷準則、治療費用，以及兒科與成人醫師對罕見且表現細微的兒童期發病腦下垂體疾病不熟悉或經驗不足，加重了轉銜的困難。
- Patients at risk of evolving pituitary endocrinopathies may not have been diagnosed or may have had suboptimal biochemical confirmation and inappropriate treatment, leading to later comorbidities.
  - 具有內分泌病變演變風險的患者可能未被診斷，或生化確認不足而接受不當治療，導致日後的共病。

### 轉銜模式與流程 Transition Models
In pediatric care, medical responsibility is largely shared between the physician and the family; however, as these patients transition into adulthood, they are expected to assume ownership of their own health care.⁸ Adequate preparation for adult-oriented care can reduce loss to follow-up and lower the risk of developing complications and comorbidities in adulthood.⁸

> 在兒科照護中，醫療責任主要由醫師與家庭共同承擔；然而當這些患者進入成年期，他們被期待要為自己的健康照護負起主導責任。⁸ 充分的成人導向照護準備可以減少失聯（loss to follow-up），並降低成年期發生併發症與共病的風險。⁸

The transition process is recommended to start in early adolescence (age 12) and should address 6 core elements: development of a transition policy or guide (ages 12–14), patient tracking and monitoring (ages 14–18), assessment of transition readiness (ages 14–18), transition planning (ages 14–18), transfer of care (ages 18–21), and confirmation of transition completion (ages 18–23).⁹ In the United Kingdom, the National Health Service has implemented the *Ready Steady Go* program, a flexible transition framework applicable across medical subspecialties. Under this model, transition begins at age 11 to 12 years, and progress is evaluated at each stage using structured questionnaires. In the final phase, termed *Hello*, the patient is formally transferred to adult care services.¹⁰

> 建議轉銜流程應從青少年早期（12 歲）開始，並涵蓋 6 項核心要素：制定轉銜政策或指引（12–14 歲）、病人追蹤與監測（14–18 歲）、轉銜準備度評估（14–18 歲）、轉銜規劃（14–18 歲）、照護移轉（18–21 歲），以及確認轉銜完成（18–23 歲）。⁹ 在英國，National Health Service 已推行 *Ready Steady Go* 計畫，這是一套可跨次專科應用的彈性轉銜架構。在此模式下，轉銜自 11 至 12 歲開始，並在每個階段以結構化問卷評估進度。最後階段稱為 *Hello*，此時病人正式移轉至成人照護服務。¹⁰ 在整個轉銜期間，兒科與成人照護團隊會共同開會並進行聯合門診，回顧病史並協調治療計畫。

## 🧭 診斷與評估 Diagnosis & Evaluation

### 為何轉銜期是重新評估的契機
For pituitary tumor survivors, transition offers an opportune time to reevaluate endocrinopathies, as changes in anterior pituitary function are well described and new deficits and comorbidities may develop or evolve. For example, between 25% and 88% of patients can recover their GH secretion at the time of transition.¹¹

> 對腦下垂體腫瘤存活者而言，轉銜期是重新評估內分泌病變的絕佳時機，因為 anterior pituitary 功能的變化已被清楚描述，且可能出現或演變出新的缺損與共病。例如，25% 至 88% 的患者可在轉銜時恢復 GH 分泌。¹¹ 轉銜期的主要生理變化是青春期，伴隨第二性徵發育、生長陡增與生長板融合以達成人身高，以及性腺成熟以產生精子與卵子。臨床醫師須依 hypopituitarism 的嚴重度，處理一種或多種 pituitary hormone 缺損的補充。

### Nonfunctioning Pituitary Adenomas（NFPAs）
NFPAs account for less than 10% to 15% of all pediatric pituitary tumors, and most are typically diagnosed after age 16 years.¹² Genetic screening is indicated, as NFPAs may be associated with *MEN1* or *AIP* pathogenic variants. Symptoms are related to mass effects of macroadenomas (eg, headache, visual field defects, or signs of hypopituitarism),¹³ while microadenomas are often found incidentally.¹⁴

> NFPAs 佔所有兒童 pituitary tumors 的比例不到 10% 至 15%，且多在 16 歲後才診斷。¹² 由於 NFPAs 可能與 *MEN1* 或 *AIP* 致病變異相關，應進行基因篩檢。症狀多與 macroadenomas 的腫塊效應有關（如頭痛、visual field defects 或 hypopituitarism 的徵象），¹³ 而 microadenomas 則常為意外發現。¹⁴ 若在青春期後才診斷，其處置與成人相似。長期 MRI 追蹤建議用於患有 macroadenomas 的兒童患者，並依手術時腫瘤的臨床病理分級、位置、病灶大小與估計生長速率制定個別化追蹤策略。

### GH 軸的再評估與 GH-stimulation testing
GH replacement should be considered only for patients with confirmed persistent GHD during the transition period. Between 30% and 50% of children diagnosed with idiopathic GHD have normal GH secretion when retested with GH-stimulation testing.²²

> GH 補充僅應用於轉銜期經確認為持續性 GHD 的患者。30% 至 50% 診斷為 idiopathic GHD 的兒童，在以 GH-stimulation testing 重新檢測時 GH 分泌其實正常。²²

Reevaluation of the GH axis is recommended for patients with GHD plus 2 or fewer of the following: other pituitary hormone deficiency, idiopathic isolated GHD, and history of cranial irradiation. Retesting patients with childhood-onset GHD is recommended after linear growth has ceased (growth velocity <1.5–2 cm/y). Any retesting must be performed at least 1 month after GH is discontinued.²⁰ Current guidelines exempt retesting patients with ≥3 pituitary hormone deficiencies and low IGF-1, documented transcription factor pathogenic variants, isolated GHD linked to identified pathogenic variants, or specific pituitary/hypothalamic structural defects (excluding ectopic posterior pituitary).²⁰

> 對於 GHD 且合併以下條件不超過 2 項者，建議重新評估 GH 軸：其他 pituitary hormone 缺損、idiopathic isolated GHD（不論是否合併輕微腦下垂體/異位後葉異常），以及顱部放射治療病史。對兒童期發病的 GHD 患者，建議在線性生長停止後（growth velocity <1.5–2 cm/年）重新檢測，以辨別真正需要長期 GH 治療者。任何重新檢測必須在停用 GH 至少 1 個月後進行。²⁰ 現行指引則豁免以下族群的重新檢測：合併 ≥3 種 pituitary hormone 缺損且 IGF-1 偏低者、已證實的轉錄因子致病變異、與特定致病變異相關的 isolated GHD，或特定腦下垂體/下視丘結構缺損（不含 ectopic posterior pituitary）。²⁰

### Thyroid、Adrenal 與 AVP 軸的評估
Diagnosis of central hypothyroidism is based on serum TSH and free T₄ levels, and levothyroxine dosing is titrated based on free T₄, not TSH, levels.

> Central hypothyroidism 的診斷依 serum TSH 與 free T₄；levothyroxine 劑量調整依 free T₄，而非 TSH。若同時存在 ACTH deficiency，應先補充 glucocorticoid，再開始 levothyroxine，以避免誘發 adrenal crisis。

Mild cases of central adrenal insufficiency can be asymptomatic; if undetected, patients can present with an adrenal crisis during times of stress, trauma, surgery, or illness.

> 輕度 central adrenal insufficiency 可能無症狀；若未被偵測，患者可能在壓力、外傷、手術或疾病時出現 adrenal crisis。

While monitoring AVP deficiency, it is crucial to monitor serum sodium levels and fluid input and urine output. Adipsic AVP deficiency is a rare yet challenging complication associated with high morbidity and mortality.

> 監測 AVP deficiency 時，關鍵在於監測 serum sodium、液體攝入與尿量，以判斷 desmopressin 是否過量或不足。Adipsic AVP deficiency 是罕見但棘手的併發症，伴隨高罹病率與死亡率，臨床、照護人員與家屬應以固定的液體進出量、體重與尿量監測方式共同監督。³⁴

## 💊 治療與處置 Management

### Hypopituitarism 的核心
The mainstay of medical treatment in patients with hypopituitarism is replacement of the appropriate hormones.

> Hypopituitarism 的內科治療核心是補充適當的荷爾蒙。

### Growth Hormone（GH）
While statural growth may have completed during the transition period, somatic development continues, progressing towards peak bone mass.¹⁵ Upon discontinuation of GH treatment during the transition period, abnormal body composition and unfavorable shifts in cardiovascular risk markers have been reported.¹⁶ Cessation of GH upon linear growth completion may also limit peak bone mass attainment, increasing susceptibility to bone mineral density deficits in adult life.¹⁷ Negative effects on quality of life and psychological well-being have also been reported.¹⁸

> 雖然身高生長可能已在轉銜期完成，但軀體發育仍持續進行，朝向 peak bone mass 前進。¹⁵ 若在轉銜期停用 GH，已有報告出現異常的身體組成與心血管風險指標的不利變化。¹⁶ 在線性生長完成時停用 GH，也可能限制 peak bone mass 的達成，增加成年後 bone mineral density 缺損的易感性，進而影響日後 osteoporosis 與脆性骨折的風險。¹⁷ 也有報告指出停藥對生活品質與心理健康有負面影響。¹⁸

Continued GH treatment beyond linear growth achievement results in increased bone mineral density and lean body mass, and improved quality of life. In accordance with current guidelines,²⁰ individuals with persistent GHD should maintain GH replacement therapy throughout transition, aiming for complete skeletal maturation and optimization of metabolic profile. Recent availability of once-weekly, long-acting GH formulations may help patients to consider resuming GH treatment.

> 在線性生長完成後持續 GH 治療，可增加 bone mineral density 與 lean body mass，並改善生活品質。²⁰ 依現行指引，持續性 GHD 者應在整個轉銜期維持 GH 補充，以達成完整骨骼成熟與最佳代謝狀態。然而青少年常將「生長結束」視為停用 GH 的時機。近期出現的每週一次長效型 GH 製劑，可能有助於患者在轉銜期考慮恢復治療。

**GH 起始劑量 GH Starting Dose**

- Current Endocrine Society guidelines recommend higher starting GH doses for patients younger than 30 years (0.4 to 0.5 mg daily) than for older adults.²⁶
  - 現行 Endocrine Society 指引建議 30 歲以下患者採用較高的 GH 起始劑量（每日 0.4 至 0.5 mg），高於年長成人。²⁶
- The American Association of Clinical Endocrinology clinical practice guidelines recommend starting at 50% of the previous pediatric dose.²⁰
  - American Association of Clinical Endocrinology 臨床實務指引則建議以先前兒科劑量的 50% 起始。²⁰
- Doses should be tailored based on age- and gender-adjusted IGF-1 levels, estrogen status, clinical response, and absence of adverse effects, with the goal of normalizing IGF-1.
  - 劑量應依年齡與性別校正後的 IGF-1、estrogen 狀態、臨床反應與無不良反應來調整，目標是使 IGF-1 正常化。

GH therapy does not increase the risk of secondary cancers in childhood cancer survivors,²⁸ and an increased risk of meningiomas was linked to radiotherapy, not to GH treatment.²⁹

> GH 治療不會增加兒童癌症存活者發生次發性癌症的風險，²⁸ 而 meningiomas 風險增加與 radiotherapy 有關，並非 GH 治療所致。²⁹

### Gonadal Steroids
Pituitary tumors can cause acquired hypogonadotropic hypogonadism (HH) in the transition age. If due to a prolactinoma, HH is treated medically, while surgery is the first-line treatment for other functioning tumors. Notably, testosterone therapy does not lead to testicular growth or spermatogenesis in men; thus, induction of fertility requires treatment with either pulsatile GnRH or exogenous gonadotropins.

> Pituitary tumors 可在轉銜年齡造成後天性 hypogonadotropic hypogonadism (HH)。若由 prolactinoma 引起，HH 以藥物治療；其他 functioning 腫瘤則以手術為第一線。值得注意的是，testosterone 治療並不會促使男性睪丸生長或 spermatogenesis；因此誘導生育須採用 pulsatile GnRH 或外源性 gonadotropins。

**男性誘導 spermatogenesis 之方案（來源舉例）**
- hCG 500 to 3000 IU twice weekly, increased to every 2 days, adjusted by serum testosterone; and recombinant FSH 75 to 225 IU 2 to 3 times weekly.³⁰
  - hCG 500 至 3000 IU 每週兩次，可增至每 2 天一次，依血清 testosterone 調整；併用 recombinant FSH 75 至 225 IU 每週 2 至 3 次。³⁰
- In men, testosterone levels are a reliable biomarker of dosing.
  - 男性可以 testosterone 作為可靠的劑量指標。

**女性 estrogen 補充（來源方案）**
Estrogen therapy should be initiated at a low dosage (15% to 25% of the adult dosage) and increased gradually every 4 to 6 months.³¹

> Estrogen 治療應以低劑量起始（成人劑量的 15% 至 25%），並每 4 至 6 個月逐步增加。³¹
- 一種可考慮的方案：含 17β-estradiol 25 mcg/24 小時的貼片，剪成 4 等份；睡前貼 1 份、隔日晨間移除。4 至 6 個月後可增為夜間 2 份（晨間移除 1 份、另 1 份日間留置）。約每 4 至 6 個月增量至 25 至 100 mcg 的整片貼片，每週更換兩次。
- 或口服 17β-estradiol 0.5 mg 每日或每隔一日起始，逐步增加。³¹
- 目標為在 2 至 3 年內發育出第二性徵。當出現「breakthrough」出血時，應給予 progesterone 治療（如 medroxyprogesterone acetate 5 mg 每日連續 10 天，每 4–5 週一次），以預防子宮內膜增生。³¹

In women, ovulation and pregnancy can be achieved by pulsatile GnRH administration or gonadotropin combination therapy; exogenous gonadotropins (eg, human menopausal gonadotropin or a combination of recombinant LH and FSH) are administered, followed by an hCG injection. The possibility of unwanted pregnancy and contraception should also be discussed openly.

> 女性可透過 pulsatile GnRH 或 gonadotropin 合併療法達成排卵與懷孕；給予外源性 gonadotropins（如 human menopausal gonadotropin，或 recombinant LH 與 FSH 併用），再接續 hCG 注射。也應公開討論非預期懷孕與避孕議題。

### Thyroid
Levothyroxine is the treatment of choice, titrated to free T₄. In females on estrogen-containing contraceptive pills and in pregnant women, the levothyroxine dosage needs to be increased. If ACTH deficiency is also present, glucocorticoid (GC) replacement should be started before levothyroxine to avoid precipitating adrenal crisis. If the patient is also on GH therapy, higher dosages of levothyroxine are needed, as GH can decrease T₄ and increase T₃ levels.²⁰

> Levothyroxine 為首選，依 free T₄ 調整。服用含 estrogen 避孕藥的女性及孕婦需增加 levothyroxine 劑量。若同時存在 ACTH deficiency，應先開始 GC 補充再給 levothyroxine，以免誘發 adrenal crisis。若患者同時使用 GH，因 GH 可降低 T₄、升高 T₃，需較高劑量的 levothyroxine。²⁰

### Adrenal
In adolescents with central adrenal insufficiency, hydrocortisone is the GC of choice because of its immediate efficacy, short duration of action, and lesser effect on linear growth. Hydrocortisone can be given thrice or twice daily, with the highest dose in the morning on waking, and the last dose no later than 18:00 hours. Long-acting GCs (eg, prednisone or prednisolone) may be considered for patients who cannot adhere to a multidose regimen; however, dexamethasone should be avoided as it can slow growth.³³

> 對青少年 central adrenal insufficiency，hydrocortisone 為首選 GC，因其即時療效、作用時間短（利於劑量微調）且對線性生長影響較小。Hydrocortisone 可每日三次或兩次給予，最高劑量於晨起服用，最後一劑不晚於 18:00。對於無法遵從多劑方案者可考慮長效 GC（如 prednisone 或 prednisolone）；但應避免 dexamethasone，因其可能延緩生長。³³

Prevention of adrenal crisis relies on anticipating precipitating factors and increasing GC doses (stress dosing), avoiding abrupt cessation, and patient education on when parenteral hydrocortisone is required. Conversely, GC overreplacement exacerbates metabolic syndrome, cardiovascular disease, and bone disease.

> 預防 adrenal crisis 有賴於預測誘發因子（感染、手術、生理或心理壓力、突然停藥），並以增加 GC 劑量（stress dosing）與避免驟然停藥來預防，同時教育患者何時需 stress dose、何時需注射型 hydrocortisone。青春期尤其棘手：遵從度較不穩定、可能開始飲酒或使用其他刺激物，並可能因作息紊亂而延後下午劑量。反之，GC 過度補充會加重代謝症候群、心血管疾病與骨骼疾病。GC 治療是救命療法，衛教是保護患者免於 adrenal crisis 的關鍵，且劑量應依患者生活型態量身調整。

### Arginine Vasopressin（AVP）Deficiency
Desmopressin, preferably administered orally, is the treatment of choice; it is also available as an intranasal spray and parenteral formulation.

> Desmopressin 為首選，以口服為佳，也有鼻噴劑與注射劑型。

When managing AVP deficiency, evaluate whether a patient on desmopressin should also be on GC replacement therapy. Adrenal insufficiency can lead to hyponatremia, which may be exacerbated by desmopressin. Clinicians and patients should be aware of the need to increase GC dosages and check electrolytes before further desmopressin administration to prevent hyponatremia.³⁵ Excessive beer binge drinking and certain recreational drugs, such as MDMA ("ecstasy"), that lead to overdrinking may be dangerous while on desmopressin.

> 處置 AVP deficiency 時，須評估使用 desmopressin 的患者是否也需 GC 補充。Adrenal insufficiency 可導致 hyponatremia，並可能因 desmopressin 而加重。患有下視丘–腦下垂體疾病的青少年常同時使用 GC 與 desmopressin，臨床醫師與患者應知悉：在追加 desmopressin 前需增加 GC 劑量並檢查電解質，以預防 hyponatremia。³⁵ 大量狂飲啤酒及某些會導致過度飲水的娛樂性藥物（如 MDMA「搖頭丸/ecstasy」），在使用 desmopressin 期間可能相當危險。應教育青少年依口渴調整飲水與 desmopressin 劑量。

### Functioning Pituitary Tumors

**Prolactinomas**
Prolactinomas are the most common functioning pituitary tumors in children and adolescents, with marked female predominance and often a genetic predisposition (eg, *MEN1* and *AIP*). Dopamine agonists are first-line therapy. Surgery is considered for dopamine agonist intolerance or resistance, emergencies (threatened visual function, apoplexy), or young patients with a well-delineated microadenoma and high likelihood of surgical cure. Delaying surgery until age 18 (for adult consent) may be considered for microadenomas. Radiotherapy should be reserved for aggressive and refractory prolactinomas.³⁶

> Prolactinomas 是兒童與青少年最常見的 functioning pituitary tumors，明顯以女性居多，且常有遺傳傾向（如 *MEN1* 與 *AIP*）。Dopamine agonists 為第一線，對縮小腫瘤體積有效且安全。手術適用於對 dopamine agonist 不耐或抗性、緊急狀況（視力受威脅、apoplexy），或具明確 microadenoma 且手術治癒機率高的年輕患者。Microadenomas 可考慮延至 18 歲（取得成人同意）再手術。Radiotherapy 對控制腫瘤生長有效但對使 prolactin 正常化較差，應保留給侵襲性且難治的 prolactinomas。³⁶

**Cushing Disease**
In pediatric Cushing disease, central weight gain, facial changes, and growth failure are almost the rule. Corticotroph adenomas are often small and less often visualized on MRI. Successful surgery may result in hypopituitarism, and long-term endocrine follow-up and GH testing are mandatory. For those not achieving remission, medical therapy (ketoconazole, metyrapone, and more recently osilodrostat) or radiotherapy may be used; cabergoline or pasireotide have not yet been validated in children. Bilateral adrenalectomy is a salvage option only for life-threatening situations.³⁷⁻³⁹

> 兒童 Cushing disease 幾乎必見中心性體重增加、臉部變化與生長遲滯。Corticotroph adenomas 常很小且較不易在 MRI 上顯影。手術成功可能導致 hypopituitarism，必須長期內分泌追蹤並進行 GH 檢測。未達緩解者需藥物治療（ketoconazole、metyrapone，以及較新的 osilodrostat）或考慮 radiotherapy；接受 radiotherapy 者皆應監測是否出現 GHD。Cabergoline 或 pasireotide 於兒童尚未被驗證。Bilateral adrenalectomy 屬激進的救援選項，僅在其他療法失敗或不適用的危及生命情況才考慮。³⁷⁻³⁹

**Somatotropinomas**
GH excess is rare in children; GH-secreting adenoma is the main cause of acromegaly/acrogigantism, but hyperplasia may occur with McCune-Albright syndrome, Carney complex, X-linked acrogigantism, or GHRH-secreting tumors. The most frequent identifiable genetic causes are *AIP* germline pathogenic variants and *GPR101* duplications (X-linked acrogigantism). Most somatotropinomas are macroadenomas, frequently invasive and difficult to control. Options mirror adults (surgery, medical therapy, radiotherapy), but remission rates are lower. First-generation somatostatin analogues (octreotide, lanreotide) are used; pegvisomant is more effective in genetic forms (especially *AIP* and *GPR101*); cabergoline has modest efficacy.⁴⁰

> 兒童 GH 過量罕見；GH-secreting adenoma 是 acromegaly/acrogigantism 的主因，但 McCune-Albright syndrome、Carney complex、X-linked acrogigantism 或 GHRH-secreting 腫瘤也可能造成瀰漫性增生或多發性腺瘤。最常見可辨識的遺傳病因為 *AIP* germline 致病變異與 *GPR101* 基因重複（後者導致 X-linked acrogigantism）。多數 somatotropinomas 在診斷時已是 macroadenomas，常具侵襲性且難以控制、復發率高。治療選項與成人相似（手術、藥物、放療），但兒童完全緩解率較低。藥物方面採第一代 somatostatin analogues（octreotide 與 lanreotide）；pegvisomant 對遺傳型（尤其 *AIP* 與 *GPR101*）較有效；cabergoline 可單用或併用，但兒童療效有限。⁴⁰

## 🧠 個案解析與臨床推理 Case Analysis & Clinical Reasoning

### Case 1 解析 — 停用 GH 最可能面臨的風險
**正解：E) Decreased peak bone mass（尖峰骨量下降）**

「轉銜期」是指青春期結束、肌肉、骨骼與體脂組成邁向完全成熟的階段。Peak bone mass 女性約在 22.3 歲、男性更晚約 26.9 歲達到。⁴² 轉銜期若 GHD 未治療，會導致無法達到此尖峰骨量（正解 E）。

臨床推理與各選項辨析：
- **E（正解）**：停用 GH 使骨質累積受限，無法達成 peak bone mass，日後 osteoporosis 與脆性骨折風險升高。此為本題最直接、最具長期意義的風險。
- **A 體重增加、D BMI 增加**：停用 GH 會造成身體組成改變（lean mass 下降、fat mass 上升），但整體體重與 BMI 通常不會明顯增加，尤其在年輕成人。⁴³ 這些身體組成的不利變化可在恢復 GH 後回復。⁴⁴
- **B 空腹血糖下降**：身體組成的不利變化不必然損害 insulin sensitivity 與葡萄糖代謝，故不會出現空腹血糖下降。⁴³
- **C 生活品質受損**：雖然未治療的成人 GHD 確有生活品質受損，但多項研究發現，轉銜期持續 GH 治療者、⁴⁶ 或剛停藥不久者⁴⁷ 並未見生活品質受損。故短期內此非最突出的風險。

**決策要點**：此案例正是「青少年因厭倦每日注射而想停藥」的典型情境。她 IGF-1 SDS –2.4、生長板幾近閉合，屬持續性 GHD，應與其溝通繼續 GH 對骨骼與代謝的長期益處，並可考慮改用每週一次長效製劑以改善遵從度。

### Case 2 解析 — 開始 hydrocortisone 與 levothyroxine 後需警覺的變化
**正解：E) Increased thirst and urination（口渴與排尿增加）**

此患者有 central hypothyroidism（free T₄ 低、TSH 未代償）、central adrenal insufficiency（9 AM cortisol 偏低、ACTH 未升高）與 hypogonadotropic hypogonadism（testosterone 低、gonadotropins 未升高），並有 2 年前口渴/頻尿後自行緩解的病史——提示曾有 partial AVP deficiency 被「掩蓋」。

機轉推理：慢性 cortisol 與 thyroid hormone 缺乏會增加腎臟 AVP 受體對 AVP 的敏感度。一旦補充這些荷爾蒙，partial AVP deficiency 患者所分泌的少量 AVP 可能不再足以維持腎臟水分再吸收，導致原本亞臨床的 AVP deficiency 顯現，表現為口渴與排尿增加（正解 E）。AVP deficiency 可在 GC 開始後立即或數週內出現。其機轉兼具 AVP-dependent 與 AVP-independent：cortisol 使 V2 受體對 AVP 產生相對抗性；CRH 神經元同時刺激 ACTH 與 AVP，而 adrenal insufficiency 時 CRH 上調；腎鈉流失與容積耗損、以及每搏輸出量與心輸出量下降，皆為 AVP 的非滲透性刺激。

各選項辨析：
- **A 低血鈉**：未治療的 AVP deficiency 會出現高血鈉而非低血鈉。
- **B 姿勢性低血壓、C 性慾下降**：生理劑量 hydrocortisone 不應造成。
- **D 心悸**：年輕患者使用生理劑量 levothyroxine 不應造成心悸。

**陷阱提醒**：單獨給予 levothyroxine（即使生理劑量）可能揭露潛在的 adrenal insufficiency，故務必先補充 GC 再給 levothyroxine。此案例呈現轉銜期常見的「多重荷爾蒙缺損補充順序」與「隱匿性 AVP deficiency 被治療掀開」兩大要點。

### Case 3 解析 — 想生育的男性下一步最佳處置
**正解：A) Obtain semen analysis（進行精液分析）**

最佳下一步是先以精液分析確認其是否真的不孕（正解 A）。雖然合併 hypopituitarism 又長期使用 testosterone 補充，理論上預期會不孕，但他仍可能有足以生育的精子數與型態。Drincic 等人⁴⁸ 發現，約 50% 的後天性 hypogonadotropic hypogonadism 男性仍有足以生育的 spermatogenesis。

各選項辨析：
- **A（正解）**：先客觀評估其生育狀態，才能決定後續。
- **C hCG、D hCG + FSH**：若精液分析為 azoospermia 或 oligospermia，可能需 hCG（甚至加 FSH）注射；但未評估前即貿然使用並不恰當。
- **B clomiphene citrate**：屬 estrogen receptor antagonist，在 hypopituitarism 患者無法有效刺激 gonadotropin 生成，故無效。
- **E 增加 testosterone 劑量**：不僅無助於生育，反而會抑制 gonadotropins、減少 spermatogenesis，進一步損害生育力。

**決策要點**：生育議題應在開始 testosterone 補充「之前」即於轉銜門診討論，尤其對 17 歲以上者，以顧及未來組建家庭的影響。此案例凸顯轉銜期「fertility counseling」與「避免 testosterone 抑制精子生成」的核心觀念。

### 共通臨床推理與陷阱 Pitfalls
- 轉銜期是重新評估（re-evaluation）而非單純延續處方的時機：anterior pituitary 功能會變化，GHD 可能恢復，也可能出現新的缺損。
- GHD 再檢測務必在停用 GH 至少 1 個月後進行，並在線性生長停止後，以免將暫時性 idiopathic GHD 誤判為終身需治療。
- 多重荷爾蒙缺損的補充順序：GC 先於 levothyroxine；使用 desmopressin 前確認 GC 是否足量並追蹤 electrolytes。
- 遵從度是轉銜期最大隱憂：飲酒、娛樂性藥物、作息紊亂、脫離父母監督，都可能危及 adrenal crisis 預防與 desmopressin 安全。

## ⭐ 重點整理 Key Takeaways
- 轉銜（transition）是腦下垂體腫瘤存活者的高風險關鍵期，需採個別化、多專科（multidisciplinary）方式，並由兒科與成人內分泌科醫師密切協作、結構化交接。
- 轉銜期應把握重新評估內分泌軸的契機：25%–88% 患者在轉銜時可恢復 GH 分泌，30%–50% 的 idiopathic GHD 兒童重測時 GH 分泌正常；GHD 再檢測須在線性生長停止後、且停用 GH 至少 1 個月才進行。²⁰ ²² 
- 持續性 GHD 應在轉銜期維持 GH 補充以達骨骼完全成熟與 peak bone mass；起始劑量 Endocrine Society 建議 30 歲以下 0.4–0.5 mg/日，AACE 建議為兒科劑量的 50%，並以 IGF-1 正常化為目標。²⁰ ²⁶
- 多重荷爾蒙缺損補充有順序：GC 需先於 levothyroxine 以免誘發 adrenal crisis；補充 cortisol 與 thyroid hormone 可能掀開潛在的 AVP deficiency（口渴、多尿）。
- 男性 testosterone 無法促進 spermatogenesis；有生育需求者應先做 semen analysis，勿逕自加量 testosterone（反而抑制生育），必要時採 hCG ± FSH 或 pulsatile GnRH。
- Functioning tumors 處置原則：prolactinomas 以 dopamine agonists 為第一線；Cushing disease 與 somatotropinomas 以手術為主、緩解率低於成人且常需長期追蹤與 GH 檢測。
- 兒童腦下垂體腫瘤常帶遺傳背景（*MEN1*、*AIP*、*GPR101*），應考慮 genetic screening。
- 遵從度與衛教是轉銜成敗關鍵：需針對飲酒、娛樂性藥物（如 MDMA）、adrenal crisis 之 stress dosing 與 desmopressin 之水分管理進行反覆衛教。
