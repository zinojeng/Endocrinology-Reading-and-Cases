## 🩺 臨床情境 Clinical Scenario

本章附有三則臨床病例，分別呈現 ECS 的診斷、定位與危急處置三個面向。以下忠實重建各病例。

### Case 1

> A 54-year-old woman presents with 3 months of progressive proximal muscle weakness, new-onset hyperglycemia, hypertension, and hypokalemia requiring supplementation. She reports easy bruising and facial rounding but has unintentionally lost 11 lb (5 kg). Laboratory evaluation confirms hypercortisolism with elevated late-night salivary cortisol on 2 occasions and markedly elevated 24-hour urinary free cortisol excretion.

一位 54 歲女性，出現為期 3 個月、進行性加重的近端肌無力（proximal muscle weakness）、新發高血糖、高血壓，以及需補充治療的 hypokalemia。她自述容易瘀青、臉部變圓，但非刻意地減輕了 11 lb（5 kg）。實驗室檢查證實為 hypercortisolism：兩次 late-night salivary cortisol 升高，且 24 小時 urinary free cortisol 排泄顯著上升。

**Laboratory test results（檢驗結果）**

| 項目 Test | 數值 Value | 參考範圍 Reference | SI 單位 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Plasma ACTH | 145 pg/mL | 7–60 pg/mL | 31.9 pmol/L (1.5–13.2) |
| Morning serum total cortisol | 28 µg/dL | 5–21 µg/dL | 772.5 nmol/L (137.9–579.3) |
| Late-night salivary cortisol | 0.8 µg/dL | <0.1 µg/dL | 22.1 nmol/L (<2.8) |
| Post-1 mg dexamethasone cortisol | 25 µg/dL | <1.8 µg/dL | 689.7 nmol/L (<49.7) |
| Post-8 mg dexamethasone cortisol | 27 µg/dL | <1.0 µg/dL | 744.9 nmol/L (<27.6) |
| Urinary free cortisol | 870 µg/24 h | <45 µg/24 h | 2401 nmol/d (<124) |
| Serum potassium | 3.2 mEq/L | 3.5–5.2 mEq/L | 3.2 mmol/L (3.5–5.2) |

> Pituitary MRI shows a 4-mm hypoenhancing lesion.

Pituitary MRI 顯示一顆 4 mm 的低顯影（hypoenhancing）病灶。

**Q：Which of the following is the most appropriate next diagnostic step?（下一步最適當的診斷步驟為何？）**

A. Transsphenoidal pituitary surgery
B. Inferior petrosal sinus sampling
C. Pituitary MRI with dynamic contrast
D. Medical therapy with a steroidogenesis inhibitor
E. Full-body cross-sectional imaging

**✅ Answer: B) Inferior petrosal sinus sampling**

當生化檢測與影像結果不一致或模稜兩可時，即應進行 inferior petrosal sinus sampling (IPSS)（Answer B）。此病例呈現急性且嚴重的表現，不符合典型 Cushing disease；在未確認 ACTH 來源為 pituitary 之前，不應貿然手術（Answer A）。重複 MRI（Answer C）無助於釐清 ACTH 來源，因 pituitary 病灶已清楚可見。在診斷尚未確立前給予 steroidogenesis inhibitor（Answer D）並不恰當，但若診斷檢查被延誤，這可作為過渡選項。由於 ACTH-dependent hypercortisolism 的亞型尚未確立，此時進行全身橫斷面影像（Answer E）亦不正確。

### Case 2

> A 46-year-old man presents with severe hypokalemia, resistant hypertension, hyperglycemia, and recurrent infections. Biochemical testing confirms ACTH-dependent hypercortisolism.

一位 46 歲男性，表現為嚴重 hypokalemia、頑固性高血壓（resistant hypertension）、高血糖與反覆感染。生化檢查證實為 ACTH-dependent hypercortisolism。

**Laboratory test results（檢驗結果）**

| 項目 Test | 數值 Value | 參考範圍 Reference | SI 單位 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Plasma ACTH | 220 pg/mL | 7–60 pg/mL | 48.4 pmol/L (1.5–13.2) |
| Morning serum total cortisol | 52 µg/dL | 5–21 µg/dL | 1434.6 nmol/L (137.9–579.3) |
| Late-night salivary cortisol | 1.8 µg/dL | <0.1 µg/dL | 49.7 nmol/L (<2.8) |
| Post-1 mg dexamethasone cortisol | 48 µg/dL | <1.8 µg/dL | 1324.2 nmol/L (<49.7) |
| Urinary free cortisol | 980 µg/24 h | <45 µg/24 h | 2705 nmol/d (<124) |
| Serum potassium | 3.0 mEq/L | 3.5–5.2 mEq/L | 3.0 mmol/L (3.5–5.2) |

> MRI of the pituitary gland demonstrates a possible 2-mm pituitary adenoma. Inferior petrosal sinus sampling does not demonstrate a central ACTH gradient. Findings on contrast-enhanced CT of the chest and abdomen are unremarkable. Ga-68 DOTATATE PET-CT reveals focal uptake in the pancreatic tail.

Pituitary MRI 顯示疑似 2 mm 的 pituitary adenoma。Inferior petrosal sinus sampling 未顯示中央 ACTH 梯度（central ACTH gradient）。胸腹部對比增強 CT 無明顯異常。Ga-68 DOTATATE PET-CT 則顯示 pancreatic tail 有局部攝取（focal uptake）。

**Q：Which of the following is the most appropriate next diagnostic step?（下一步最適當的診斷步驟為何？）**

A. Recommend no further testing
B. Proceed with endoscopic ultrasonography
C. Repeat cross-sectional imaging in 6 months
D. Proceed with FDG PET-CT
E. Repeat inferior petrosal sinus sampling

**✅ Answer: B) Proceed with endoscopic ultrasonography**

由於功能性影像已在 pancreas（ECS 可能來源之一）顯示攝取，下一步最佳選擇是採用更精準的定位技術，如 endoscopic ultrasonography（Answer B）。腹部 CT 對某些 pancreatic 病灶的偵測並不準確。若等待 6 個月後再重複橫斷面影像（Answer C），將延誤診斷與治療。對 neuroendocrine tumor 而言，Ga-68 DOTATATE PET 較 FDG PET-CT（Answer D）更為準確，且 68Ga DOTATATE PET 已顯示 pancreatic 攝取。既無理由懷疑 ECS 亞型的判定，重複 IPSS（Answer E）並不正確。

### Case 3

> A 62-year-old man with newly diagnosed metastatic small cell lung carcinoma presents with profound muscle weakness, hypokalemia, uncontrolled diabetes, and sepsis from pneumonia.

一位 62 歲男性，新診斷為轉移性 small cell lung carcinoma，表現為嚴重肌無力、hypokalemia、失控的糖尿病，以及肺炎引發的 sepsis。

**Laboratory test results（檢驗結果）**

| 項目 Test | 數值 Value | 參考範圍 Reference | SI 單位 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Plasma ACTH | 189 pg/mL | 7–60 pg/mL | 41.6 pmol/L (1.5–13.2) |
| Morning serum total cortisol | 78 µg/dL | 5–21 µg/dL | 2151.9 nmol/L (137.9–579.3) |
| Urinary free cortisol | 3650 µg/24 h | <45 µg/24 h | 10,074 nmol/d (<124) |
| Serum potassium | 2.4 mEq/L | 3.5–5.2 mEq/L | 2.4 mmol/L (3.5–5.2) |

> Despite initiation of a steroidogenesis inhibitor (metyrapone, 1000 mg every 6 hours), cortisol levels remain elevated, and his clinical condition continues to deteriorate. The oncologic team anticipates that systemic cancer therapy will be delayed due to the instability of his condition.

儘管已開始使用 steroidogenesis inhibitor（metyrapone，1000 mg 每 6 小時一次），cortisol 濃度仍持續升高，臨床狀況持續惡化。腫瘤團隊預期，因病情不穩定，全身性抗癌治療將被延遲。

**Q：Which of the following is the best next step to control this patient's hypercortisolism?（控制此患者 hypercortisolism 的下一步最佳選擇為何？）**

A. Add another oral steroidogenesis inhibitor (ie, combination therapy)
B. Treat metastatic lesions with radiation
C. Perform bilateral adrenalectomy
D. Start etomidate therapy and admit to the intensive care unit until chemotherapy initiation
E. Continue current medical therapy and add antibacterial prophylaxis and anticoagulation

**✅ Answer: C) Perform bilateral adrenalectomy**

Bilateral adrenalectomy 可對 cortisol 提供即時且確定性的控制。再加上另一種口服 steroidogenesis inhibitor（Answer A）或對轉移病灶進行放射治療（Answer B），都不太可能及時控制 hypercortisolism 或達成完全緩解。Etomidate 治療（Answer D）雖能有效控制 hypercortisolism，但需入住加護病房，且非可持續的長期方案，尤其化療並無法有效控制 hypercortisolism。雖然緊急給予抗菌預防與抗凝治療（Answer E）確有其適應症，但此策略無法預防次佳控制的 hypercortisolism 所帶來的所有併發症。

## 🔬 背景與重要性 Background & Significance

> ECS is a rare but life-threatening cause of endogenous hypercortisolism, accounting for approximately 5% to 10% of cases of ACTH-dependent Cushing syndrome. Affected individuals often present with rapidly progressive and severe hypercortisolism, leading to profound metabolic derangements, opportunistic infections, thromboembolism, cardiovascular events, and excess mortality.

ECS 是罕見但致命的內源性 hypercortisolism 病因，約占 ACTH-dependent Cushing syndrome 病例的 5% 至 10%。患者常以快速進展且嚴重的 hypercortisolism 表現，導致嚴重代謝紊亂、伺機性感染（opportunistic infections）、血栓栓塞（thromboembolism）、心血管事件，以及超額死亡率。

> Unlike pituitary Cushing disease, ECS is characterized by marked heterogeneity in clinical presentation, tumor biology, and disease trajectory. While some patients exhibit classic cushingoid features, others present with catabolic manifestations, such as weight loss, muscle wasting, hypokalemia, and acute metabolic decompensation, particularly in the setting of aggressive or metastatic malignancy.

與 pituitary Cushing disease 不同，ECS 的特徵在於臨床表現、腫瘤生物學與疾病軌跡上顯著的異質性。部分患者呈現典型的 cushingoid 特徵，另一些則表現為分解代謝（catabolic）症狀，如體重減輕、肌肉流失、hypokalemia 與急性代謝失償，尤其常見於侵襲性或轉移性惡性腫瘤。

> Diagnostic evaluation is further complicated by overlapping biochemical profiles with pituitary disease, imperfect performance of dynamic endocrine tests, and frequent challenges in localizing the ectopic ACTH source despite advanced imaging techniques.

診斷評估更因下列因素而複雜化：與 pituitary disease 重疊的生化表現、動態內分泌檢測不夠完美的效能，以及即使採用先進影像技術仍常難以定位異位 ACTH 來源。

> Historically, evidence guiding ECS management has been limited to small retrospective series and single-center experiences, with the largest early cohorts comprising fewer than 150 patients.

歷史上，指引 ECS 處置的證據僅限於小型回溯性系列與單一中心經驗，早期最大的世代研究也不足 150 名患者。這些研究雖奠定了診斷與處置的基本原則，卻受限於轉診偏差（referral bias）、檢測策略異質性，以及不斷演進的影像與治療選項。

### 病理生理 Overview and Pathophysiology

> ECS results from ACTH secretion by tumors located outside pituitary gland, leading to adrenal cortisol excess. Although ECS accounts for a minority of ACTH-dependent Cushing syndrome cases, it is disproportionately associated with severe biochemical hypercortisolism, rapid clinical deterioration, and excess mortality compared with pituitary Cushing disease. Tumors causing ECS are heterogeneous in origin and biological behavior, ranging from indolent neuroendocrine tumors to highly aggressive carcinomas.

ECS 源自 pituitary gland 以外的腫瘤分泌 ACTH，導致 adrenal cortisol 過量。雖然 ECS 僅占 ACTH-dependent Cushing syndrome 的少數，但相較於 pituitary Cushing disease，它不成比例地與嚴重的生化 hypercortisolism、快速臨床惡化及超額死亡率相關。造成 ECS 的腫瘤在來源與生物行為上具異質性，從惰性（indolent）的 neuroendocrine tumor 到高度侵襲性的 carcinoma 皆有。

> Pulmonary neuroendocrine tumors are consistently reported as the most common source of ectopic ACTH secretion, followed by pancreatic neuroendocrine tumors, small cell lung carcinoma, thymic neuroendocrine tumors, and medullary thyroid carcinoma.

Pulmonary neuroendocrine tumor 一貫被報告為異位 ACTH secretion 最常見的來源，其次為 pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor、small cell lung carcinoma、thymic neuroendocrine tumor，以及 medullary thyroid carcinoma。

### 臨床與生化表現 Clinical and Biochemical Presentation

> Patients with ECS often present with more severe biochemical hypercortisolism than those with pituitary Cushing disease, frequently accompanied by hypokalemia, hypertension, hyperglycemia, and proximal muscle weakness. At high concentrations, cortisol overwhelms renal 11β-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase type 2, allowing cortisol to activate the mineralocorticoid receptor, and resulting in renal potassium wasting, sodium retention, and hypertension, thus explaining hypokalemia in patients with severe ECS.

ECS 患者的生化 hypercortisolism 通常比 pituitary Cushing disease 更嚴重，常伴隨 hypokalemia、高血壓、高血糖與近端肌無力。在高濃度下，cortisol 使 renal 11β-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase type 2 飽和，讓 cortisol 得以活化 mineralocorticoid receptor，導致腎臟排鉀（renal potassium wasting）、鈉滯留與高血壓，因而解釋了嚴重 ECS 患者的 hypokalemia。

> Clinical presentation of patients with ECS is heterogeneous... Patients with aggressive or metastatic malignancies may have insufficient time to develop the phenotypic features of chronic hypercortisolism, despite markedly elevated cortisol levels.

ECS 患者的臨床表現具異質性。部分患者發展出典型 cushingoid 特徵，另一些則以分解代謝表現（如體重減輕、肌肉流失、感染或血栓栓塞事件）呈現。此差異可能反映了 cortisol 過量的程度與持續時間不同：具侵襲性或轉移性惡性腫瘤的患者，儘管 cortisol 顯著升高，卻可能沒有足夠時間發展出慢性 hypercortisolism 的表型特徵。

### 併發症與死亡率 Complications and Mortality

> ECS is associated with a high burden of complications, including opportunistic infections, venous thromboembolism, cardiovascular events, fractures, and metabolic decompensation. These complications often develop early in the disease course and may occur even before a definitive diagnosis is established.

ECS 伴隨高度的併發症負擔，包括 opportunistic infections、venous thromboembolism、心血管事件、骨折與代謝失償。這些併發症常在病程早期即出現，甚至可能在確定診斷之前就發生。

> Mortality in ECS remains substantial and is higher than in pituitary Cushing disease. Both tumor-related factors and complications of hypercortisolism contribute to adverse outcomes. Prior cohorts have demonstrated that uncontrolled cortisol excess is a major driver of mortality, emphasizing the importance of early and effective biochemical control.

ECS 的死亡率仍然可觀，且高於 pituitary Cushing disease。腫瘤相關因素與 hypercortisolism 的併發症皆造成不良結局。既往世代研究顯示，未受控制的 cortisol 過量是死亡的主要驅動因子，凸顯早期且有效的生化控制之重要性。

## 🧭 診斷與評估 Diagnosis & Evaluation

> Differentiating ECS from pituitary Cushing disease can be challenging. Biochemical overlap between the 2 conditions is common, and no single dynamic test reliably distinguishes them. High-dose dexamethasone-suppression testing and corticotropin-releasing hormone stimulation may be informative but demonstrate imperfect sensitivity and specificity.

鑑別 ECS 與 pituitary Cushing disease 具有挑戰性。兩者間的生化重疊常見，且沒有任何單一動態檢測能可靠地區分。High-dose dexamethasone-suppression testing 與 corticotropin-releasing hormone (CRH) stimulation 或可提供資訊，但其敏感度與特異度並不完美。

> Inferior petrosal sinus sampling is considered the reference standard when noninvasive testing is inconclusive; however, false-negative and false-positive results occur due to technical factors, venous anatomy, or tumor biology. Consequently, diagnostic interpretation must integrate biochemical testing, imaging findings, and clinical context rather than rely on a single modality.

當非侵入性檢測無法定論時，inferior petrosal sinus sampling (IPSS) 被視為參考標準（reference standard）；然而，因技術因素、靜脈解剖或腫瘤生物學，仍會出現偽陰性與偽陽性結果。因此，診斷判讀必須整合生化檢測、影像發現與臨床情境，而非仰賴單一模式。

> Localization of the ectopic ACTH source is frequently difficult. Cross-sectional imaging may fail to identify small or occult tumors, and discordance between anatomical and functional imaging is common. Functional imaging, including somatostatin receptor–based positron emission tomography, has improved the detection of neuroendocrine tumors but does not eliminate the challenge of diagnosing occult ECS.

異位 ACTH 來源的定位常有困難。橫斷面影像（cross-sectional imaging）可能無法辨識小型或隱匿（occult）腫瘤，且解剖影像與功能影像之間的不一致常見。功能性影像，包括以 somatostatin receptor 為基礎的 PET（如 Ga-68 DOTATATE），已提升 neuroendocrine tumor 的偵測率，但仍無法完全消除診斷隱匿性 ECS 的難題。

## 💊 治療與處置 Management

> The cornerstone of ECS management is rapid and effective control of cortisol excess, given the strong association between ongoing hypercortisolism and morbidity and mortality. When the ectopic ACTH-secreting tumor is localized and surgically resectable, tumor excision is the preferred definitive therapy and usually results in a cure.

ECS 處置的基石是快速且有效地控制 cortisol 過量，因為持續的 hypercortisolism 與罹病率及死亡率高度相關。當異位 ACTH-secreting tumor 已定位且可手術切除時，腫瘤切除是首選的確定性治療，通常可達治癒。

> Unfortunately, many patients present with unresectable or metastatic disease, necessitating alternative strategies. Medical therapy with agents targeting cortisol synthesis or action is often initiated as first-line treatment, particularly in patients with metastatic or occult disease. However, achieving complete biochemical control is frequently difficult with medical therapy alone.

遺憾的是，許多患者就診時已為無法切除或轉移性疾病，須採取替代策略。針對 cortisol 合成或作用的藥物治療常作為第一線治療，尤其用於轉移性或隱匿性疾病患者。然而，單靠藥物治療常難以達到完全的生化控制。

> Bilateral adrenalectomy provides immediate and definitive control of hypercortisolism and has long been recognized as a life-saving option in patients with severe ECS. Accumulating evidence suggests that bilateral adrenalectomy should be considered earlier in selected patients with refractory or rapidly progressive disease, particularly when definitive tumor-directed therapy is not feasible.

Bilateral adrenalectomy 可對 hypercortisolism 提供即時且確定性的控制，長久以來被視為嚴重 ECS 患者的救命選項。越來越多證據顯示，對於難治性或快速進展的特定患者，尤其在無法施行確定性腫瘤導向治療時，應更早考慮 bilateral adrenalectomy。

**藥物與處置要點（源自病例）**

- Steroidogenesis inhibitor：metyrapone，例如 1000 mg 每 6 小時一次（Case 3 使用劑量）。
- Etomidate：可有效快速控制 hypercortisolism，但需加護病房監測，非長期方案。
- Bilateral adrenalectomy：對難治性、快速惡化且腫瘤導向治療不可行者的確定性救命選擇。

> ECS represents a medical emergency requiring prompt recognition, careful diagnostic evaluation, and aggressive management. The heterogeneity of presentation and frequent diagnostic uncertainty necessitate a multidisciplinary approach integrating endocrinology, oncology, radiology, and surgery. Given the limitations of available diagnostic tests and therapies, clinical judgment remains central to optimizing outcomes in this high-risk population.

ECS 是一種內科急症，需要及時辨識、審慎的診斷評估與積極處置。其表現的異質性與頻繁的診斷不確定性，需要整合 endocrinology、oncology、radiology 與 surgery 的多科團隊照護。鑑於現有診斷檢測與治療的侷限，臨床判斷仍是優化此高風險族群結局的核心。

## 🧠 個案解析與臨床推理 Case Analysis & Clinical Reasoning

**核心推理主軸——「先確立亞型，再定位，再控制」**

三則病例依序演示 ECS 處置的三個決策節點：

1. **鑑別亞型（Case 1）**：當 ACTH-dependent hypercortisolism 併有「不典型於 Cushing disease」的線索——急性、嚴重、體重減輕、明顯 hypokalemia、high-dose dexamethasone 無法抑制——即使 pituitary MRI 看到小病灶，也不可直接視為 Cushing disease。臨床上相當比例的正常人可帶有 pituitary incidentaloma，4 mm 低顯影病灶本身不足以定論。此時 IPSS 是釐清中央 vs 周邊 ACTH 來源的關鍵，避免對非致病 pituitary 病灶施行不必要手術。

2. **精準定位（Case 2）**：IPSS 無中央梯度、常規 CT 陰性，而 Ga-68 DOTATATE PET 於 pancreatic tail 顯示攝取，方向已明確指向 pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor。下一步應以更高解析度的 endoscopic ultrasonography 確認並取得組織/精準定位，而非退回較不敏感的 CT、延遲追蹤或重複已陰性的 IPSS。此處體現「功能影像陽性→以最佳解剖模式定位」的邏輯。

3. **危急控制（Case 3）**：轉移性 small cell lung carcinoma 併極重度 hypercortisolism（cortisol 78 µg/dL、UFC 3650 µg/24 h、K 2.4 mEq/L、sepsis），metyrapone 已無法控制，且化療將延遲。當藥物無法及時達標、腫瘤導向治療不可行時，bilateral adrenalectomy 提供立即且確定的 cortisol 控制，是救命之選。

**鑑別診斷 Differential Diagnosis**

- ACTH-dependent：pituitary Cushing disease（最常見）vs ectopic ACTH secretion。
- 生化區分不完美：high-dose dexamethasone-suppression 與 CRH stimulation 敏感度/特異度皆有限。
- 影像陷阱：pituitary incidentaloma 可誤導；ectopic 來源可能為隱匿小腫瘤。

**常見陷阱 Pitfalls**

- 看到小 pituitary 病灶就等同 Cushing disease，忽略臨床脈絡（急性、嚴重、hypokalemia、體重減輕）。
- 過度信賴單一檢測；診斷須整合生化、影像與臨床。
- 在轉移性侵襲性癌症中，因缺乏典型 cushingoid 外觀而低估 hypercortisolism 的致命性。
- 危急時仍執著於逐步加藥，延誤 bilateral adrenalectomy 或 etomidate 等快速控制手段。

**決策要點 Decision Points**

- 生化與影像不一致/模稜兩可 → IPSS。
- 功能影像陽性 → 以最精準解剖模式（如 EUS）定位。
- 藥物無法及時控制且腫瘤治療不可行 → 及早 bilateral adrenalectomy。

## ⭐ 重點整理 Key Takeaways

- ECS 是內科急症，特徵為嚴重、常快速進展的 hypercortisolism，併有高比率的代謝併發症、感染、thromboembolism、心血管事件與超額死亡率。
- Hypokalemia 是 ECS 的標誌性生化特徵，反映高 cortisol 濃度下 renal 11β-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase type 2 飽和，使 cortisol 活化 mineralocorticoid receptor。
- 鑑別 ECS 與 pituitary Cushing disease 常具挑戰性，需整合生化檢測、動態內分泌檢測、影像與臨床情境；沒有任何單一模式具決定性。
- 動態檢測結果與影像不一致時，inferior petrosal sinus sampling (IPSS) 為釐清 ACTH 來源的參考標準，但仍有偽陰/偽陽性。
- 功能性影像（如 Ga-68 DOTATATE PET）提升 neuroendocrine tumor 偵測率；功能影像陽性後應以最精準的解剖定位技術（如 endoscopic ultrasonography）跟進。
- 單靠 medical therapy 常無法達成持續生化控制，許多患者反應不完全或短暫。
- 快速有效控制 cortisol 過量是可調控結局中最重要的因素；當腫瘤導向治療不可行或藥物無法及時控制時，應強烈考慮 bilateral adrenalectomy。
