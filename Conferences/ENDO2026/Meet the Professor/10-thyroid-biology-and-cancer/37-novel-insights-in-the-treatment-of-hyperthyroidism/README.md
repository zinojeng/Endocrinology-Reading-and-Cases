## 🩺 臨床情境 Clinical Scenario

本章以三個臨床案例貫穿說明 hyperthyroidism 的個人化治療決策，以下先摘要案例，詳細解析見「個案解析與臨床推理」段落。

**Case 1 — De novo Graves disease（新診斷 Graves disease）**
A 30-year-old woman is referred because of de novo Graves disease. She presented with weight loss, nervousness, and tachycardia, and one week ago was started on a β-adrenergic blocker and MMI, 30 mg daily. Examination shows a diffuse goiter but no orbitopathy. Family history is positive for Graves disease (paternal grandmother and paternal aunt).
一位 30 歲女性因新診斷 Graves disease 轉診而來，以體重減輕、神經緊張與心搏過速表現，一週前開始使用 β-adrenergic blocker 及 MMI 30 mg/日。理學檢查有瀰漫性 goiter，無 orbitopathy。家族史陽性（父系祖母與姑姑均有 Graves disease）。

- TSH = <0.001 mIU/L（0.4–4.3 mIU/L）
- Free T4 = 6.9 ng/dL（0.7–1.5 ng/dL）（SI: 89.0 pmol/L [9.0–19.0 pmol/L]）
- TRAb = 10.0 IU/L（<1.9 IU/L）

**Case 2 — Toxic multinodular goiter（毒性多結節性甲狀腺腫）**
A 67-year-old woman is referred for biochemical hyperthyroidism found during cardiovascular follow-up. She reports occasional palpitations, reduced exercise tolerance, and fatigue, without weight loss, tremor, or heat intolerance. History includes ischemic heart disease with prior revascularization, chronic atrial fibrillation on anticoagulation, COPD, and stage 3 chronic kidney disease. She lives alone with limited social support.
一位 67 歲女性因心血管追蹤時發現生化性 hyperthyroidism 轉診，主訴偶發心悸、運動耐受度下降與疲倦，無體重減輕、顫抖或怕熱。病史包含缺血性心臟病（曾做冠狀動脈血管重建）、慢性 atrial fibrillation（服用抗凝血劑）、COPD 及第三期慢性腎臟病。獨居、社會支持有限。

- Serum TSH = 0.04 mIU/L（0.4–4.3 mIU/L）
- Free T4 = 1.6 ng/dL（0.7–1.5 ng/dL）（SI: 21.0 pmol/L [9.0–19.0 pmol/L]）
- Total T3：正常；TPO antibodies：陰性；TRAb：陰性
- 甲狀腺超音波：明顯腫大之多結節性甲狀腺，數顆結節 >4 cm。
- 甲狀腺 scintigraphy：多個自主功能結節，周圍甲狀腺組織受抑制，符合 toxic multinodular goiter。

**Case 3 — Unplanned pregnancy on ATD（非計畫性懷孕中的 ATD 使用）**
A 28-year-old woman with Graves disease reports she is newly pregnant, estimated at 4 to 5 weeks' gestation. The pregnancy was unplanned. She was diagnosed with Graves disease 7 months earlier and treated with MMI, 10 mg daily. She has been biochemically stable (most recent TSH 1.9 mIU/L, 8 weeks ago), but TRAb remain elevated at 4.5 IU/L (reference <1.9 IU/L). She reports mild nausea attributed to early pregnancy and no thyrotoxic symptoms.
一位 28 歲 Graves disease 女性來電告知已懷孕，估計妊娠 4 至 5 週，屬非計畫性懷孕。她於 7 個月前診斷 Graves disease，使用 MMI 10 mg/日治療，生化上穩定（最近一次 TSH 1.9 mIU/L，為 8 週前檢測），但 TRAb 仍升高至 4.5 IU/L（參考值 <1.9 IU/L）。目前僅有輕微噁心（歸因於早孕），無甲狀腺毒症症狀。

## 🔬 背景與重要性 Background & Significance

**Untreated thyrotoxicosis is associated with substantial morbidity, including impaired quality of life and increased risk of cardiovascular complications and all-cause mortality.** Selection of the most appropriate therapeutic strategy is primarily determined by the underlying cause, with Graves disease, toxic nodular goiter, and thyroiditis representing the most common etiologies of overt thyrotoxicosis.
未經治療的 thyrotoxicosis 會帶來顯著的疾病負擔，包括生活品質下降，以及心血管併發症與全因死亡率的增加。選擇最合適的治療策略主要取決於潛在病因，其中 Graves disease、toxic nodular goiter 與 thyroiditis 是 overt thyrotoxicosis 最常見的病因。

**The incidence of drug-induced thyrotoxicosis has risen in recent years**, largely due to the expanding use of newer agents such as immune checkpoint inhibitors, tyrosine kinase inhibitors, and targeted monoclonal antibody therapies. The review by Barroso-Sousa et al is an excellent resource on endocrine dysfunction after immune checkpoint inhibitors.
近年來藥物誘發的 thyrotoxicosis 發生率上升，主要與 immune checkpoint inhibitors、tyrosine kinase inhibitors 及標靶單株抗體治療等新型藥物使用增加有關。Barroso-Sousa 等人的回顧文章是探討 immune checkpoint inhibitors 相關內分泌功能異常的極佳參考。

**Terminology.** By definition, thyrotoxicosis describes the clinical state resulting from excessive thyroid hormone action at the tissue level, whereas hyperthyroidism specifically refers to disorders characterized by increased synthesis and secretion of thyroid hormones by the thyroid gland. Both terms are often used interchangeably in everyday practice.
名詞界定：thyrotoxicosis 指的是組織層面過量甲狀腺荷爾蒙作用所致的臨床狀態，而 hyperthyroidism 特指甲狀腺本身合成與分泌甲狀腺荷爾蒙增加的疾病。兩者在日常臨床實務中常被交替使用。

**Treatment modalities and regional patterns.** Therapeutic decision-making is guided by the underlying pathophysiological mechanism, with ATDs, RAI therapy, and thyroidectomy constituting the main treatment modalities. In Europe and the Asia–Pacific region, ATDs are generally the preferred first-line treatment for Graves disease. In the United States, treatment patterns have shifted over the past 2 decades toward a growing preference for ATDs over RAI. Emerging data supporting prolonged low-dosage ATD therapy in Graves disease are likely to further influence future treatment paradigms.
治療模式與地區差異：治療決策取決於潛在病理生理機轉，主要治療模式為 ATDs、RAI therapy 與 thyroidectomy。在歐洲與亞太地區，ATDs 普遍為 Graves disease 的首選一線治療；在美國，過去二十年的治療趨勢已轉向偏好 ATDs 而非 RAI。支持在 Graves disease 中使用長期低劑量 ATD 治療的新興證據，可能進一步影響未來的治療模式。

**Individualization.** After diagnosis, optimal management is influenced not only by etiology but also by disease severity and phase, and by individual patient characteristics including age, sex, and comorbidities. While biochemical confirmation is usually straightforward, diagnostic uncertainty may arise from analytical interference or less common causes such as TSH-secreting pituitary adenomas or iatrogenic hyperthyroidism.
個人化：診斷後的最佳處置不僅取決於病因，也受疾病嚴重度與病程、以及年齡、性別、共病等個別病人特徵影響。雖然生化確診多半直接明確，但仍可能因分析干擾或較少見病因（如 TSH-secreting pituitary adenoma、醫源性 hyperthyroidism）而出現診斷不確定性。

### Practice Gaps 臨床實務落差

- **Limited large-scale RCTs** directly comparing clinical outcomes among the various therapeutic approaches across distinct categories of hyperthyroid disease.
  缺乏大規模隨機對照試驗直接比較不同 hyperthyroidism 類別中各種治療方式的臨床結果。
- **Marked regional variability** in physicians' treatment preferences worldwide.
  全球各地醫師的治療偏好差異顯著。
- **Insufficient awareness** that all established treatment options may each be considered as an initial strategy, depending on etiology, severity, and patient preference, as well as the strategy for recurrent disease.
  未充分認知到：所有既有治療選擇皆可依病因、嚴重度與病人偏好作為初始治療策略，復發時的治療策略亦然。
- **Persistent uncertainty** regarding management of hyperthyroidism in patients desiring pregnancy, requiring careful consideration of both maternal and fetal thyroid physiology.
  對於有懷孕意願之 hyperthyroidism 患者的處置仍存在持續的不確定性，須同時審慎考量母體與胎兒的甲狀腺生理。

## 🧭 診斷與評估 Diagnosis & Evaluation

**Biochemical definition.** Overt thyrotoxicosis is biochemically defined by a suppressed serum TSH, typically below 0.01 mIU/L, together with elevated serum free T4 and/or increased total or free T3. Free T4 (the biologically active unbound fraction) is preferred over total T4. Owing to methodological limitations of current free T3 assays, either total or free T3 is acceptable.
生化定義：overt thyrotoxicosis 的生化定義為血清 TSH 受抑制（通常低於 0.01 mIU/L），合併血清 free T4 升高與／或 total 或 free T3 增加。free T4（生物活性的未結合部分）優於 total T4。由於目前 free T3 檢驗方法學上的限制，測定 total 或 free T3 皆可接受。

**TRAb / TSI.** In the setting of biochemical hyperthyroidism, assessment of TRAb is a cornerstone of the workup. Widely used immunoassays detect TRAb without distinguishing between thyroid-stimulating antibodies (TSI) and thyroid-blocking antibodies, both of which may coexist — a lack of functional differentiation that can complicate interpretation. More recently, automated bridge-based binding assays preferentially (though not exclusively) detect TSI. In general populations, both conventional TRAb immunoassays and newer TSI-focused assays show high sensitivity and specificity.
TRAb／TSI：在生化性 hyperthyroidism 情境下，TRAb 檢測是診斷評估的基石。廣泛使用的 immunoassay 偵測 TRAb，但無法區分 thyroid-stimulating antibodies（TSI）與 thyroid-blocking antibodies，而兩者可能並存——此種無法作功能區分的特性在特定情境下會使判讀複雜化。較新的自動化 bridge-based binding assay 則優先（但非專一）偵測 TSI。在一般族群中，傳統 TRAb immunoassay 與較新的 TSI 導向檢驗皆具高敏感度與特異度。

**Scintigraphy.** When etiology cannot be readily established, thyroid scintigraphy is a valuable adjunct:
甲狀腺 scintigraphy：當無法從臨床與生化結果輕易確立病因時，甲狀腺 scintigraphy 是有價值的輔助工具：

| 病因 Etiology | Scintigraphy 攝取型態 Uptake pattern |
| --- | --- |
| Graves disease | 瀰漫且均勻的放射性示蹤劑攝取（如 123I、131I、99mTc），攝取值升高或高於正常上限 Diffuse, homogeneous uptake; elevated or high-normal |
| 各型 Thyroiditis 與 iodine-induced hyperthyroidism | 攝取明顯降低或缺乏（碘過量抑制攝取）Markedly reduced or absent uptake |
| Toxic nodular goiter | 局部攝取增加與減少並存，對應 hot 與 cold nodules Focal increased and decreased uptake (hot/cold nodules) |

**Ultrasonography.** Thyroid ultrasonography is indicated when nodules are found on examination or when scintigraphy identifies cold nodules requiring further evaluation. Where scintigraphy is unavailable, color-flow Doppler ultrasonography can assess vascularity: increased thyroidal blood flow typically indicates Graves disease, whereas vascularity is normal or reduced in most thyroiditis. A limitation is the increased likelihood of detecting incidental nodules, potentially leading to overdiagnosis of clinically insignificant nodules or microcarcinomas.
超音波：當理學檢查發現結節，或 scintigraphy 顯示需進一步評估的 cold nodules 時，應行甲狀腺超音波。在無法進行 scintigraphy 的中心，可用 color-flow Doppler 超音波評估血流：甲狀腺血流增加通常提示 Graves disease，而多數 thyroiditis 之血流正常或減少。其限制在於偶發結節偵測率上升，可能導致臨床上無意義的結節或 microcarcinoma 被過度診斷。

## 💊 治療與處置 Management

**General principle.** Selection of therapy is mainly determined by the underlying mechanism, but disease severity and patient age must also be considered. The 3 principal modalities are ATDs, RAI therapy, and thyroidectomy. In patients with cardiovascular symptoms—most notably palpitations—β-adrenergic blockade can be initiated for symptom control regardless of etiology.
一般原則：治療選擇主要取決於潛在機轉，但也須考量疾病嚴重度與病人年齡。三大治療模式為 ATDs、RAI therapy 與 thyroidectomy。對出現心血管症狀（尤其是心悸）的患者，無論病因為何，皆可先給予 β-adrenergic blockade 作為症狀控制。

### Toxic Adenoma / Toxic Nodular Goiter 毒性腺瘤／毒性結節性甲狀腺腫

RAI therapy and surgery are traditionally the preferred definitive treatments, with RAI being first-line for most patients. As both are definitive, the choice depends mainly on patient factors and goiter characteristics:
RAI therapy 與手術傳統上為首選確定性治療，其中 RAI 為多數患者的一線治療。由於兩者皆屬確定性治療，選擇主要取決於病人因素與 goiter 特徵：

- **RAI** — 適用於因共病而手術風險高的年長患者，以及腺瘤為小至中等且無壓迫症狀者。Preferred in older patients with high surgical risk and in small- to moderate-sized adenomas without compressive symptoms.
- **Surgery** — 適用於大型 goiter，尤其伴壓迫症狀、懷疑 thyroid cancer，或需快速控制 hyperthyroidism 時。Preferred for large goiters, compressive symptoms, suspicion of cancer, or when rapid control is needed.
- **Long-term low-dosage ATDs** — 累積證據顯示，長期低劑量 ATDs 亦為有效策略，尤其對年長者或不適合 RAI／手術者。Accumulating evidence shows prolonged low-dosage ATDs can be effective, particularly in elderly patients or poor candidates for RAI or surgery.

### Graves Disease

All 3 modalities are generally effective, but ATDs are often favored by patients as the initial approach. Notably, patients treated with RAI have reported lower quality of life up to 10 years after therapy compared with those managed with ATDs or thyroidectomy.
三種治療模式一般皆有效，但病人常偏好以 ATDs 作為初始治療。值得注意的是，接受 RAI 治療的患者在治療後長達 10 年間，其生活品質報告低於以 ATDs 或 thyroidectomy 處置者。

**ATDs.** Carbimazole and its active metabolite methimazole (MMI) are generally preferred over propylthiouracil (PTU) owing to superior efficacy and a more favorable safety profile. Per American and European guidelines, newly diagnosed Graves hyperthyroidism is typically treated with carbimazole or MMI for 12 to 18 months. Treatment may be stopped if TSH has normalized and TRAb are no longer detectable. If TRAb remain elevated during therapy or relapse occurs after withdrawal, patients may take a second course (commonly ≥12 months) or choose definitive treatment.
ATDs：由於療效較佳且安全性較優，carbimazole 及其活性代謝物 methimazole（MMI）一般優於 propylthiouracil（PTU）。依美國與歐洲指引，新診斷的 Graves hyperthyroidism 通常以 carbimazole 或 MMI 治療 12 至 18 個月。若 TSH 已正常化且 TRAb 不再可測得，可考慮停藥。若治療期間 TRAb 持續升高或停藥後復發，患者可接受第二療程（通常 ≥12 個月）或選擇確定性治療。

- Relapse after a single ATD course is relatively high（接近 50%），but a second course achieves sustained remission in more than 75% of patients.
  單一療程 ATD 後復發率相對偏高（接近 50%），但第二療程可在超過 75% 的患者達成持續緩解。
- Recent studies suggest long-term or even lifelong low-dosage carbimazole/MMI is a safe and effective alternative.
  近期研究顯示，長期甚至終生低劑量 carbimazole／MMI 為安全且有效的替代策略。

**Agranulocytosis.** A rare but serious adverse effect, occurring in fewer than 0.5% of treated patients, most often within the first 3 months. Patients must be explicitly instructed to seek immediate evaluation if they develop fever and/or sore throat. If confirmed, ATDs must be permanently discontinued.
Agranulocytosis：罕見但嚴重的不良反應，發生率低於 0.5%，最常見於治療前 3 個月內。務必明確衛教病人：若出現發燒與／或喉嚨痛須立即就醫評估。若確診，ATDs 須永久停用。

**Dosing strategy.** ATDs may be given as dose-titration (monotherapy) aimed at euthyroidism, or as a "block-and-replace" regimen using higher ATD doses to fully inhibit hormone production combined with levothyroxine. Overtreatment causing hypothyroidism should be avoided, as it may precipitate or exacerbate thyroid eye disease in Graves disease.
給藥策略：ATDs 可採劑量調整（單一治療）以維持 euthyroidism，或採「block-and-replace」方案，以較高劑量 ATD 完全抑制荷爾蒙生成並合併 levothyroxine 補充。應避免過度治療導致 hypothyroidism，因其可能誘發或惡化 Graves disease 患者的 thyroid eye disease。

**RAI in Graves disease.** Permanent hypothyroidism develops in approximately 50% to 85%, more frequently with higher administered activities. The most clinically significant adverse effect is development or progression of thyroid eye disease, with cigarette smokers at particularly increased risk; RAI is contraindicated in severe Graves orbitopathy. In patients with mild orbitopathy or at increased risk of de novo thyroid eye disease (smokers, severe or unstable hyperthyroidism, or high TRAb), prophylactic glucocorticoid therapy is recommended at RAI initiation. Although most studies show no overall increase in cancer incidence or mortality, some data suggest a potential dose-dependent association with increased solid-malignancy mortality; these findings remain controversial.
Graves disease 之 RAI：約 50% 至 85% 會出現永久性 hypothyroidism，給予活度越高越常見。臨床上最重要的不良反應是 thyroid eye disease 的發生或惡化，吸菸者風險特別高；severe Graves orbitopathy 為 RAI 之禁忌症。對輕度 orbitopathy 或有新發 thyroid eye disease 風險（吸菸、嚴重或不穩定 hyperthyroidism、高 TRAb）者，建議於 RAI 起始時給予預防性 glucocorticoid。雖多數研究顯示整體癌症發生率或死亡率並未上升，部分資料提示與實體惡性腫瘤死亡率可能有劑量相依關聯，惟此發現仍具爭議。

**Thyroidectomy.** Can be a first-line option in selected cases, based on patient preference or when ATDs or RAI are ineffective, poorly tolerated, or contraindicated (e.g., severe Graves orbitopathy).
Thyroidectomy：在特定情況下可作為一線選項，依病人偏好，或當 ATDs 或 RAI 無效、耐受不佳或有禁忌（如 severe Graves orbitopathy）時採用。

### Novel Treatment Options 新型治療選擇

Treatment modalities have remained similar for decades and carry important limitations—high relapse rates after ATD withdrawal, and permanent hypothyroidism after RAI or surgery. In recent years, drugs with novel targets for Graves disease have been tested in small, mainly open-label phase I and II studies. Mechanisms include:
數十年來治療模式大致未變，且有重要限制——停用 ATDs 後復發率高，以及 RAI 或手術後的永久性 hypothyroidism。近年來，針對 Graves disease 新標靶的藥物已在小型、多為開放標籤的第一與第二期臨床試驗中受測，機轉包括：

1. **Restoration of immune tolerance** — immunomodulatory TSH receptor peptides。恢復免疫耐受性（immunomodulatory TSH receptor peptides）。
2. **Counteracting TSH-receptor signaling** — TRAb blockers, small-molecule TSH receptor antagonists。對抗 TSH receptor 訊息傳遞（TRAb blockers、小分子 TSH receptor antagonists）。
3. **Modulating B-cell function and activation** — rituximab, iscalimab, belimumab。調節 B 細胞功能與活化（rituximab、iscalimab、belimumab）。
4. **Inhibiting IgG recycling by neonatal Fc receptor blockade** — rozanolixizumab, efgartigimod。透過 neonatal Fc receptor 阻斷抑制 IgG 回收再利用（rozanolixizumab、efgartigimod）。

Large randomized multicenter trials are anticipated in the coming years to establish or refute these promising yet preliminary results.
未來數年預期會有大型隨機多中心試驗，以證實或推翻這些有前景但仍屬初步的結果。

### Hyperthyroidism and Pregnancy (Planning) 甲狀腺功能亢進與懷孕（規劃）

Overt hyperthyroidism during pregnancy is associated with adverse maternal and neonatal outcomes—miscarriage, intrauterine fetal demise, preeclampsia, preterm delivery, and low birth weight—though these risks are substantially reduced or absent with timely antenatal care and appropriate biochemical control.
懷孕期間的 overt hyperthyroidism 與不良母嬰結局相關——流產、子宮內胎兒死亡、preeclampsia、早產與低出生體重——但若能及時產前照護並達成適當的生化控制，這些風險可大幅降低甚至消失。

- **ATD teratogenicity.** ATD use in early gestation, particularly between gestational weeks 5 and 11, carries a small but clinically relevant risk of congenital malformations. Ideally, women planning pregnancy receive definitive therapy before conception. When ATDs are needed in the first trimester, the type and severity of teratogenic effects differ between agents, with patterns generally favoring PTU over carbimazole/MMI in this window.
  ATD 致畸性：早孕期使用 ATD（尤其妊娠第 5 至 11 週）帶有雖小但具臨床意義的先天畸形風險。理想上，計畫懷孕的婦女應在受孕前接受確定性治療。當第一孕期需使用 ATDs 時，各藥物的致畸類型與嚴重度不同，此期間整體型態一般偏好 PTU 優於 carbimazole／MMI。
- **RAI and pregnancy timing.** When RAI is chosen as definitive treatment, women are typically advised to postpone pregnancy for 6 to 12 months. Clinicians should note that serum TRAb may transiently rise after RAI and remain elevated for up to 1 year. Because TRAb cross the placenta, persistently elevated levels during pregnancy may stimulate the fetal thyroid, increasing the risk of fetal or neonatal hyperthyroidism.
  RAI 與懷孕時機：若選擇 RAI 作為確定性治療，通常建議延後懷孕 6 至 12 個月。臨床醫師須注意，血清 TRAb 於 RAI 後可能暫時上升並持續升高達 1 年。由於 TRAb 可穿過胎盤，懷孕期間持續升高的濃度可能刺激胎兒甲狀腺，增加胎兒或新生兒 hyperthyroidism 的風險。

## 🧠 個案解析與臨床推理 Case Analysis & Clinical Reasoning

### Case 1 — 新診斷 Graves disease、2 年內有懷孕意願

**Q1：若以 ATD 治療 12–18 個月，她的復發風險為何？**
**答案：D) >65%**

多項因素與 Graves disease 在初始 ATD 療程後復發風險增加有關，包括：男性、年齡小於 40 歲、就診時 hyperthyroidism 嚴重、中重度 orbitopathy、就診時 TRAb 高、ATD 治療期間 TRAb 持續存在，以及吸菸。一項前瞻性觀察研究以 4 項臨床指標（年齡、free T4、TRAb、goiter 大小）建立評分，最高風險組於 ATD 治療 1 年後有 68% 的復發風險。本案病人年輕（30 歲）、free T4 顯著升高（6.9 ng/dL）、TRAb 高（10.0 IU/L）且有明顯 goiter，屬高風險族群。

**Q2：對她的 Graves disease，下列何者為最佳治療？**
**答案：D) RAI therapy**

臨床推理：雖然多數患者以 ATD 為首選初始治療，本案有充分理由考慮直接選擇確定性治療。依其起始表現，她在 12–18 個月 ATD 治療後很可能復發；屆時正逢其懷孕意願成真之時，而第一孕期暴露於 ATDs 與先天畸形風險增加相關。因此在此階段即討論確定性治療是合理的，RAI therapy 為講者的偏好選擇。

**關鍵決策要點與陷阱 pitfalls：**
- 唯一「真正正確」的作法是與病人共享決策（shared decision-making）。所有列出的選項皆有利弊，最佳選擇應與病人共同權衡，兼顧未來懷孕的健康與母體整體健康。
- 若選擇 ATD，對非備孕者而言 PTU 並非一線治療（因嚴重肝損傷風險），PTU 一般為 MMI 之後的二線治療。
- 接受 131I 治療 Graves disease 的女性，應建議至少延後懷孕 6 個月（自分化型甲狀腺癌 131I 治療資料外推）。其血清 TRAb 與／或 TSI 於 RAI 後可能上升，但一般於治療後 12 至 18 個月回到治療前數值。

### Case 2 — 67 歲、多重共病之 Toxic multinodular goiter

**Q：對此病人最合適的處置策略為何？**
**答案：D) 起始長期、低劑量 ATD 治療**

鑑別診斷與推理：TPO antibodies 與 TRAb 皆陰性、scintigraphy 顯示多個自主功能結節且周圍組織受抑制，確立為 toxic multinodular goiter（非自體免疫疾病）。

- **總 thyroidectomy（A）**：因高齡且多重心肺共病，圍手術期風險過高。
- **RAI therapy（B）**：雖技術上可行，但受限於輻射安全防護的後勤問題，且病人配合治療後隔離要求的能力有限（獨居、社會支持不足）。
- **觀察不治療（C）**：不適當；此年齡即使輕度 hyperthyroidism 也與 atrial fibrillation 進展、心血管病變與骨質流失風險增加相關。
- **固定 12–18 個月 ATD（E）**：不太可能達成持續 euthyroidism，因 toxic nodular goiter 非自體免疫疾病，不會自發緩解。
- **長期低劑量 ATD（D）**：在此臨床情境下安全、有效且務實。低劑量 MMI 可維持生化 euthyroidism，且在年長患者以穩定低劑量使用時不良反應少，兼顧風險最小化與有效控制荷爾蒙過量，為本案首選。

### Case 3 — Graves disease 患者非計畫性早孕（4–5 週）

**Q：對此病人的最佳處置為何？**
**答案：D) 停用 MMI 並於當日評估甲狀腺功能**

臨床推理：第一孕期暴露於 ATDs（MMI 與 PTU 皆然）與先天畸形風險增加相關。鑑於早孕期充分記載的致畸風險，以降低胎兒風險為導向的決策比等待重複甲狀腺功能檢驗更為重要——尤其她受孕前約 4 個月甲狀腺功能已生化穩定。若先取得新的檢驗結果（選項 A、C、E）會造成延遲，在器官形成關鍵期不必要地延長胎兒暴露。

**藥物選擇的細節：**
- 現有證據顯示，MMI 暴露的先天異常絕對風險高於 PTU，且畸形譜不同：PTU 暴露較常與相對輕微的異常（如顏面頸部或泌尿道缺陷）相關，MMI 則與範圍更廣、更嚴重、影響多器官系統的先天異常有關。因此第一孕期若須用 ATD，通常偏好 PTU（若可取得）優於 MMI。
- 但更重要的是：早孕期應「優先考慮停用 ATD」，特別是預期復發風險低的患者。

**為何本案可直接停藥而非換藥（D 而非 B）：**
- Graves hyperthyroidism 於 ATD 停藥後平均復發時間約為 3 個月；若真復發，多半發生在最關鍵的致畸窗口已過之後。
- 本案具備所有降低復發風險的臨床因素：低劑量 ATD（<10 mg/日）、懷孕前 >6 個月穩定 euthyroidism（最好血清 TSH >0.35 mIU/L）、TRAb 低於正常上限 3 倍。
- 此時測甲狀腺功能主要用於建立新的生化基準，以利日後停用 MMI 後對甲狀腺功能結果的判讀。

## ⭐ 重點整理 Key Takeaways

- 雖然多數 Graves disease 患者以 ATD 為首選初始治療，但對於**高復發風險且近期有懷孕意願**的患者，可考慮直接選擇確定性治療（definitive treatment），此時須權衡確定性治療與 ATDs 的風險與效益。
- **長期、低劑量 MMI** 在特定 toxic nodular goiter 患者是安全、有效且務實的策略；在部分 Graves disease 患者亦可考慮。
- Hyperthyroid 患者一旦驗孕陽性，早孕期**首要步驟應優先考慮停用 ATD**，尤其是預期復發風險低者（低劑量 ATD <10 mg/日、懷孕前 euthyroidism 穩定 >6 個月且理想上 TSH >0.35 mIU/L、TRAb <正常上限 3 倍）。
- **Agranulocytosis** 雖罕見（<0.5%）但嚴重，最常見於治療前 3 個月；務必衛教病人出現發燒或喉嚨痛時立即就醫，確診後永久停用 ATDs。
- **RAI** 於 Graves disease 後有約 50–85% 產生永久性 hypothyroidism，且可能誘發或惡化 thyroid eye disease；severe Graves orbitopathy 為禁忌，高風險者起始時應給予預防性 glucocorticoid。
- **第一孕期 ATD 選擇**：MMI 致畸絕對風險與嚴重度高於 PTU，若第一孕期須用藥則偏好 PTU；非備孕者則因肝毒性風險，PTU 為 MMI 之後的二線。
- 新型治療策略——**恢復免疫耐受、干擾病理性 TSH receptor 訊息、調節 B 細胞活化與功能、標靶 IgG 回收路徑**——為 Graves disease 具前景的新興方向，未來可能大幅重塑其處置。
- 所有治療決策的核心是**共享決策（shared decision-making）**，依病因、嚴重度、共病與病人偏好個人化選擇。
