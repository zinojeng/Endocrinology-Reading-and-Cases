## 📋 教學目標 Educational Objectives

- **Stop measuring pancreatic enzymes as a precaution for people receiving GLP-1 receptor agonists (GLP-1 RAs).**
  停止對使用 GLP-1 receptor agonists（GLP-1 RAs）的患者例行性預防式監測胰臟酵素。
- **Use GLP-1 RAs in people with a history of pancreatitis from other causes.**
  對於因其他原因曾罹患胰臟炎的患者，仍可使用 GLP-1 RAs。
- **Use GLP-1 RAs in people with pancreatic lesions, with an understanding of the uncertainty regarding the effect of these medications on the risk of pancreatic cancer in individuals at high risk.**
  在具有胰臟病灶的患者身上使用 GLP-1 RAs，但須理解此類藥物對高風險族群 pancreatic cancer 風險影響的不確定性。

## 🩺 臨床情境 Clinical Scenario

本章以三個臨床案例貫穿，涵蓋「有胰臟炎病史者」「使用後出現胰臟酵素上升者」以及「合併胰臟囊性病灶者」三種最常令臨床醫師卻步的情境。

**Significance of the clinical problem.** GLP-1 RA and GLP-1/GIP dual agonist (GLP-1/GIP DA) medications are effective for glycemic control and for preventing weight- and diabetes-related complications. GLP-1 RAs account for about 35% of prescriptions for type 2 diabetes (T2D) and obesity, and about 7% of prescriptions for obesity. For the first time, the obesity rate in the United States is expected to decline.

GLP-1 RA 與 GLP-1/GIP dual agonist（GLP-1/GIP DA）在血糖控制、以及預防與體重和糖尿病相關的併發症上都相當有效。GLP-1 RAs 約占 type 2 diabetes（T2D）與肥胖處方的 35%，其中約 7% 屬於肥胖適應症的處方。美國的肥胖盛行率首度預期將會下降。

However, some time ago, use of GLP-1 RAs was reported to be associated with increased risks of acute pancreatitis and adenocarcinoma of the pancreas. The FDA has never recommended against using GLP-1 RA and GLP-1/GIP DA in people at higher risk of pancreatic cancer or acute pancreatitis. However, clinical practice guidelines advise that in individuals with a history of pancreatitis, use of incretin medications (ie, GLP-1 RA, GLP-1/GIP DA, and DPP-4 inhibitor) should be avoided. Inappropriate withholding of these agents from patients with clear indications deprives them of their benefits.

然而，較早期的報告曾指出 GLP-1 RAs 的使用與 acute pancreatitis 及 pancreatic adenocarcinoma 風險上升有關。FDA 從未建議在 pancreatic cancer 或 acute pancreatitis 高風險族群中避免使用 GLP-1 RA 與 GLP-1/GIP DA；但臨床指引則建議，對於有胰臟炎病史者，應避免使用 incretin 類藥物（即 GLP-1 RA、GLP-1/GIP DA 與 DPP-4 inhibitor）。若對明確有適應症的患者不當地保留這些藥物，反而剝奪了他們應得的療效。

## 🔬 背景與重要性 Background & Significance

### 臨床實務落差 Practice Gaps

- 由於早期報告暗示 GLP-1 RAs 與胰臟炎或 pancreatic cancer 可能相關，加上部分專家的疑慮，臨床醫師常對「被認為有此類風險」的患者避免開立這些藥物。
- 部分醫師會定期監測使用 GLP-1 RA 或 GLP-1/GIP DA 患者的胰臟酵素濃度。
- 目前並不清楚這些預防措施究竟是剝奪了患者的用藥效益，還是屬於合理的避害措施。

### 適應症 Indications

There is robust evidence supporting the efficacy of GLP-1 RA and GLP-1/GIP DA in glycemic control, weight loss, cardiovascular (CV) and kidney protection, and emerging roles in other metabolic and obesity-related conditions.

在血糖控制、體重減輕、心血管（CV）與腎臟保護，以及其他代謝與肥胖相關疾病的新興角色上，GLP-1 RA 與 GLP-1/GIP DA 的療效都有堅實的證據支持。目前一種或多種此類藥物的具體適應症包括：

- 作為飲食與運動輔助，改善成人 T2D 的血糖控制。
- 合併低熱量飲食與增加身體活動，用於成人肥胖、或合併至少一項體重相關共病的過重成人，減少過多體重並維持長期減重。
- 在已確立 CVD 且合併 T2D、肥胖或過重的成人，以及具高風險且合併 T2D 者，降低 major adverse cardiovascular events（MACE）風險。
- 在合併 T2D 與 chronic kidney disease（CKD）的成人，降低 eGFR 持續下降、末期腎病與 CV 死亡的風險。
- 治療合併中度至重度肝纖維化（stage F2 至 F3）的非肝硬化 metabolic dysfunction-associated steatohepatitis（MASH）成人。
- 治療合併肥胖的成人中度至重度 obstructive sleep apnea（OSA）。

The perceived risk of adverse effects (eg, pancreatitis and pancreatic cancer) limits the use of these medications in real-world settings. These conditions occur more often in people with excess weight and diabetes — the very population in which these drugs are most commonly used.

對副作用（如胰臟炎與 pancreatic cancer）的既有印象，限制了真實世界中這些藥物的使用；然而這些疾病本就好發於體重過重與糖尿病族群，而這正是最常使用這些藥物的族群。

### 胰臟腫塊 Pancreatic Masses

A pancreatic mass is identified in about 50% of autopsies and in 15% to 75% of cross-sectional imaging studies, more often in elderly individuals. About one-half to three-fourths of patients with high-risk stigmata and one-quarter of those with worrisome features already have malignancy. Risk of pancreatic cancer goes up with increasing body weight (by 10% for every 5-point increase in BMI) and the presence of diabetes (by 50% [95% CI, 40%-60%]).

約 50% 的解剖與 15% 至 75% 的橫斷面影像檢查可發現胰臟腫塊，年長者更為常見。具 high-risk stigmata 者約有二分之一至四分之三、具 worrisome features 者約有四分之一已存在惡性病變。Pancreatic cancer 的風險隨體重增加（BMI 每上升 5 分增加 10%）與糖尿病存在（增加 50%[95% CI, 40%-60%]）而升高。

### 胰臟癌 Pancreatic Cancer

Although earlier studies suggested a relationship between incretin-based therapy and pancreatic cancer, several recent robust studies have not confirmed this. A recent meta-analysis of randomized, placebo-controlled trials included 48 trials with 94,245 participants and demonstrated, with moderate certainty, that GLP-1 RAs probably have little or no effect on pancreatic cancer risk (odds ratio, 0.84 [95% CI, 0.53-1.35]). Observational studies suggest that GLP-1 RAs, compared with other antidiabetes drugs, may be associated with a lower risk of obesity-related cancers, including pancreatic cancer (vs insulin: hazard ratio, 0.41 [95% CI, 0.33-0.50]).

雖然早期研究曾暗示 incretin 治療與 pancreatic cancer 相關，但近期數項高品質研究並未證實此關聯。一項納入 48 項試驗、94,245 名受試者的 randomized、placebo-controlled trials 統合分析以中度確定性顯示，GLP-1 RAs 對 pancreatic cancer 風險可能幾無影響（odds ratio 0.84[95% CI, 0.53-1.35]）。觀察性研究則顯示，相較於其他降血糖藥物，GLP-1 RAs 可能與較低的肥胖相關癌症（含 pancreatic cancer）風險有關（相較 insulin：hazard ratio 0.41[95% CI, 0.33-0.50]）。

Most often, pancreatic adenocarcinomas arise from precancerous lesions, including intraductal papillary mucinous neoplasms (IPMNs) and mucinous cystic neoplasms. Between 8% and 25% of people with IPMNs develop pancreatic adenocarcinoma over a 10-year period. This risk is higher when more family members have a history of pancreatic cancer (standardized incidence ratio 4.9 [95% CI, 4.0-5.9]), especially at a younger age. No studies have specifically investigated whether GLP-1 RAs modify risk in people at higher-than-normal risk of pancreatic cancer.

多數 pancreatic adenocarcinoma 由癌前病灶演變而來，包括 intraductal papillary mucinous neoplasms（IPMNs）與 mucinous cystic neoplasms。IPMN 患者在 10 年間有 8% 至 25% 會發展為 pancreatic adenocarcinoma；當家族中有更多 pancreatic cancer 病史者（standardized incidence ratio 4.9[95% CI, 4.0-5.9]）、且發病年齡較輕時，此風險更高。因此部分醫療人員對 pancreatic adenocarcinoma 高風險者使用 GLP-1 RAs 較為謹慎。然而，目前尚無研究專門探討 GLP-1 RAs 是否會改變「高於一般風險」族群的 pancreatic cancer 風險。

### 急性胰臟炎 Acute Pancreatitis

Risk factors for acute pancreatitis include hypertriglyceridemia, gallstone disease, excessive alcohol use, and smoking. The 2 major indications for these therapies — diabetes and obesity — by themselves pose a risk for acute pancreatitis: diabetes increases the risk by 75% (95% CI, 33%-129%) and obesity increases it by 180% (95% CI, 80%-330%). Moreover, hypertriglyceridemia — dose-dependently related to local complications, organ failure, and maximum C-reactive protein level — is itself significantly and dose-dependently related to obesity and uncontrolled diabetes.

Acute pancreatitis 的危險因子包括 hypertriglyceridemia、膽結石疾病、過度飲酒與吸菸。這些藥物的兩大主要適應症——糖尿病與肥胖——本身即為 acute pancreatitis 的風險：糖尿病使風險增加 75%（95% CI, 33%-129%），肥胖使風險增加 180%（95% CI, 80%-330%）。此外，hypertriglyceridemia 與胰臟炎的局部併發症、器官衰竭及最高 C-reactive protein 濃度呈劑量相關，而其本身又與肥胖及血糖控制不佳呈顯著的劑量相關。

Recent robust studies based on randomized controlled trials demonstrate no association between GLP-1 RA use and acute pancreatitis. A recent meta-analysis included 55 placebo-controlled trials involving 106,395 participants and found that GLP-1 RA use has little or no effect on pancreatitis risk (relative risk, 0.96 [95% CI, 0.67-1.38]). Other meta-analyses of randomized controlled trials have also shown no association.

近期以 randomized controlled trials 為基礎的高品質研究顯示，GLP-1 RA 使用與 acute pancreatitis 之間並無關聯。一項納入 55 項 placebo-controlled trials、106,395 名受試者的統合分析發現，GLP-1 RA 對胰臟炎風險幾無影響（relative risk 0.96[95% CI, 0.67-1.38]）；其他 RCT 統合分析亦未顯示 GLP-1 RA 與 acute pancreatitis 發作有關。

## 🧭 診斷與評估 Diagnosis & Evaluation

### Acute pancreatitis 的診斷 Diagnosis of Acute Pancreatitis

Diagnosis of acute pancreatitis is established by the presence of 2 of the 3 following:

1. abdominal pain consistent with the disease
2. serum amylase and/or lipase greater than 3 times the upper limit of normal, and/or
3. characteristic findings on abdominal imaging

Acute pancreatitis 的診斷需符合下列三項中的兩項：

1. 與此疾病相符的腹痛
2. 血清 amylase 和／或 lipase 大於正常上限的 3 倍，和／或
3. 腹部影像上的特徵性表現

Abdominal discomfort while taking a GLP-1 RA or GLP-1/GIP DA has been reported from various causes, including a direct effect of the drug, biliary complications, gastroparesis, gastritis, constipation, intussusception, and acute pancreatitis.

使用 GLP-1 RA 或 GLP-1/GIP DA 期間出現的腹部不適可源自多種原因，包括藥物直接作用、膽道併發症、gastroparesis、gastritis、便秘、腸套疊（intussusception）以及 acute pancreatitis。

**疼痛特徵 Character of pain.** GLP-1 RA use is associated with abdominal pain occurring in 10% to 20% of patients, compared with 5% to 10% in placebo groups. This pain is typically mild, transient, dose-dependent, and nonspecific in location and character. Upper abdominal pain, especially with fever, may suggest biliary complications, which occur at higher rates with GLP-1 RA therapy. In contrast, the pain of acute pancreatitis is usually severe, typically in the epigastric region or left upper quadrant, constant, and may radiate to the back, chest, or flanks.

使用 GLP-1 RA 者有 10% 至 20% 出現腹痛，placebo 組則為 5% 至 10%。此類疼痛通常輕微、短暫、具劑量相關，且位置與性質不具特異性。上腹痛（尤其合併發燒）可能提示膽道併發症，而膽道併發症在 GLP-1 RA 治療下發生率較高。相對地，acute pancreatitis 的疼痛通常較劇烈，多位於上腹部或左上腹，呈持續性，可能放射至背部、胸部或腰脅。值得注意的是，GLP-1 RA 試驗中對 acute pancreatitis 僅以「abdominal pain consistent with acute pancreatitis」為標準，並未進一步定義部位、放射或性質。

**酵素判讀 Enzyme interpretation.** Serum lipase is a more specific marker of acute pancreatitis than amylase; however, lipase concentrations increase in a variety of nonpancreatic diseases, sometimes by more than 3 times normal. Even in the absence of acute pancreatitis, GLP-1 RA use is associated with increased pancreatic enzyme levels. At higher dosages, about 3% of people have lipase concentrations more than 3 times the upper limit of the normal reference range.

血清 lipase 比 amylase 對 acute pancreatitis 更具特異性；然而 lipase 在多種非胰臟疾病中也會上升，有時甚至超過正常值的 3 倍。即使沒有 acute pancreatitis，使用 GLP-1 RA 也會伴隨胰臟酵素上升。在較高劑量下，約 3% 的人 lipase 濃度會超過正常參考值上限的 3 倍。

### 膽囊與膽道疾病 Gallbladder and Biliary Diseases

Cholelithiasis, cholecystitis, and biliary disease occur more often with GLP-1 RA treatment. Acute cholecystitis presents with abdominal pain and is a differential diagnosis of acute pancreatitis. Gallstones and biliary sludge are often causes of acute pancreatitis. Therefore, if a person taking a GLP-1 RA presents with abdominal pain, it is imperative to rule out gallbladder and biliary disease as the cause.

Cholelithiasis、cholecystitis 與膽道疾病在 GLP-1 RA 治療下較常發生。Acute cholecystitis 以腹痛表現，是 acute pancreatitis 的鑑別診斷之一；膽結石與 biliary sludge 也常是 acute pancreatitis 的成因。因此，使用 GLP-1 RA 者若出現腹痛，務必排除膽囊與膽道疾病作為疼痛（以及可能的胰臟炎）的成因。

### 小結 Summary

When taken together, a judgment based on a combination of abdominal pain and elevated lipase concentrations may lead to overdiagnosis of acute pancreatitis among people treated with GLP-1 RAs. It is not surprising that some retrospective cohort studies show a positive relationship whereas others do not; meta-analyses of randomized placebo-controlled trials do not confirm this relationship. Therefore, it is imperative that we draw conclusions from randomized placebo-controlled trials rather than from retrospective cohort studies.

綜合來看，僅以「腹痛加上 lipase 升高」的組合作判斷，可能導致 GLP-1 RA 使用者被過度診斷為 acute pancreatitis。因此，部分回溯性 cohort 研究呈現正相關、部分則否，並不令人意外；而 randomized placebo-controlled trials 的統合分析並未證實此關聯。所以我們應以 randomized placebo-controlled trials，而非回溯性 cohort 研究，作為結論依據。

## 💊 治療與處置 Management

### 案例一 Case 1 — 有 hypertriglyceridemia 相關胰臟炎病史者

A 37-year-old woman is referred for discussion of GLP-1 RA treatment to lose weight. At age 18 she was noted to have elevated triglycerides (close to 1000 mg/dL [11.30 mmol/L]). She developed acute pancreatitis in her early 20s while on oral contraceptives, then had many episodes of acute pancreatitis with hypertriglyceridemia until she experienced temporary relief with plasmapheresis, ileal bypass, and vertical banded gastroplasty. After regaining weight, she had recurrent episodes with severe hypertriglyceridemia. She is not currently symptomatic. BP 118/87 mm Hg, pulse 84 beats/min, temperature 98.1°F (36.7°C); height 67 in (170.2 cm), weight 227.1 lb (103 kg), BMI 35.6 kg/m². The abdomen is mildly and diffusely tender to deep palpation, with no masses.

37 歲女性因討論 GLP-1 RA 減重而轉診。18 歲時發現 triglycerides 升高（近 1000 mg/dL[11.30 mmol/L]）；20 歲出頭服用口服避孕藥時發生 acute pancreatitis，之後在 hypertriglyceridemia 下多次發作，曾以 plasmapheresis、ileal bypass 與 vertical banded gastroplasty 獲得暫時緩解。體重回升後又反覆發作並伴隨嚴重 hypertriglyceridemia，目前無症狀。BMI 35.6 kg/m²，腹部深觸診有輕微瀰漫壓痛、無腫塊。

**最佳建議 Best advice：D) GLP-1 RA therapy should be considered for her — 應考慮為她使用 GLP-1 RA 治療。**

Several analyses of randomized, placebo-controlled trials demonstrate that GLP-1 RAs are not associated with an increased risk of acute pancreatitis. In patients with a history of acute pancreatitis, a recent retrospective cohort study found that GLP-1 RAs are associated with a lower risk of pancreatitis than SGLT-2 inhibitors or DPP-4 inhibitors: over one year, risk reductions of 0.071 (95% CI, –0.085 to –0.057) vs SGLT-2 inhibitors, and 0.064 (95% CI, –0.080 to –0.048) vs DPP-4 inhibitors.

多項 randomized、placebo-controlled trials 分析顯示 GLP-1 RAs 與 acute pancreatitis 風險上升無關。在有 acute pancreatitis 病史者中，近期一項回溯性 cohort 研究發現，GLP-1 RAs 較 SGLT-2 inhibitors 或 DPP-4 inhibitors 有更低的胰臟炎風險：一年內相較 SGLT-2 inhibitors 的風險下降 0.071（95% CI, –0.085 至 –0.057），相較 DPP-4 inhibitors 下降 0.064（95% CI, –0.080 至 –0.048）。

The postprandial rise in triglycerides was completely abolished during GLP-1 infusion in healthy volunteers. In a study of people with lipodystrophy, 1 year of GLP-1 RA therapy significantly reduced triglyceride levels; of 26 patients, the 1 who was not adherent developed pancreatitis, while 2 women adhering to therapy had no further episode over 11 and 8 years of follow-up despite pancreatitis 6 and 20 years earlier. A case report also suggests that GLP-1 RA-associated weight loss reduces the risk of recurrent hypertriglyceridemia-related pancreatitis.

在健康志願者中，GLP-1 輸注可完全消除餐後 triglycerides 上升。在 lipodystrophy 患者研究中，1 年 GLP-1 RA 治療顯著降低 triglyceride；26 名患者中唯一未遵醫囑者發生胰臟炎，而 2 名遵從治療的女性在先前分別於 6 年及 20 年前發生過胰臟炎後，追蹤 11 年與 8 年間均未再發作。案例報告亦顯示 GLP-1 RA 相關的減重可降低 hypertriglyceridemia 相關胰臟炎的復發風險。因此，對本例使用 GLP-1 RA 是合理的；密切追蹤 triglycerides 可能為適當作法。

### 案例二 Case 2 — 使用後出現 hyperamylasemia／hyperlipasemia

A 65-year-old man initiates semaglutide to manage diabetes with coronary heart disease. Six months of GLP-1 RA therapy has improved his hemoglobin A1c and led to weight loss (3.3 lb [1.5 kg]), but he has developed hyperamylasemia and hyperlipasemia, and semaglutide has been stopped.

65 歲男性因糖尿病合併冠心病開始使用 semaglutide。6 個月治療改善了 HbA1c 並減重（3.3 lb[1.5 kg]），但出現 hyperamylasemia 與 hyperlipasemia，semaglutide 已被停用。

**最佳建議 Best advice：A) Elevated pancreatic enzyme levels may occur with GLP-1 RAs without pancreatitis, reflecting adaptive pancreatic growth rather than inflammation — 使用 GLP-1 RAs 時可在無胰臟炎下出現胰臟酵素上升，反映的是適應性胰臟生長而非發炎。**

GLP-1 RA use is associated with increases in median lipase and pancreatic amylase levels, not associated with adverse events. Within the first 6 months, liraglutide increases lipase 4 times more than the 7% increase with placebo; elevations are reversible on discontinuation. At higher doses, lipase elevations above the upper normal limit occur 3 times more often than placebo, and elevations greater than 3 times the upper normal limit occur 2 times more often than placebo. Enzyme elevations have a very low positive-predictive value for acute pancreatitis (<1%).

GLP-1 RA 使用與 lipase 及 pancreatic amylase 中位數上升有關，但與不良事件無關。前 6 個月內，liraglutide 使 lipase 上升的幅度為 placebo（上升 7%）的 4 倍；停藥後可回復。在較高劑量下，lipase 超過正常上限的發生率為 placebo 的 3 倍，超過正常上限 3 倍者則為 placebo 的 2 倍。酵素上升對 acute pancreatitis 的陽性預測值極低（<1%）。

Review of data provides no basis for routine amylase/lipase monitoring during liraglutide treatment except in cases of suspected acute pancreatitis. GLP-1 receptor activation induces signaling in the exocrine pancreas that promotes cell growth and concomitant enzyme release, suggesting that increases reflect adaptive growth rather than early-stage pancreatitis; histopathological studies in animal models showed no evidence of pancreatitis, inflammation, or structural changes despite enzyme increases. The diagnostic criteria for acute pancreatitis should not be modified for GLP-1 RA users, but clinicians should apply existing criteria with heightened awareness that both abdominal pain and enzyme elevations can occur independently of pancreatitis.

資料回顧顯示，除疑似 acute pancreatitis 的情況外，liraglutide 治療期間並無例行監測 amylase/lipase 的依據。GLP-1 receptor 活化會誘發 exocrine pancreas 促進細胞生長與伴隨的酵素釋放的訊號路徑，提示酵素上升反映的是適應性生長而非早期胰臟炎；動物模型的組織病理研究在酵素上升的同時並未見胰臟炎、發炎或結構變化。對 GLP-1 RA 使用者不應修改 acute pancreatitis 的診斷標準，但臨床醫師應在套用既有標準時，提高警覺——腹痛與酵素上升在此族群中皆可獨立於胰臟炎而發生。腹痛與異常升高的 lipase 濃度會在停用 GLP-1 RA 後恢復正常。

### 案例三 Case 3 — 合併低風險 IPMN

A 56-year-old man with obesity (BMI 38.4 kg/m²) has had T2D for 10 years. After a recent myocardial infarction, GLP-1 RA therapy is being considered for glycemic control and reduction in atherosclerotic CVD risk. He has an 8-mm IPMN with an average growth rate of less than 1 mm per year over 3 years without increase in the solid component. Main pancreatic duct diameter is less than 5 mm; there are no mural nodules, no enhancing septations, and no lymphadenopathy. He has no history of pancreatitis or elevated pancreatic enzymes, and CA 19-9 is normal.

56 歲肥胖男性（BMI 38.4 kg/m²），T2D 病史 10 年。近期心肌梗塞後，考慮以 GLP-1 RA 治療兼顧血糖控制與降低 atherosclerotic CVD 風險。他有一顆 8 mm 的 IPMN，過去 3 年平均生長速率每年小於 1 mm、固體成分無增加。Main pancreatic duct 直徑小於 5 mm；無 mural nodule、無強化間隔、無 lymphadenopathy。無胰臟炎或胰臟酵素上升病史，CA 19-9 正常。

**最佳建議 Best advice：D) Risk of pancreatic carcinoma is unknown and GLP-1 RA therapy should be considered — pancreatic carcinoma 風險未知，仍應考慮使用 GLP-1 RA 治療。**

This patient has low-risk IPMN. GLP-1 RA therapy may be cautiously initiated for a patient with a low-risk 0.9-cm IPMN and diabetes without worrisome features, provided routine imaging surveillance continues and the patient is closely monitored. The potential CV and metabolic benefits outweigh the theoretical risk of IPMN progression in low-risk cases.

本例屬 low-risk IPMN。對於一顆低風險 0.9-cm IPMN、合併糖尿病且無 worrisome features 的患者，只要持續例行影像監測並密切追蹤，即可謹慎啟動 GLP-1 RA 治療。在低風險個案中，GLP-1 RA 潛在的 CV 與代謝效益超過 IPMN 進展的理論風險。

In patients with T2D, long-acting GLP-1 RAs (injectable liraglutide, oral and injectable semaglutide, and injectable dulaglutide) demonstrated a 14% reduction in MACE, 14% reduction in hospitalization for heart failure, and 12% reduction in all-cause mortality across 10 trials involving more than 71,000 patients. Pancreatic cysts were not explicitly listed as an exclusion criterion in the CVD trials, and data on direct effect on IPMN progression remain limited and largely theoretical. Shared decision-making with patients with IPMNs should begin with an understanding of the unknown risk posed by IPMN and GLP-1 RA use, alongside the clear CV benefits.

在 T2D 患者中，long-acting GLP-1 RAs（注射型 liraglutide、口服與注射型 semaglutide、注射型 dulaglutide）在納入超過 71,000 名患者的 10 項試驗中，顯示 MACE 下降 14%、心衰竭住院下降 14%、all-cause mortality 下降 12%。在為降低 atherosclerotic CVD 事件而核准的試驗中，pancreatic cysts 並未被明列為排除條件；有關對 IPMN 進展直接影響的資料仍有限且多屬理論性。與 IPMN 患者的 shared decision-making，應從理解「IPMN 與 GLP-1 RA 使用所帶來的未知風險」以及「明確的 CV 效益」開始。

## 🧠 個案解析與臨床推理 Case Analysis & Clinical Reasoning

**核心命題。** 三個案例共同傳達一個訊息：胰臟炎與 pancreatic cancer 的風險並非使用 GLP-1 RA／GLP-1/GIP DA 的絕對禁忌。臨床醫師的常見錯誤，是把「早期觀察性報告的疑慮」當成「因果證據」，因而對明確有適應症的患者卻步。來源強調應以 randomized placebo-controlled trials 為判斷依據，而非回溯性 cohort。

**鑑別診斷 Differential diagnosis（使用者出現腹痛時）。** 需系統性排除藥物直接作用、biliary disease（cholelithiasis／cholecystitis／biliary sludge）、gastroparesis、gastritis、便秘、intussusception，最後才是 acute pancreatitis。上腹痛合併發燒偏向膽道併發症；劇烈、持續、放射至背部的上腹或左上腹痛才較符合胰臟炎。

**常見陷阱 Pitfalls。**
- 把單獨的 lipase 或 amylase 上升當作胰臟炎診斷——酵素上升對 acute pancreatitis 陽性預測值 <1%，且 lipase 在多種非胰臟疾病亦會升高。
- 例行監測胰臟酵素——除疑似胰臟炎外，並無實證依據，反而導致過度診斷與不必要停藥。
- 對 hypertriglyceridemia 相關胰臟炎病史者一律禁用——GLP-1 RA 的減重與降 triglyceride 作用，理論上反而可能降低復發風險。
- 對低風險 IPMN 一律視為禁忌——high-risk stigmata 與 worrisome features 才是需要提高警覺的依據。

**決策要點 Decision points。** 診斷 acute pancreatitis 仍應嚴守「三取二」標準，但需同時警覺此族群腹痛與酵素上升可獨立於胰臟炎發生。低風險 IPMN 可在持續影像監測下謹慎啟用。真正的絕對禁忌另有其類，見下方重點整理。

## ⭐ 重點整理 Key Takeaways

- 高達三分之一的 GLP-1 RA 使用者可能出現胰臟酵素上升；作為預防而例行監測胰臟酵素無助於預測胰臟炎（陽性預測值 <1%）。
- 胰臟炎病史並非 GLP-1 RA 的禁忌。雖然曾有 acute pancreatitis 者再發風險較高，但觀察性研究與案例報告顯示 GLP-1 RA 使用並不增加風險；若透過減重與大幅降低 triglycerides，反而可能降低胰臟炎風險（尚待嚴謹證實）。
- 早期研究曾暗示 GLP-1 RA 與 pancreatic malignancy 相關，但多項 RCT 分析並未顯示風險增加；對高風險族群的影響則仍未知，須以 shared decision-making、multidisciplinary input 與持續 surveillance 因應。
- 酵素上升反映的是 GLP-1 receptor 訊號驅動的 exocrine pancreas 適應性生長，而非發炎；停藥後可回復，動物模型無胰臟炎或結構變化證據。
- 使用者出現腹痛時，務必先排除 gallbladder 與 biliary disease（cholelithiasis／cholecystitis／biliary sludge）等可治療且可能引發胰臟炎的成因。
- 低風險 IPMN（無 high-risk stigmata 或 worrisome features）可在持續影像監測下謹慎使用 GLP-1 RA；CV 與代謝效益通常超過理論上的進展風險。
- Acute pancreatitis 的診斷標準不應為 GLP-1 RA 使用者而更動，但套用時須提高警覺：腹痛與酵素上升在此族群可獨立於胰臟炎而出現。
- 真正的禁忌為：personal 或 family history of medullary thyroid cancer 或 MEN type 2、懷孕與哺乳期、以及對藥物 hypersensitivity。
