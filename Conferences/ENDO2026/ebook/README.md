# 2026 Endocrine Case Management — Meet the Professor (Bilingual zh-TW / English)

Bilingual (Traditional Chinese, Taiwan usage + original English) edition of the
Endocrine Society's *2026 Endocrine Case Management: Meet the Professor* ebook.

## How it was made

- Tool: [bilingual_book_maker](https://github.com/yihong0618/bilingual_book_maker)
- Model: Google `gemini-3.5-flash`
- Target language: 繁體中文（台灣用語, zh-Hant）
- Mode: single-paragraph translation (each source paragraph is followed by its
  Traditional Chinese translation, so the two stay aligned)

## Translation policy

專有醫學名詞一律保留英文（不加中文翻譯）：疾病名、藥名與荷爾蒙名、檢驗與單位
（如 TSH、HbA1c、fT4）、解剖與病理名詞、臨床試驗與指引名稱、標準醫學縮寫。
一般敘述性文字則翻為自然流暢的台灣繁體中文。

Specialized medical terminology (disease names, drugs/hormones, lab tests and
units, anatomical/pathological terms, trial and guideline names, standard
abbreviations) is kept in English with no Chinese gloss; ordinary narrative text
is rendered into natural Traditional Chinese as used in Taiwan.
