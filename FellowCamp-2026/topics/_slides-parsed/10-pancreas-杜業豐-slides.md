# NIPHS

1999 Mayo Clinic, 命名一個新的低血糖症後群

## 特徵：

* Neuroglycopenia (exclusively <span style="color: red">postprandial</span>)
* <mark><span style="color: red">Negative 72-h fast</span></mark>
* Positive ASVS (SACS) 選擇性動脈鈣刺激試驗陽性
* <span style="color: lightgrey">Islet hypertrophy/ nesidioblastosis</span>
* No disease causing mutation in Kir6.2 and SUR1 genes

## 治療：

* 飲食調整、少量多餐、AGI
* Diazoxide/ Octreotide
* Distal pancreatectomy (較難決定要切掉多少)

# Nesidioblastosis?

被誤用的term

## Nesidioblastosis的原始定義：

* Islet cells differentiating out of the duct epithelium to build islets, called **nesidioblasts** – islet builders
* **Nesidioblastosis**: diffuse proliferation of nesidioblasts (ductuloinsular complex)
* Ductuloinsular complex 在36.7%的正常人可見

## NIPHS真正有意義的病理特徵：

* Multiple β-cells with <span style="color: red">**enlarged hyperchromatic nuclei**</span> and clear cytoplasm

Arterial stimulation and venous sampling (ASVS)
=Selective arterial calcium stimulation (SAVS)
=Intra-arterial Calcium Stimulation test (IACS)

* β-cell 破壞速度非常快
