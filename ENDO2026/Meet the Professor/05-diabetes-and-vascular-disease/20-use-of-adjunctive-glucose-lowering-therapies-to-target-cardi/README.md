## 🩺 臨床情境 Clinical Scenario

本章附有兩則臨床病例，忠實呈現如下。

### Case 1

> A 42-year-old man with a 25-year history of T1D presents for follow-up. His medical history is notable for celiac disease, which was diagnosed 1 year before T1D. He uses a hybrid closed-loop insulin pump and a continuous glucose monitor. His total daily insulin dose (insulin aspart) is 97.2 units (59% basal insulin). His hemoglobin A1c value is 7.1% (54 mmol/mol). Over the preceding 2 weeks, 60% of his glucose values were in the target range of 70 to 180 mg/dL (3.9–10 mmol/L), with no significant hypoglycemia. He has never had to use glucagon. His glucose coefficient of variation is 31.4%. On physical examination, his weight is 195 lb (88.5 kg) (BMI = 30.6 kg/m²).

一位 42 歲男性，T1D 病史 25 年，前來追蹤。病史值得注意的是 celiac disease，於 T1D 診斷前 1 年確診。他使用 hybrid closed-loop insulin pump 與 continuous glucose monitor（CGM）。每日總胰島素劑量（insulin aspart）為 97.2 units（其中 59% 為基礎胰島素）。HbA1c 為 7.1%（54 mmol/mol）。過去 2 週有 60% 的血糖值落在 70–180 mg/dL（3.9–10 mmol/L）的目標範圍內，且無明顯低血糖，從未需要使用 glucagon。血糖變異係數（coefficient of variation, CV）為 31.4%。理學檢查體重 195 lb（88.5 kg），BMI = 30.6 kg/m²。

> He would like to discuss the benefits and risks of oral diabetes adjunctive agents. He is particularly interested in using metformin and empagliflozin to reduce his total daily insulin dose and weight, with the ultimate aim of improving his CV risk profile.

他希望討論口服輔助性糖尿病藥物的效益與風險，特別想使用 metformin 與 empagliflozin 來減少每日總胰島素劑量與體重，最終目標是改善其 CV 風險。

### Case 2

> A 51-year-old man with a 32-year history of T1D consults you for the first time. His treatment includes insulin lispro, 10 units with meals, and insulin glargine, 15 units twice daily. He has taken rosuvastatin, 10 mg daily, and aspirin, 100 mg daily, for the past 5 years. He was hospitalized for diabetic ketoacidosis 11 years ago. His diabetes is complicated by proliferative retinopathy that was treated with laser therapy 15 years ago. His retinopathy is inactive. His history includes Addison's disease, coronary artery disease (stent inserted), hypertension, and dyslipidemia. His hemoglobin A1c value is 9.8% (84 mmol/mol), and weight is 275 lb (125 kg) (BMI 40.8 kg/m²). His insulin dose is 0.48 units/kg per day. He is keen to trial adjunctive semaglutide.

一位 51 歲男性，T1D 病史 32 年，首次前來就診。治療包含 insulin lispro（隨餐 10 units）與 insulin glargine（15 units、每日兩次）。過去 5 年服用 rosuvastatin 10 mg/day 及 aspirin 100 mg/day。11 年前曾因 diabetic ketoacidosis（DKA）住院。糖尿病併發 proliferative retinopathy，15 年前接受 laser therapy，目前 retinopathy 已無活動性。病史另有 Addison's disease、coronary artery disease（曾置放 stent）、hypertension 與 dyslipidemia。HbA1c 為 9.8%（84 mmol/mol），體重 275 lb（125 kg），BMI 40.8 kg/m²，胰島素劑量 0.48 units/kg/day。他很想嘗試輔助性 semaglutide。

## 🔬 背景與重要性 Background & Significance

### 臨床問題的重要性 Significance of the Clinical Problem

> Cardiovascular disease (CVD) is the leading cause of morbidity and mortality in adults with T1D and occurs at a younger age than in those with T2D. CVD risk factors, which vary with age and duration of T1D, include hyperglycemia (related to insulin deficiency and insulin resistance), hypertension, kidney disease, dyslipidemia, obesity, and smoking.

Cardiovascular disease（CVD）是 T1D 成人罹病與死亡的首要原因，且發生年齡比 T2D 患者更早。CVD 的風險因子會隨年齡與 T1D 病程而變化，包括高血糖（與 insulin deficiency 及 insulin resistance 有關）、hypertension、腎臟疾病、dyslipidemia、肥胖與吸菸。

> Although not discussed in detail here, antihypertensive and LDL cholesterol–lowering medications confer CVD benefit in people with diabetes, although more studies have focused on T2D than on T1D. Statins have been shown to reduce lipids equally in T1D and T2D. In the Heart Protection Study, the reduction in CVD events among 615 adults with T1D compared with 5348 adults with T2D was not significantly different, although the confidence intervals were wide. The Endocrine Society guideline, Lipid Management in Patients with Endocrine Disorders, made a conditional recommendation to initiate statin therapy irrespective of CV risk score in adults with T1D aged 40 years and older with a 20-year duration of diabetes and/or microvascular complications.

抗高血壓與降低 LDL cholesterol 的藥物對糖尿病患者具 CVD 益處（本章未深入討論），但相關研究以 T2D 居多、T1D 較少。Statins 在 T1D 與 T2D 降血脂的幅度相當。在 Heart Protection Study 中，615 位 T1D 成人與 5348 位 T2D 成人相比，CVD 事件的降幅並無顯著差異，但信賴區間較寬。Endocrine Society 的指引《Lipid Management in Patients with Endocrine Disorders》做出條件式建議：對於年齡 40 歲以上、糖尿病病程達 20 年且／或已有 microvascular complications 的 T1D 成人，不論 CV risk score 為何，皆應啟動 statin 治療。

> This chapter focuses on medications that improve glycemic control and specifically target CVD outcomes in T1D.

本章聚焦於「能改善血糖控制、並針對 T1D 之 CVD 預後」的藥物。

### Insulin resistance 是被低估的 CV 風險因子

> Beyond insulin deficiency, insulin resistance is an underrecognized and undertreated CV risk factor in this population. Insulin resistance is present in adolescents and adults living with T1D. It is induced, in part, by nonphysiological insulin delivery via the subcutaneous route, which results in portal hypoinsulinemia and peripheral hyperinsulinemia, thereby disrupting the normal balance of insulin exposure between the liver and peripheral tissues.

除了 insulin deficiency 之外，insulin resistance 是此族群中被低估且治療不足的 CV 風險因子，青少年與成人 T1D 患者皆存在此現象。它部分源自經皮下途徑的非生理性胰島素給予，導致 portal hypoinsulinemia（門脈低胰島素）與 peripheral hyperinsulinemia（周邊高胰島素），破壞了肝臟與周邊組織之間正常的胰島素暴露平衡。

> Peripheral hyperinsulinemia is associated with weight gain, which compounds insulin resistance, and with impaired glucose disposal, inappropriate free fatty acid release, GH hypersecretion (due to low IGF-1), and amplification of prandial glucagon secretion due to inadequate insulin exposure to hepatic tissue and pancreatic α-cells. These defects combine to increase CV risk in T1D and are potential therapeutic targets to improve CV outcomes.

Peripheral hyperinsulinemia 與體重增加相關（進而加重 insulin resistance），也與葡萄糖處置受損、free fatty acid 不當釋放、GH 過度分泌（因 IGF-1 偏低）以及餐後 glucagon 分泌放大（因肝組織與 pancreatic α-cells 接觸的胰島素不足）有關。這些缺陷共同升高 T1D 的 CV 風險，也是改善 CV 預後的潛在治療標的。

### 發炎與內臟脂肪 Inflammation and visceral adiposity

> Inflammation is a potential link among insulin resistance, atherothrombosis, and ischemic heart disease. Insulin resistance increases the production of proinflammatory cytokines, which can further impair insulin signaling. Inflammation contributes to early atherogenesis and progression of atherosclerotic lesions, and circulating inflammatory markers strongly correlate with ischemic events.

發炎可能是連結 insulin resistance、atherothrombosis 與 ischemic heart disease 的橋樑。Insulin resistance 會增加促發炎細胞激素的產生，進一步損害 insulin signaling。發炎參與早期 atherogenesis 與 atherosclerotic 病灶的進展，且循環中的發炎指標與缺血事件高度相關。

> Furthermore, body composition and fat distribution may be important. Visceral adipose tissue (VAT) is a hyperinflammatory fat depot that is more metabolically harmful than subcutaneous adipose tissue (SAT). VAT secretes a greater burden of proinflammatory cytokines than SAT, including interleukin-6, tumor necrosis factor-α, and monocyte chemotactic protein-1. These circulating inflammatory mediators may link adipose tissue inflammation to vascular inflammation, thereby increasing CV risk. Indeed, inflammation is a strong predictor of adverse CV outcomes in people with T1D.

此外，身體組成與脂肪分布可能也很重要。Visceral adipose tissue（VAT，內臟脂肪）是一種高度發炎的脂肪庫，代謝上比 subcutaneous adipose tissue（SAT，皮下脂肪）更具傷害性。VAT 分泌的促發炎細胞激素負荷高於 SAT，包括 interleukin-6、tumor necrosis factor-α 與 monocyte chemotactic protein-1。這些循環發炎介質可能將脂肪組織發炎連結至血管發炎，進而升高 CV 風險。事實上，發炎是 T1D 患者不良 CV 預後的強力預測因子。

> SGLT-2 inhibitors and GLP-1 receptor agonists have cardiometabolic protective effects in people with and without T2D. Adults and children with T1D have been excluded from CV outcome trials. Given the elevated CV risk in T1D, there is a compelling need to consider these medication classes. Efficacy and safety data from well-designed randomized controlled trials are needed to support their use in this patient population.

SGLT-2 inhibitors 與 GLP-1 receptor agonists 對有或無 T2D 者皆具心血管代謝保護效果。然而 T1D 的成人與兒童一直被排除於 CV outcome trials 之外。鑑於 T1D 的 CV 風險升高，實有必要認真考慮這些藥物類別。要支持其在此族群使用，仍需來自設計良好的隨機對照試驗（RCT）的療效與安全性資料。

### 現行照護的落差 Practice Gaps

- **需更深入了解「特別造成 T1D 中 CV 風險升高」的機轉。**
- **需要指引以辨識哪些 T1D 患者屬於特別高 CV 風險（部分源自 insulin resistance），而最可能從輔助性心臟保護代謝療法中獲益。**
- **需更深入了解這些輔助性心血管代謝療法在 T1D 的作用機轉、風險與效益。**

> Only a few guidelines address the benefits and safe use of cardioprotective adjunctive agents in people with T1D. An in-depth understanding of the mechanisms of action, potential metabolic benefits, and risks associated with these medications is essential before they are offered off-label. In many cases, their use is driven by patients, based on data from mostly retrospective case note reviews of metabolic benefits. Few randomized, placebo-controlled trials have been published, and evidence from CV outcome trials is unavailable.

僅有少數指引處理心臟保護輔助藥物在 T1D 的效益與安全使用。在以 off-label 方式提供這些藥物前，深入理解其作用機轉、潛在代謝益處與相關風險至關重要。許多情況下，其使用是由患者主導，且多半根據回溯性病歷回顧所示的代謝效益。目前少有隨機、安慰劑對照試驗發表，也尚無 CV outcome trials 的證據。

## 🧭 診斷與評估 Diagnosis & Evaluation

### 針對輔助治療候選者的評估重點

本章雖以治療為核心，但在評估 T1D 患者是否適合輔助療法時，有數項重要的病理生理與檢驗觀念需釐清。

**關於 T1D 的兩個常見迷思 Misconceptions about T1D**

> The first concerns the assumption that people with T1D cannot secrete glucagon. Rather, despite a blunted glucagon response to hypoglycemia, people with T1D exhibit paradoxical, exaggerated postprandial glucagon secretion, which contributes to postprandial hyperglycemia. In addition, aberrant glucagon secretion contributes to glycemic variability in T1D. Because exogenous insulin does not reach the paracrine levels of insulin secreted from β cells, it is ineffective in lowering postprandial glucagon levels.

第一個迷思是「T1D 患者無法分泌 glucagon」。事實上，儘管他們對低血糖的 glucagon 反應變得遲鈍，T1D 患者卻表現出矛盾性、過度的餐後 glucagon 分泌，導致餐後高血糖；異常的 glucagon 分泌也造成 T1D 的血糖變異性。由於外源性 insulin 無法達到 β cells 旁分泌（paracrine）的胰島素濃度，因此在降低餐後 glucagon 上並無效果。

> The second misconception is that most people with T1D cannot secrete insulin. Residual insulin secretion is, in fact, present in many individuals with T1D. It is best detected using high-sensitivity C-peptide assays (eg, ultrasensitive ELISA) after a meal, rather than during fasting. Fasting C-peptide is detectable in more than one-third of those with longstanding T1D, especially with postpubertal onset, and stimulated C-peptide (90 minutes after a mixed-meal tolerance test) is detectable in most (insulin microsecretors).

第二個迷思是「大多數 T1D 患者無法分泌 insulin」。事實上許多 T1D 患者仍有殘餘的胰島素分泌（residual insulin secretion）。最佳偵測方式是使用高敏感度 C-peptide 檢驗（如 ultrasensitive ELISA），並在餐後而非空腹時檢測。長病程 T1D 中有超過三分之一可測得空腹 C-peptide（尤其是青春期後發病者），而刺激後 C-peptide（於 mixed-meal tolerance test 後 90 分鐘）在大多數人皆可測得，此即所謂「insulin microsecretors（胰島素微分泌者）」。

**C-peptide 檢驗的臨床判讀（源自 Case 2 解析）**

- 在 T1D，測量**刺激後（stimulated）**C-peptide 比空腹 C-peptide 更具參考價值。
- 與 liraglutide 不同，目前**沒有證據顯示 C-peptide 的存在可預測對 semaglutide 的反應**；因此不應以「空腹 C-peptide 測不到」作為排除使用 semaglutide 的依據。

**啟用 SGLT-2 inhibitor 前後的安全監測**

> Ensuring routine home blood ketone monitoring with clear sick-day rules is the most important preventive strategy.

在 T1D 使用 SGLT-2 inhibitor 時，**建立常規居家血酮監測與明確的病日守則（sick-day rules）**是預防 DKA 最重要的策略。病人衛教應包含：

- 於生病、禁食、不明原因噁心或持續高血糖時，測量血酮。
- 即使進食減少，也**絕不停用基礎胰島素**。
- 於併發疾病、手術或長時間禁食期間，暫時停用 SGLT-2 inhibitor。
- 若已採取矯正措施但血酮仍高，應立即就醫。

## 💊 治療與處置 Management

### Metformin

> Metformin improves insulin sensitivity and surrogate measures of vascular health (as measured by MRI-derived aortic pulse wave velocity) in youth with T1D. In a 3-month randomized placebo-controlled trial of 48 adolescents with T1D, those randomly assigned to metformin, 2000 mg daily, exhibited more favorable changes in vascular health, insulin sensitivity, weight, and fat mass than placebo-treated individuals. Interestingly, insulin sensitivity also improved in normal-weight participants. Metformin had no significant effect on hemoglobin A1c, total daily insulin dose, blood pressure, or lipid profile.

Metformin 可改善 T1D 青少年的 insulin sensitivity 與血管健康的替代指標（以 MRI 測得的 aortic pulse wave velocity 評估）。在一項為期 3 個月、納入 48 位 T1D 青少年的隨機安慰劑對照試驗中，隨機分配至 metformin 2000 mg/day 者，其血管健康、insulin sensitivity、體重與脂肪量的變化皆優於安慰劑組。值得注意的是，即使是正常體重的受試者，insulin sensitivity 也獲改善。Metformin 對 HbA1c、每日總胰島素劑量、血壓或血脂並無顯著影響。

> In a recently published 26-week randomized, placebo-controlled trial of 40 adults with T1D, metformin had no effect on hemoglobin A1c, liver or muscle insulin sensitivity (measured by clamp), adiposity, liver fat, or arterial stiffness. However, metformin reduced the insulin dose by 0.1 units/kg per day compared with placebo. The lack of change in insulin resistance suggests that metformin may have metabolic effects in T1D that are independent of changes in insulin action.

在最近發表、納入 40 位 T1D 成人、為期 26 週的隨機安慰劑對照試驗中，metformin 對 HbA1c、以 clamp 測得的肝臟或肌肉 insulin sensitivity、脂肪量、肝臟脂肪或動脈硬度皆無影響。然而，相較於安慰劑，metformin 使胰島素劑量減少 0.1 units/kg/day。Insulin resistance 未見改變，顯示 metformin 在 T1D 的代謝效果可能獨立於 insulin action 的變化之外。

> In the REMOVAL trial of 428 adults with T1D, metformin had no effect on the progression of mean carotid artery intima media thickness (cIMT, the primary endpoint) over 3 years, although maximal cIMT (a prespecified tertiary outcome) was significantly reduced compared with placebo. Hemoglobin A1c improved with metformin, driven by a temporary 0.24% reduction at 3 months (P < .0001). Metformin significantly reduced weight by 1.17 kg and LDL cholesterol by 0.13 mmol/L, and increased the estimated glomerular filtration rate by 4 mL/min per 1.73 m². The insulin dose was 0.023 units/kg per day lower in those assigned to metformin (P = .0018).

在納入 428 位 T1D 成人的 REMOVAL trial 中，metformin 對 3 年間的平均 carotid artery intima media thickness（cIMT，主要終點）進展並無影響，但最大 cIMT（預設的三級終點）相較安慰劑顯著下降。Metformin 使 HbA1c 改善，主要來自 3 個月時暫時性的 0.24% 下降（P < .0001）。Metformin 顯著降低體重 1.17 kg、LDL cholesterol 0.13 mmol/L，並使 eGFR 增加 4 mL/min/1.73 m²。Metformin 組的胰島素劑量較低 0.023 units/kg/day（P = .0018）。

> Taken together, these results suggest that metformin may have insulin-sensitizing and favorable metabolic effects in adolescents with T1D and, to a lesser extent, in adults, with the predominant effect appearing to be related to insulin dose reduction.

綜合而言，metformin 在 T1D 青少年可能具 insulin-sensitizing 與有利的代謝效果，在成人則效果較弱，主要效益似乎與減少胰島素劑量有關。

> 💡 重點：Metformin 已核准用於 T2D，但**未核准用於 T1D（法國除外）**。

### SGLT-2 Inhibitors

> SGLT-2 inhibitors lower glucose by promoting renal tubular glucose excretion and have associated natriuretic effects. This medication class is approved for use in T2D to improve glycemic control, with trial data demonstrating significant benefits for CV, heart failure, and kidney outcomes.

SGLT-2 inhibitors 藉由促進腎小管的葡萄糖排泄來降低血糖，並具有相關的排鈉（natriuretic）作用。此藥物類別已核准用於 T2D 改善血糖控制，試驗資料顯示對 CV、heart failure 與腎臟預後有顯著益處。

> Dapagliflozin was approved by the European Medicines Agency in 2019 for use in patients with T1D and overweight or obesity, and by the Japanese Ministry of Health, Labour, and Welfare for the treatment of T1D in patients aged 15 years and older with insufficient glycemic control. However, the European Medicines Agency approval was retracted in 2021 due to concerns regarding the risk of (euglycemic) ketoacidosis. In a study using a propensity score-matched cohort of Japanese individuals with T1D, the incidence of ketoacidosis was slightly higher in patients prescribed dapagliflozin than in nonusers, but the confidence intervals were wide and overlapped. The introduction of continuous ketone monitoring may reduce this risk in the future.

Dapagliflozin 於 2019 年獲 European Medicines Agency（EMA）核准用於 T1D 且過重或肥胖者，並獲日本厚生勞動省核准用於 15 歲以上、血糖控制不足的 T1D 患者。然而 EMA 的核准於 2021 年因（正常血糖性）ketoacidosis 風險疑慮而撤回。一項以 propensity score 配對的日本 T1D 世代研究中，使用 dapagliflozin 者的 ketoacidosis 發生率略高於未使用者，但信賴區間寬且重疊。未來引進 continuous ketone monitoring（連續酮體監測）或可降低此風險。

**EASE 試驗計畫 The EASE Trial Program**

> The Empagliflozin as Adjunct to inSulin therapy (EASE) trial program included 2 large double-blind, placebo-controlled phase 3 trials of empagliflozin in patients with T1D. EASE-2 studied empagliflozin 10 mg (n = 243) and 25 mg (n = 244) vs placebo (n = 243) over 52 weeks. EASE-3 used empagliflozin 2.5 mg (n = 241), 10 mg (n = 248), and 25 mg (n = 245) vs placebo (n = 241) over 26 weeks.

EASE（Empagliflozin as Adjunct to inSulin therapy）試驗計畫包含 2 項大型雙盲、安慰劑對照的 phase 3 試驗，探討 empagliflozin 用於 T1D。EASE-2 比較 empagliflozin 10 mg（n = 243）與 25 mg（n = 244）對比安慰劑（n = 243），為期 52 週。EASE-3 比較 empagliflozin 2.5 mg（n = 241）、10 mg（n = 248）、25 mg（n = 245）對比安慰劑（n = 241），為期 26 週。

| 收縮壓下降 SBP | 2.1 mm Hg | 至多 3.9 mm Hg | 至多 3.9 mm Hg | — |
| DKA 發生率 DKA rate | 0.8% | 4.3% | 3.3% | 1.2% |

> In both EASE-2 and EASE-3, all empagliflozin doses led to significant reductions in hemoglobin A1c after 26 weeks. The beneficial effect on hemoglobin A1c persisted at week 52 in EASE-2. One of the most noteworthy findings was that there were no excess cases of diabetic ketoacidosis with the 2.5 mg dose (0.8%) compared with placebo (1.2%), whereas the 10 mg (4.3%) and 25 mg (3.3%) doses showed higher rates. Whether empagliflozin 5 mg is associated with a heightened ketoacidosis risk is not known.

在 EASE-2 與 EASE-3 中，所有 empagliflozin 劑量於 26 週後皆顯著降低 HbA1c；EASE-2 中此效益延續至第 52 週。最值得注意的發現之一是：2.5 mg 劑量的 DKA（0.8%）並未較安慰劑（1.2%）增加，而 10 mg（4.3%）與 25 mg（3.3%）劑量則發生率較高。Empagliflozin 5 mg 是否與較高的 ketoacidosis 風險相關，目前未知。

**降低 euglycemic DKA 風險：STICH protocol**

> SGLT-2 inhibitors increase urinary glucose excretion and reduce insulin requirements, which can promote lipolysis and ketone production even when blood glucose levels are normal or only mildly elevated. In people with T1D, this increases the risk of euglycemic diabetic ketoacidosis, which may be harder to recognize because hyperglycemia is less prominent. To minimize this risk in patients with T1D treated with SGLT-2 inhibitors and insulin, the STICH protocol recommends stopping the SGLT-2 inhibitor, injecting an insulin bolus, consuming 30 g of carbohydrate, and hydrating if ketosis is detected.

SGLT-2 inhibitors 增加尿糖排泄並減少胰島素需求，即使血糖正常或僅輕度升高，也可能促進 lipolysis 與酮體生成。在 T1D 患者，這增加 euglycemic diabetic ketoacidosis（正常血糖性 DKA）的風險，且因高血糖較不明顯而更難辨識。為將此風險降至最低，STICH protocol 建議：若偵測到 ketosis，應停用 SGLT-2 inhibitor、注射一劑 insulin bolus、攝取 30 g 碳水化合物並補充水分。

### GLP-1 Receptor Agonists

> GLP-1 and GIP are incretin hormones critical to nutrient metabolism. Secreted by intestinal enteroendocrine cells, they activate receptors across broad sites, including pancreatic tissues (to promote insulin secretion and suppress postprandial glucagon secretion), the CV system, and the central nervous system. Residual insulin secretion may be a target (and necessary) for GLP-1 receptor agonist action, although this remains unproven for the later-generation, longer-acting agents. Furthermore, glucagon suppression by GLP-1 receptor agonists may underlie potential therapeutic advantages over insulin monotherapy.

GLP-1 與 GIP 是對營養代謝至關重要的 incretin hormones，由腸道 enteroendocrine cells 分泌，可活化廣泛部位的受體，包括胰臟組織（促進 insulin 分泌並抑制餐後 glucagon 分泌）、CV 系統與中樞神經系統。殘餘的胰島素分泌可能是 GLP-1 receptor agonist 作用的標的（且為必要條件），但對於較新一代、較長效的藥物而言尚未證實。此外，GLP-1 receptor agonists 對 glucagon 的抑制，可能是相較於 insulin 單一治療潛在治療優勢的基礎。

> Recent studies have reported that people with T1D have a number of unmet needs, including simple and predictable diabetes management, reduced mental effort to manage diabetes, prevention of weight gain, achievement of a greater time-in-range, and prevention of hypoglycemia. Most participants surveyed would consider adding an adjunctive agent to insulin to address these unmet needs. The nutrient-stimulated hormone (NuSH) therapies, such as GLP-1 receptor agonists, help address these unmet needs in T1D.

近期研究指出 T1D 患者存在多項未被滿足的需求，包括：簡單且可預測的糖尿病管理、降低管理糖尿病所需的心力、預防體重增加、達到更高的 time-in-range，以及預防低血糖。多數受訪者願意在 insulin 之外加上輔助藥物以因應這些需求。Nutrient-stimulated hormone（NuSH，營養刺激荷爾蒙）療法，如 GLP-1 receptor agonists，有助於回應 T1D 的這些未滿足需求。

**Semaglutide 在 T1D 的證據**

> In a retrospective study of a small number of people with newly diagnosed T1D, early initiation of semaglutide enabled most participants to discontinue insulin by 12 months. Semaglutide increased C-peptide levels and improved glycemic control. This hypothesis was tested in the ADJUST-T1D trial, which recruited 72 adults with obesity and T1D who used an automated insulin delivery system. Participants were randomly assigned to receive once-weekly semaglutide (1 mg) or placebo for 26 weeks. The primary outcome was a composite endpoint of greater than 70% time-in-range, less than 4% time-in-hypoglycemia, and at least 5% weight reduction. This endpoint was achieved by 36% of patients in the semaglutide group and by none in the placebo group. Between-group differences in hemoglobin A1c, weight, and total daily insulin dose were –0.3%, –8.8 kg, and –22.3 units, respectively. There were no safety concerns related to hypoglycemia or ketoacidosis.

在一項納入少數新診斷 T1D 患者的回溯性研究中，早期啟用 semaglutide 使多數受試者於 12 個月內得以停用 insulin，semaglutide 提高了 C-peptide 濃度並改善血糖控制。此假說在 ADJUST-T1D trial 中受檢驗，該試驗納入 72 位肥胖且使用 automated insulin delivery 系統的 T1D 成人，隨機分配接受每週一次 semaglutide（1 mg）或安慰劑，為期 26 週。主要結果為一複合終點：time-in-range 大於 70%、time-in-hypoglycemia 小於 4% 且體重減少至少 5%。Semaglutide 組有 36% 達標，安慰劑組則無一人達標。兩組間的 HbA1c、體重與每日總胰島素劑量差異分別為 –0.3%、–8.8 kg、–22.3 units。未見與低血糖或 ketoacidosis 相關的安全性疑慮。

### GLP-1/GIP Receptor Agonists（Tirzepatide）

> Although GLP-1 and GIP share metabolic roles, they differ in their expression of adipose tissue receptors and in their effects on glucagon secretion. Specifically: (1) GIP receptors, but not GLP-1 receptors, are expressed in adipose tissue; and (2) whereas GLP-1 suppresses glucagon, GIP may promote its release in a glucose-dependent manner. When combined, GIP receptor activation synergistically boosts the therapeutic efficacy of GLP-1. Accordingly, dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonists, such as tirzepatide, have been developed to treat T2D and obesity.

儘管 GLP-1 與 GIP 有共同的代謝角色，兩者在脂肪組織受體的表現與對 glucagon 分泌的影響上有所不同。具體而言：(1) GIP receptors 表現於脂肪組織，而 GLP-1 receptors 則否；(2) GLP-1 抑制 glucagon，GIP 則可能以葡萄糖依賴的方式促進其釋放。兩者結合時，GIP 受體活化可協同增強 GLP-1 的療效。因此，dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonists（如 tirzepatide）被開發用於治療 T2D 與肥胖。

> Tirzepatide is a first-in-class peptide that coagonizes GIP and GLP-1 receptors and is administered as a once-weekly subcutaneous injection. In the SURPASS program among people with T2D, tirzepatide demonstrated superiority over placebo in reducing hemoglobin A1c and body weight at doses of 5 mg, 10 mg, and 15 mg over 40 weeks, without increasing hypoglycemia. Separate obesity-focused trials (eg, SURMOUNT) demonstrated tirzepatide's capacity to induce dose-dependent weight loss in people with obesity but without diabetes.

Tirzepatide 是一種同時活化 GIP 與 GLP-1 受體的 first-in-class 胜肽，每週一次皮下注射。在 T2D 的 SURPASS 計畫中，tirzepatide 於 5 mg、10 mg、15 mg 劑量、為期 40 週皆優於安慰劑地降低 HbA1c 與體重，且未增加低血糖。另有專注肥胖的試驗（如 SURMOUNT）顯示 tirzepatide 能在肥胖但無糖尿病者身上誘導劑量依賴性的體重減輕。

> Tirzepatide has demonstrated clinically meaningful CV benefits beyond its glucose-lowering effect. Recent CV outcome data establish that tirzepatide is at least noninferior to established GLP-1 receptor agonists (dulaglutide) for major adverse CV events and may offer additional reductions in all-cause mortality. Emerging mechanistic evidence suggests that tirzepatide may exert direct cardioprotective effects by attenuating inflammatory, profibrotic, hypertrophic, and apoptotic pathways in the diabetic myocardium, possibly mediated by improved insulin sensitivity.

Tirzepatide 已展現超越其降血糖作用、具臨床意義的 CV 效益。近期 CV outcome 資料顯示，就 major adverse CV events 而言，tirzepatide 至少不劣於既有的 GLP-1 receptor agonists（dulaglutide），並可能額外降低 all-cause mortality。新興的機轉證據顯示，tirzepatide 可能藉由減弱糖尿病心肌中的發炎、促纖維化、肥厚與凋亡途徑（可能經由改善 insulin sensitivity 中介）而發揮直接的心臟保護作用。

**TIRTLE1 試驗**

> TIRTLE1 is the first placebo-controlled, randomized study assessing tirzepatide (titrated to 5.0 mg over 12 weeks) in 24 individuals with T1D (mean age, 40.5 ± 11 years; BMI, 34.2 ± 3.4 kg/m²; hemoglobin A1c, 7.3 ± 1.3%; total daily insulin dose, 74.1 ± 26.8 units; 58% male; 50% using continuous subcutaneous insulin infusion). The primary endpoint was change in body weight. At 12 weeks, tirzepatide had reduced body weight (mean difference –8.7 kg vs placebo, P < .0001), hemoglobin A1c (mean difference –0.4% vs placebo, P = .05), and insulin dose (mean difference –35% vs placebo, P = .0002). The reduction in insulin dose was evident as early as 2 weeks after randomization among individuals using insulin pumps. Weight loss was predominantly fat mass (estimated treatment difference −7.2 kg [95% CI, −10.5 to −3.9 kg]; P = .0002); the between-group difference in lean mass was not significant. There were no episodes of diabetic ketoacidosis or severe hypoglycemia, and treatment satisfaction was favorable. A phase 3, multicenter, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel-group study evaluating the efficacy and safety of tirzepatide in adults with overweight/obesity and T1D (SURPASS-T1D-1) is underway.

TIRTLE1 是首個以安慰劑對照、隨機方式評估 tirzepatide（12 週內逐步調升至 5.0 mg）於 24 位 T1D 患者的研究（平均年齡 40.5 ± 11 歲；BMI 34.2 ± 3.4 kg/m²；HbA1c 7.3 ± 1.3%；每日總胰島素劑量 74.1 ± 26.8 units；58% 為男性；50% 使用 continuous subcutaneous insulin infusion）。主要終點為體重變化。12 週時，tirzepatide 降低體重（與安慰劑相比平均差 –8.7 kg，P < .0001）、HbA1c（平均差 –0.4%，P = .05）與胰島素劑量（平均差 –35%，P = .0002）。在使用 insulin pump 者，胰島素劑量的下降早在隨機分派後 2 週即可見。體重減輕以脂肪量為主（估計治療差 −7.2 kg [95% CI, −10.5 至 −3.9 kg]；P = .0002），瘦體組織（lean mass）的組間差異則不顯著。無 DKA 或嚴重低血糖事件，治療滿意度良好。目前一項評估 tirzepatide 用於過重／肥胖 T1D 成人的 phase 3、多中心、隨機、雙盲、安慰劑對照、平行分組研究（SURPASS-T1D-1）正在進行中。

## 🧠 個案解析與臨床推理 Case Analysis & Clinical Reasoning

### Case 1 解析：口服輔助藥物與 DKA 預防

**Q1 正解為 A**：Metformin 已一致顯示能改善 T1D 青少年（而非成人）的 insulin sensitivity。

推理要點：
- 與 T2D 及無糖尿病者不同，**目前沒有已發表資料顯示 SGLT-2 inhibitors 能降低 T1D 的 CV 事件**（故選項 B 錯誤）。
- SGLT-2 inhibitors 雖曾於 2019 年獲 EMA 核准用於過重／肥胖 T1D，但該核准已於 2021 年因 ketoacidosis 風險撤回（故選項 C 錯誤）。
- 在 T1D 中，能有效降低 HbA1c、體重與每日總胰島素劑量的**最低 empagliflozin 劑量為 2.5 mg/day**，且其 ketoacidosis 風險與安慰劑相當（故選項 D「5 mg」、選項 E「不論劑量皆增加 DKA」皆錯誤）。

**Q2 正解為 B**：若考慮使用 SGLT-2 inhibitor，降低 DKA 風險最重要的策略是**常規居家血酮監測搭配明確的病日守則**。

常見陷阱 Pitfalls：
- 減少基礎胰島素（A）會增加 insulin deficiency 與 ketoacidosis 風險。
- 選用 empagliflozin 2.5 mg（C）雖可能降低 DKA 風險，但並非「最重要」的預防策略。
- 限制碳水化合物（D）可能加重 ketosis。
- 降低血糖目標（E）會增加胰島素給予的變異，卻未觸及 DKA 風險的根本機轉。

### Case 2 解析：GLP-1 與 GLP-1/GIP receptor agonists

**Q1 正解為 C**：Semaglutide 尚未在 T1D 族群的 CV outcomes trial 中證實可降低 CVD。

推理要點：
- Semaglutide 僅核准用於 T2D 與肥胖；T2D 研究雖顯示 CV 益處，但尚無針對 T1D 的長期 CV outcomes trial 加以證實。現有 T1D 資料來自較小型隨機研究或 off-label 使用的回溯性分析，不足以確立降低 CVD 的適應症。
- 在 T1D 應測量**刺激後而非空腹**的 C-peptide；且與 liraglutide 不同，並無證據顯示 C-peptide 的存在可預測對 semaglutide 的反應（故選項 A 錯誤）。
- T1D 的餐後高 glucagon 血症（postprandial hyperglucagonemia）確實促成高血糖，而 semaglutide 可抑制之（故選項 B 錯誤）。
- 雖有證據顯示在 T2D 中若血糖（不論以 insulin 或 semaglutide）快速改善，可能加速活動性 retinopathy 進展（尤其孕期），但此觀察尚未在 T1D 測試；且本例 retinopathy 已無活動性，故不構成禁忌（選項 D 錯誤）。
- Gastroparesis 是使用 NuSH 療法的相對禁忌，但即使胃排空緩慢，這些療法仍可能有效，故不必於啟用前常規安排 gastric emptying study（選項 E 錯誤）。

**Q2 正解為 E**：因 GIP 可抑制 GLP-1 誘發的噁心，tirzepatide 可能比 semaglutide 更易耐受。

推理與釐清：
- Tirzepatide 具**隨機安慰劑對照試驗（TIRTLE1）證據**，顯示 T1D 患者的體重、HbA1c 與每日總胰島素劑量顯著下降（故選項 B「無 RCT 證據」錯誤）。
- 因脂肪組織具 GIP receptors，tirzepatide 在 T1D 可能是比 semaglutide 更強效的 NuSH，特別是在較高 BMI 者可望進一步減重與改善血糖（故選項 A 錯誤）。
- 使用 hybrid closed-loop 系統者並無限制使用 tirzepatide 的特定建議；事實上 closed-loop 系統可依血糖變化調整胰島素給予，反而有助於降低低血糖風險，惟仍需謹慎監測與調整劑量（故選項 C 錯誤）。
- Tirzepatide 減緩胃排空的程度與 semaglutide 相近，並無確切證據顯示其胃排空較慢因而更易耐受（故選項 D 錯誤）；tirzepatide 較佳耐受性的假說來自 GIP 成分對噁心的抑制，而非胃排空差異。

### 整合臨床決策 Integrated decision-making

- 在為 T1D 患者選擇輔助療法時，須同時衡量代謝效益（減重、降低胰島素劑量、改善 time-in-range）與 T1D 特有風險（尤其 SGLT-2 inhibitor 的 euglycemic DKA）。
- 所有輔助藥物在 T1D 皆屬 off-label（法國的 metformin 除外），決策應以充分衛教、知情同意與嚴密監測為前提。
- 對於高 BMI、高胰島素劑量、insulin resistance 表現明顯的 T1D 患者，GLP-1 或 GLP-1/GIP receptor agonists 的代謝效益最為顯著。

## ⭐ 重點整理 Key Takeaways

- T1D 的 CV 風險顯著升高，除傳統血管風險因子外，還由 **insulin resistance 與 inflammation** 驅動。
- 用於 T2D 的心臟保護藥物（**SGLT-2 inhibitors 與 GLP-1 receptor agonists**）新興證據顯示對 T1D 具代謝益處，但目前**均未核准用於 T1D**。
- **Metformin** 在 T1D 成人的主要效益是**減少胰島素劑量**；已核准用於 T2D，但未核准用於 T1D（法國除外）。
- **Empagliflozin** 於 2.5 mg、10 mg、25 mg 皆能降低 HbA1c、體重與每日總胰島素需求，但**僅 2.5 mg 劑量不伴隨 DKA 風險上升**。
- 選擇 off-label 使用 **SGLT-2 inhibitor** 的患者，必須獲得明確指示以降低 **euglycemic ketoacidosis** 風險（血酮監測、病日守則、STICH protocol）。
- 近期隨機安慰劑對照試驗（**ADJUST-T1D、TIRTLE1**）顯示 **semaglutide 與 tirzepatide** 皆能降低體重、HbA1c 與每日總胰島素劑量，且不增加低血糖或 DKA 風險。
- T1D 患者存在矛盾性的**餐後高 glucagon 血症**與殘餘的 **C-peptide 分泌**（insulin microsecretors），這些是 GLP-1 receptor agonist 療效的重要機轉背景。
- **Tirzepatide（GLP-1/GIP）**因脂肪組織具 GIP receptors 且 GIP 可抑制噁心，理論上可能比純 GLP-1 receptor agonist 更強效且更易耐受。
