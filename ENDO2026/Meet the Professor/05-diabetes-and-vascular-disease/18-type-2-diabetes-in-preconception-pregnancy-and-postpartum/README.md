## 🩺 臨床情境 Clinical Scenario

本章以三個臨床案例貫穿孕前、孕期與產後三個階段。以下先呈現原始案例，詳細解析見〈個案解析與臨床推理〉。

### Case 1

> A 28-year-old woman is referred for urgent endocrine evaluation after an unexpected pregnancy is diagnosed. She is gravida 1, para 0, and ultrasonography estimates the gestational age to be 11 weeks and 2 days. She has obesity and a 2-year history of type 2 diabetes. Her current BMI is 38.4 kg/m². She has no diabetes-related complications or other comorbidities. Her blood pressure is 118/72 mm Hg. Her current medications include semaglutide, 2.4 mg once weekly (last dose 6 days ago), metformin, 1000 mg twice daily, and insulin glargine, 34 units at night. Her hemoglobin A1c is 5.7% (39 mmol/mol). She uses a CGM, and fasting glucose values range from 80 to 95 mg/dL (4.4-5.3 mmol/L), with 2-hour postmeal values of 115 to 130 mg/dL (6.4-7.8 mmol/L). Although the pregnancy was unplanned, she is keen to proceed.

28 歲女性，因意外懷孕被緊急轉介至內分泌科評估。G1P0，超音波推估妊娠週數為 11 週又 2 天。她有 obesity 與 2 年的 type 2 diabetes 病史，目前 BMI 38.4 kg/m²，無糖尿病相關併發症或其他共病，血壓 118/72 mm Hg。目前用藥為 semaglutide 2.4 mg 每週一次（最後一劑為 6 天前）、metformin 1000 mg 每日兩次、insulin glargine 睡前 34 units。HbA1c 為 5.7%（39 mmol/mol）。她使用 CGM，空腹血糖介於 80-95 mg/dL（4.4-5.3 mmol/L），餐後 2 小時血糖 115-130 mg/dL（6.4-7.8 mmol/L）。雖為非計畫性懷孕，但她意願繼續妊娠。

### Case 2

> A 41-year-old pregnant woman originally from South Asia (gravida 4, para 1) presents for follow-up. She is 30 weeks' gestation and has lived with type 2 diabetes for 18 years. She previously developed gastrointestinal adverse effects with metformin and GLP-1 RA–based therapies and transitioned to hybrid closed-loop insulin pump therapy 5 years ago. She has background diabetic retinopathy. Her BMI is 28 kg/m², and her gestational weight gain has been at the lower end of the recommended range. On her current regimen, she uses an average of 140 units of insulin aspart per day, 65% of which is bolus insulin. She continues to use her pump's automated mode. Her hemoglobin A1c was 6.1% (43 mmol/mol) 1 week ago, and her time-in-pregnancy target range is 78% with 4% in time-below-range. She reports being very frustrated with her glycemic control, noting that even when she boluses 15 minutes before meals as recommended, her glucose spikes and then rapidly drops 3 to 4 hours later, often requiring treatment for hypoglycemia.

41 歲孕婦，來自南亞（G4P1），前來追蹤。妊娠 30 週，罹患 type 2 diabetes 已 18 年。她過去使用 metformin 與 GLP-1 RA 類藥物皆出現腸胃道副作用，5 年前改為 hybrid closed-loop insulin pump 治療。她有 background diabetic retinopathy。BMI 28 kg/m²，孕期體重增加位於建議範圍的下限。目前每日平均使用 insulin aspart 140 units，其中 65% 為 bolus insulin，並持續使用幫浦的自動模式（automated mode）。一週前 HbA1c 為 6.1%（43 mmol/mol），妊娠目標範圍內時間（time-in-range）為 78%、time-below-range 為 4%。她對血糖控制感到非常挫折，表示即使依建議在餐前 15 分鐘施打 bolus，餐後血糖仍飆高，接著在 3-4 小時後快速下降，常需要處理 hypoglycemia。

### Case 3

> A 32-year-old pregnant woman (gravida 3, para 2) presents at 36 weeks and 5 days' gestation for diabetes evaluation. Labor induction is planned for the following week. She was diagnosed with type 2 diabetes after her previous pregnancy and was prescribed metformin, 1000 mg twice daily, but did not take it consistently. Her hemoglobin A1c was 8.4% (68 mmol/mol) at her first prenatal visit (10 weeks' gestation) and is now 5.9% (41 mmol/mol) on insulin glargine, 52 units once daily, and insulin aspart, 34 units before meals. She plans to breastfeed and is eager to lose weight postpartum.

32 歲孕婦（G3P2），妊娠 36 週又 5 天前來評估糖尿病，預計下週引產。她在前次懷孕後被診斷為 type 2 diabetes，並開立 metformin 1000 mg 每日兩次，但未規律服用。首次產檢（妊娠 10 週）時 HbA1c 為 8.4%（68 mmol/mol），目前在 insulin glargine 52 units 每日一次加 insulin aspart 餐前 34 units 治療下降至 5.9%（41 mmol/mol）。她計畫哺乳，並希望產後減重。

## 🔬 背景與重要性 Background & Significance

> Historically, preexisting diabetes in pregnancy was predominantly due to type 1 diabetes, with type 2 diabetes constituting a smaller proportion. However, this landscape has changed dramatically over the past several decades. Data from the United States reveal that the overall rate of pregestational diabetes was 10.9 per 1000 births in 2021, a 27% increase from 2016. The observed increases in pregestational diabetes are driven predominantly by a sharp rise in the prevalence of type 2 diabetes, as exemplified by United Kingdom national data showing that it now accounts for two-thirds of all cases of pregestational diabetes.

過去，孕期的既存糖尿病（preexisting diabetes）主要源自 type 1 diabetes，type 2 diabetes 僅佔少數。然而過去數十年間此情況已劇烈改變。美國資料顯示，2021 年孕前糖尿病（pregestational diabetes）整體發生率為每 1000 名新生兒 10.9 例，較 2016 年增加 27%。此增長主要由 type 2 diabetes 盛行率的急遽上升所驅動；英國全國資料即顯示 type 2 diabetes 目前已佔所有孕前糖尿病病例的三分之二。

> Underpinning this increase is the growing prevalence of obesity and type 2 diabetes among young people, which is highest in indigenous populations. Furthermore, more people are becoming pregnant at advanced ages, and we may also be witnessing increased intergenerational transmission due to in utero exposure to hyperglycemia.

支撐此趨勢的是年輕族群中 obesity 與 type 2 diabetes 盛行率的攀升，且以原住民族群為最高。此外，愈來愈多人於高齡懷孕，也可能因子宮內 hyperglycemia 暴露而出現世代間傳遞（intergenerational transmission）增加的現象。

### 母嬰風險 Maternal & Fetal Risk

> A recent systematic review and meta-analysis found that compared with pregnancies unaffected by diabetes, those with maternal type 2 diabetes were more likely to have babies with congenital anomalies (OR 1.76 [95% CI, 1.11-2.79]), large-for-gestational-age (OR 2.79 [95% CI, 1.93-4.04]), perinatal mortality (OR 4.18 [95% CI, 2.91-6.01]), and stillbirth (OR 7.27 [95% CI, 3.01-17.53]). In fact, compared with pregnancies affected by type 1 diabetes, those with type 2 diabetes were more likely to have small-for-gestational-age babies and to experience higher neonatal and perinatal mortality.

近期一篇 systematic review 與 meta-analysis 發現，相較於未受糖尿病影響的懷孕，母體 type 2 diabetes 的懷孕更容易出現先天異常（congenital anomalies，OR 1.76 [95% CI, 1.11-2.79]）、large-for-gestational-age（OR 2.79 [95% CI, 1.93-4.04]）、perinatal mortality（OR 4.18 [95% CI, 2.91-6.01]）與 stillbirth（OR 7.27 [95% CI, 3.01-17.53]）。事實上，相較於 type 1 diabetes 的懷孕，type 2 diabetes 更容易出現 small-for-gestational-age 嬰兒，且新生兒與周產期死亡率更高（分別為 OR 2.29 [95% CI, 1.12-4.67]、OR 1.53 [95% CI, 1.20-1.94]、OR 1.31 [95% CI, 1.07-1.61]）。

> High-quality diabetes care before, during, and after pregnancy is therefore essential for improving clinical outcomes in this vulnerable population.

因此，於懷孕前、中、後提供高品質的糖尿病照護，對改善此弱勢族群的臨床結果至關重要。

### 臨床實務缺口 Practice Gaps

- 雖然孕前照護（preconception care）與更佳的孕程準備及較低的嚴重不良妊娠結果風險相關，多數 type 2 diabetes 患者仍未接受孕前諮詢或照護。
- 非胰島素類降血糖藥物於孕前、孕期與產後的使用仍具爭議，使臨床醫師對最佳做法感到不確定。
- 婦產科、基層醫療與各專科之間的溝通與照護銜接不佳，導致產後照護與長期風險降低的機會被錯過。

## 🧭 診斷與評估 Diagnosis & Evaluation

### 孕前評估 Preconception Assessment

> For people of reproductive age with type 2 diabetes, every clinical visit should incorporate screening for pregnancy intent. In addition, individuals with a history of gestational diabetes should undergo preconception screening for diabetes. If pregnancy is not desired, the use of effective contraception is strongly recommended.

對於育齡的 type 2 diabetes 患者，每次就診皆應納入懷孕意願（pregnancy intent）的篩檢。此外，有 gestational diabetes 病史者應接受孕前糖尿病篩檢。若無懷孕計畫，強烈建議使用有效避孕。首選 long-acting reversible contraception，但選擇應個別化，考量患者偏好、共病（含 obesity 與 hypertension）、可能影響避孕效力的併用治療（如 GLP-1 RAs）及懷孕時程。多數避孕方式的風險低於非計畫性懷孕的風險。

> Given the direct relationship between early-pregnancy maternal hyperglycemia and congenital malformations, it is recommended to achieve glucose levels as close to normal as safely possible, with a hemoglobin A1c of less than 6.5% (<48 mmol/mol).

鑑於孕早期母體 hyperglycemia 與 congenital malformations 之間的直接關係，建議在安全前提下使血糖盡量接近正常，目標 HbA1c 小於 6.5%（<48 mmol/mol）。

**孕前血糖目標 Preconception glucose goals：**

| 指標 Parameter | 目標 Goal |
| --- | --- |
| Preprandial glucose 餐前血糖 | 80-110 mg/dL（4.4-6.1 mmol/L） |
| 2-hour postprandial glucose 餐後 2 小時血糖 | <155 mg/dL（<8.6 mmol/L） |
| HbA1c | <6.5%（<48 mmol/mol） |

### 微血管併發症篩檢 Microvascular complication screening

> Screening for microvascular complications should include a physical examination, a dilated eye examination, measurement of serum creatinine, and a urine albumin-to-creatinine ratio in the year before pregnancy. If microvascular complications and additional comorbidities are present, they should be optimally treated before pregnancy.

微血管併發症篩檢應於懷孕前一年內完成，包含理學檢查、散瞳眼底檢查（dilated eye examination）、血清 creatinine 測量與尿液 albumin-to-creatinine ratio。若存在微血管併發症及其他共病，應於懷孕前給予最佳化治療。若合併多重共病，是否繼續懷孕的決定應審慎討論預期的母體與胎兒風險及成功機率。

### 孕期糖尿病診斷 Screening in pregnancy

> At Mayo Clinic, if diabetes has not already been diagnosed preconceptually, all pregnant people undergo screening for undiagnosed type 2 diabetes at their first prenatal visit. Type 2 diabetes is diagnosed when the early-pregnancy hemoglobin A1c is 6.5% or higher (≥48 mmol/mol).

在 Mayo Clinic，若孕前尚未診斷糖尿病，所有孕婦於首次產檢時皆接受未診斷 type 2 diabetes 的篩檢。當孕早期 HbA1c ≥6.5%（≥48 mmol/mol）時，即診斷為 type 2 diabetes。雖可考慮針對具危險因子者的選擇性篩檢，但本院發現採普及性篩檢（universal screening）於實務上更為有效。

### 孕期血糖目標與監測 Glycemic goals & monitoring in pregnancy

> The following glycemic goals are recommended once pregnancy is confirmed, with the lower limits only applicable if the individual is using insulin therapy.

一旦確認懷孕，建議下列血糖目標，其中下限僅適用於使用 insulin 治療者：

| 指標 Parameter | 目標 Goal |
| --- | --- |
| Fasting glucose 空腹血糖 | 70-95 mg/dL（3.9-5.3 mmol/L） |
| 1-hour postprandial 餐後 1 小時 | 110-140 mg/dL（6.1-7.8 mmol/L）或 |
| 2-hour postprandial 餐後 2 小時 | 100-120 mg/dL（5.6-6.7 mmol/L） |
| HbA1c（每月監測） | <6.0%（<42 mmol/mol） |
| CGM 妊娠目標範圍 target range | 63-140 mg/dL（3.5-7.8 mmol/L） |
| Time-in-range（type 2） | >90%（type 1 為 >70%） |
| Hypoglycemia 閾值 | 血糖 <70 mg/dL（<3.9 mmol/L）且／或 sensor glucose <63 mg/dL（<3.5 mmol/L） |

> Either capillary glucose testing or continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) may be used. To date, no clinical trials have demonstrated the efficacy of CGM over capillary glucose testing for pregnancy-specific outcomes in type 2 diabetes. When using CGM, the pregnancy-specific target range of 63 to 140 mg/dL differs from the default device targets and must be manually adjusted.

可使用指尖血糖檢測（capillary glucose testing）或 continuous glucose monitoring（CGM）達成上述血糖目標。至今尚無臨床試驗證實 CGM 在 type 2 diabetes 孕期的特定結果上優於指尖血糖檢測；然而 CGM 於非孕期觀察到的益處（如降低高風險者的 hypoglycemia）預期同樣適用於孕期。使用 CGM 時，妊娠特定目標範圍 63-140 mg/dL（3.5-7.8 mmol/L）不同於裝置預設值，必須手動調整。

> Compared with those with type 1 diabetes, individuals with type 2 diabetes are less likely to develop diabetic ketoacidosis. However, if diabetic ketoacidosis does occur, it is associated with increased fetal mortality. Therefore, clinicians should maintain a high index of suspicion, particularly in higher-risk situations such as use of exogenous glucocorticoids.

相較於 type 1 diabetes，type 2 diabetes 患者較不易發生 diabetic ketoacidosis（DKA）。然而一旦發生 DKA，與胎兒死亡率上升相關。因此臨床醫師應保持高度警覺，尤其在使用外源性 glucocorticoids 等高風險情境下。若患者有嚴重 hypoglycemia 或 hypoglycemia unawareness 病史，則放寬上述低血糖閾值。

### 產科影像與監測 Obstetric imaging & surveillance

> A detailed fetal anatomy scan should be performed at 18 to 20 weeks' gestation, with further evaluation as needed. After 32 weeks, close monitoring, including ultrasound evaluation of fetal well-being, is advised. A dilated eye examination is advised in each trimester.

應於妊娠 18-20 週進行詳細的胎兒解剖構造超音波（fetal anatomy scan），必要時進一步評估。妊娠 32 週後建議密切監測，包含以超音波評估胎兒健康狀況（fetal well-being）。每一孕期（trimester）皆建議進行散瞳眼底檢查。

## 💊 治療與處置 Management

### 孕前用藥調整 Preconception medication adjustment

> Before pregnancy, it is necessary to discontinue potentially teratogenic medications, including GLP-1 RAs, SGLT-2 inhibitors, DPP-4 inhibitors, angiotensin receptor blockers, ACE inhibitors, and, in most cases, lipid-lowering therapies. Therefore, since sulfonylureas are no longer favored for use in pregnancy, periconceptual glucose-lowering pharmacotherapy centers on insulin and/or metformin.

懷孕前必須停用可能致畸胎的藥物，包括 GLP-1 RAs、SGLT-2 inhibitors、DPP-4 inhibitors、angiotensin receptor blockers（ARB）、ACE inhibitors，以及多數情況下的降血脂治療。由於 sulfonylureas 已不再是孕期偏好用藥，圍孕期（periconceptual）的降血糖藥物治療即以 insulin 及／或 metformin 為核心。

> Based on their half-life, it is accepted that semaglutide should be stopped 2 months before pregnancy and tirzepatide 1 month before pregnancy. For blood pressure control, pregnancy-safe options include labetalol, nifedipine, or methyldopa.

依據其半衰期，一般接受 semaglutide 應於懷孕前 2 個月停用、tirzepatide 於懷孕前 1 個月停用。停藥時機須個別化規劃，考量停止避孕後受孕的可能性、使用中的劑型，以及懷孕前長時間停藥的風險。血壓控制方面，孕期安全的選項包括 labetalol、nifedipine 或 methyldopa。

> Folic acid supplementation (400-800 mcg daily) should be initiated at least 4 weeks before conception and continued until 12 weeks' gestation.

Folic acid 補充（每日 400-800 mcg）應於受孕前至少 4 週開始，並持續至妊娠 12 週。

### 孕期胰島素治療 Insulin therapy in pregnancy

> Although not required preconceptually, most people with type 2 diabetes require insulin therapy during pregnancy, and insulin is the preferred agent for managing hyperglycemia. None of the commercially available insulins has been shown to cross the placenta. We typically use a multiple daily injection insulin regimen with rapid-acting insulin (eg, lispro, aspart, glulisine) and basal insulin (eg, glargine, degludec).

雖然孕前不一定需要，但多數 type 2 diabetes 患者在孕期需要 insulin 治療，且 insulin 是處理 hyperglycemia 的首選藥物。目前市售的 insulin 皆未被證實會通過胎盤。通常採用 multiple daily injection（MDI）方案，合併 rapid-acting insulin（如 lispro、aspart、glulisine）與 basal insulin（如 glargine、degludec）。

> Whether using injections or an insulin pump, the key to achieving and maintaining glycemic control during pregnancy is frequent insulin dose adjustments and understanding that bolus insulin requirements contribute a larger proportion of the total daily dose as pregnancy progresses.

無論使用注射或 insulin pump，孕期達成並維持血糖控制的關鍵在於頻繁調整 insulin 劑量，並理解隨著懷孕進展，bolus insulin 需求佔每日總劑量（total daily dose）的比例會逐漸增加。若 type 2 diabetes 患者在使用 insulin pump 時懷孕，本院做法是（仿照 type 1 diabetes 建議）以 off-label 方式使用 hybrid closed-loop 功能，並搭配變通做法以最佳化血糖控制；此做法需使用者能取得對 hybrid closed-loop 演算法及幫浦設定調整影響有深入了解的專家建議。本院每 1-2 週（必要時更頻繁）透過電子病歷入口與雲端管理平台提供個別化的 insulin 劑量調整建議，並輔以每 2-4 週一次的正式（實體或遠距）門診。

### Metformin 於孕期的角色 Role of metformin

> Metformin crosses the placenta, and although it may reduce insulin requirements, weight gain, and cesarean delivery rates, it has been associated with an increased risk of small-for-gestational-age babies, particularly in the context of maternal hypertension or nephropathy. Our practice is to use metformin cautiously in lower-risk individuals and to avoid it in those with baseline chronic hypertension or nephropathy.

Metformin 會通過胎盤，雖然可能降低 insulin 需求、體重增加與剖腹產率，但與 small-for-gestational-age 嬰兒風險上升相關，尤其在合併母體 hypertension 或 nephropathy 的情況下。本院做法是在較低風險者謹慎使用 metformin，並在具基礎慢性 hypertension 或 nephropathy 者避免使用。對於可能存在明顯胰島素分泌缺陷者（如較低 BMI、糖尿病病程長，或南亞、東亞族裔者），也避免使用 metformin。

### Preeclampsia 預防與血壓控制 Preeclampsia prophylaxis & BP control

> Pregnant individuals with type 2 diabetes should be prescribed aspirin (100-150 mg daily) starting at 12 to 16 weeks' gestation to lower the risk of preeclampsia. A blood pressure threshold of less than 140/90 mm Hg is recommended for initiating and titrating therapy.

Type 2 diabetes 孕婦應自妊娠 12-16 週起開立 aspirin（每日 100-150 mg）以降低 preeclampsia 風險。建議以血壓 <140/90 mm Hg 作為啟動與調整治療的閾值。

### 分娩時機與產程血糖 Delivery timing & intrapartum glucose

> Recent evidence and recommendations indicate that risk may outweigh the benefit of expectant management beyond 38 weeks' gestation in all cases of pregestational diabetes. Earlier induction should be considered in high-risk circumstances, such as poor glycemic control, previous stillbirth, or diabetes-related obstetric complications. During active labor and delivery, hourly glucose monitoring is recommended.

近期證據與建議指出，對所有孕前糖尿病病例而言，妊娠超過 38 週後繼續期待療法（expectant management）的風險可能超過其效益。在高風險情況下（如血糖控制不佳、先前 stillbirth 或糖尿病相關產科併發症）應考慮提早引產。分娩過程中建議每小時監測血糖。本院做法是將血糖目標控制在 70-110 mg/dL（3.9-6.1 mmol/L），若高於目標則開始靜脈注射 insulin。

### 產後照護 Postpartum care

> Insulin sensitivity improves dramatically after delivery of the placenta, increasing the risk of hypoglycemia among those using exogenous insulin. Glycemic goals return to prepregnancy recommendations.

胎盤娩出後 insulin sensitivity 大幅改善，使用外源性 insulin 者發生 hypoglycemia 的風險升高。血糖目標回歸孕前建議。建議事先在電子病歷中記錄暫定的產後糖尿病計畫，並與患者討論。

> Important components of postpartum care include contraceptive and pregnancy planning, obesity care, lactation support, management of postpartum hypertension, screening for depression, and management of thyroid disorders. Glycemic control tends to deteriorate postpartum, and lapses in diabetes care are common—20% at 12 months in a population-based study.

產後照護的重要組成包括避孕與懷孕規劃、obesity 照護、哺乳支持、產後 hypertension 處置、憂鬱症篩檢與 thyroid disorders 處置。既存糖尿病者產後併發症（含 hypertension 與 sepsis）風險增加，且較不易哺乳。產後血糖控制往往惡化，且糖尿病照護中斷常見——一項以人口為基礎的研究顯示 12 個月時中斷率達 20%。由於產後照護往往即成為下一次的孕前照護，此為支持 type 2 diabetes 患者改善健康的關鍵時機。

## 🧠 個案解析與臨床推理 Case Analysis & Clinical Reasoning

### Case 1 — 意外懷孕、正使用 GLP-1 RA 的患者

**問題：除了停用 semaglutide，還應建議下列何者？**
選項：A) 停用 metformin　B) 加上 glimepiride　C) 開始 prandial insulin　D) 建議終止妊娠　E) 檢測 fructosamine

**正解：C) Start prandial insulin（開始餐前胰島素）**

臨床推理：此患者餐後血糖已高於孕期目標範圍，且隨著 semaglutide 藥效消退，餐後血糖控制很可能顯著惡化。因此應開立 prandial insulin。由於血糖僅輕度升高，可自每餐前約 12 units 起始，並衛教患者未來數天至數週需向上調整劑量。

**逐一鑑別 pitfalls：**
- **停用 metformin（A）**：可能加劇 GLP-1 RA 停藥後的反彈性體重增加（rebound weight gain）；此患者無共病，此時合理繼續使用。近期一項大型 retrospective cohort 證實先前使用 GLP-1 RA 並因懷孕停藥與較大的孕期體重增加相關。她將受益於 medical nutrition therapy 與結構化運動計畫以預防體重反彈。
- **加上 glimepiride（B）**：sulfonylureas 已不再是孕期偏好用藥，因其會通過胎盤、與 neonatal hypoglycemia 風險增加相關，且缺乏長期子代安全資料。
- **建議終止妊娠（D）**：雖然動物研究與部分人類病例報告對 GLP-1 RA 暴露子代的 congenital malformations 風險有疑慮，但近期大型觀察性研究並未證實此關聯。因此雖宜停藥，但並非常規建議終止妊娠。
- **檢測 fructosamine（E）**：雖然孕期紅血球更新加速使 HbA1c 較不可靠，但其普及且升高與不良妊娠結果相關。fructosamine 相較 HbA1c 並未提升對妊娠結果的預測，且在此情境下加開 prandial insulin 前並不需要額外的血糖指標。

### Case 2 — 幫浦治療下的餐後高血糖繼發低血糖

**問題：應建議下列何者？**
選項：A) 換成 U500 insulin　B) 餐後吃蛋白質點心　C) 幫浦改為手動模式　D) 餐前 30 分鐘施打 bolus　E) 每餐碳水化合物限制在 30 g

**正解：D) Bolus 30 minutes before eating（餐前 30 分鐘施打）**

臨床推理：孕期需要 insulin 的糖尿病常見的挑戰是餐後 1-2 小時 hyperglycemia，接著在餐後 3-4 小時 hypoglycemia。此型態源自餐後 insulin 濃度達峰時間延遲，加上隨懷孕進展周邊 insulin resistance 增加。因此建議孕早期於餐前 15 分鐘、孕晚期於餐前 30-40 分鐘施打 prandial insulin。額外策略為減少糖分攝取並在每餐納入更多複合性碳水化合物。

**逐一鑑別 pitfalls：**
- **U500 insulin（A）**：其作用型態與 U100 截然不同（近似 intermediate-acting insulin），孕期極少使用。相對地，rapid-acting U200 insulin 的藥效動力學與 U100 為 bioequivalent，可減少注射總體積與更換輸注管組的頻率，適用於高 insulin 需求者；使用濃縮 insulin 時須在病歷與幫浦上清楚標示，並在調整幫浦設定時納入考量（幫浦僅為 U100 設計）。
- **餐後蛋白質點心（B）**：雖可降低 hypoglycemia 風險，但此為被動反應式做法，不如處理根本問題——insulin 與進食時機的錯配。
- **幫浦改為手動模式（C）**：會使患者失去自動 basal insulin 暫停的益處，反而可能加劇問題。
- **碳水化合物限制在 30 g（E）**：過度限制碳水化合物可能有害，並不建議。

### Case 3 — 產後即刻的胰島素劑量設定

**問題：產後即刻應建議下列何者？**
選項：A) Metformin 500 mg 晚餐＋glargine 30 units 睡前　B) Glargine 20 units 睡前＋aspart 10 units 餐前　C) Metformin 500 mg 晚餐＋tirzepatide 2.5 mg 每週　D) 餐前及睡前 correction-scale insulin　E) Glargine 37 units 睡前＋aspart 24 units 餐前

**正解：B) Insulin glargine 20 units 睡前＋insulin aspart 10 units 餐前**

臨床推理：本院典型做法是將孕前及孕早期的 insulin 劑量（basal 與 bolus）減少 30% 作為產後起始點。但當孕前 insulin 需求未知或孕早期血糖控制不佳時，合理的做法是給予產前劑量的三分之一至二分之一（選項 B）。由於產後建議劑量僅為實際需求的粗略估計，最重要的是確保密切血糖監測，並讓患者了解 hypoglycemia 與 hyperglycemia 的雙向風險。

**逐一鑑別 pitfalls：**
- **Metformin＋basal insulin（A）**：未來可能是此患者的選項，但 metformin 需數天才開始影響血糖，本院通常於產後 3-4 天再重新開始以最佳化耐受性。
- **含 tirzepatide 的方案（C）**：GLP-1 RA 類療法因缺乏安全資料，且其食慾抑制與體重減輕理論上可能影響泌乳量，不適合哺乳期使用。
- **Correction-scale insulin（D）**：為被動反應式做法，對此孕期曾需每日超過 150 units insulin 的患者無法達成理想血糖控制。
- **選項 E**：其劑量僅為產前劑量減少 30%，於產後 insulin sensitivity 劇增的情況下可能導致 hypoglycemia。

## ⭐ 重點整理 Key Takeaways

- Type 2 diabetes 孕婦的不良妊娠結果（congenital anomalies、LGA、stillbirth、perinatal mortality）與 type 1 diabetes 相當，甚至在 SGA 與新生兒／周產期死亡率上更高，不可低估其風險。
- 每次就診皆應篩檢懷孕意願（pregnancy intent）；孕前照護與孕前血糖最佳化（HbA1c <6.5%、<48 mmol/mol）能顯著改善妊娠結果。
- 孕前須停用致畸胎藥物：GLP-1 RAs、SGLT-2 inhibitors、DPP-4 inhibitors、ARB、ACE inhibitors；semaglutide 需提前 2 個月、tirzepatide 提前 1 個月停用；圍孕期降血糖以 insulin 及／或 metformin 為核心。
- Insulin 是孕期 type 2 diabetes 的首選藥物；市售 insulin 皆不通過胎盤。孕期成功關鍵在於頻繁調整劑量，且 bolus 佔 total daily dose 比例隨孕程增加。
- Metformin 會通過胎盤並與 SGA 相關，於合併 hypertension、nephropathy 或疑似胰島素分泌缺陷者應避免使用。
- 餐後高血糖繼發低血糖的處理：孕早期餐前 15 分鐘、孕晚期餐前 30-40 分鐘施打 prandial insulin，而非被動補充蛋白質或改為幫浦手動模式。
- 自妊娠 12-16 週起給予 aspirin 100-150 mg／日以降低 preeclampsia；血壓治療閾值為 <140/90 mm Hg。
- 產後 insulin sensitivity 劇增，應將產前劑量減至三分之一至二分之一並密切監測；GLP-1 RA 不建議用於哺乳期；預先於病歷記錄產後計畫以確保安全銜接。
