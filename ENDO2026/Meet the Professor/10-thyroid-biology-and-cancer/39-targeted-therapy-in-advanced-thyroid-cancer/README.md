## 📋 教學目標 Educational Objectives

- Recognize the role of driver molecular alterations in advanced differentiated thyroid cancers (DTCs) and the need to identify them to make optimal treatment recommendations for patients who require systemic therapy.
  - 認識 driver molecular alterations（驅動性分子變異）在晚期 differentiated thyroid cancers (DTCs) 中所扮演的角色，並理解對於需要 systemic therapy（全身性治療）的病人，辨識這些變異對於制定最佳治療建議的必要性。

- Explain how the genotype-phenotype correlation in DTC, which links molecular alterations to histologic subtypes, provides insights into the varied natural histories of disease and offers opportunities to optimize systemic therapy for patients with advanced disease.
  - 說明 DTC 中的 genotype-phenotype correlation（基因型與表現型的相關性）如何將分子變異連結至組織學亞型，藉此闡明疾病多變的自然病程，並為晚期病人的 systemic therapy 提供最佳化的契機。

## 🩺 臨床情境 Clinical Scenario

本章以兩個真實個案貫穿，說明晚期 RAI-refractory DTC（放射碘難治型分化型甲狀腺癌）的分子驅動變異如何主導治療決策。

### Case 1 — BRAF V600E–positive、tall cell variant PTC

In most patients with advanced, RAI-refractory DTC, the disease is limited to the locoregional area at the time of initial diagnosis. Patient 1 was a 60-year-old man who presented with a left neck mass in 2014. Ultrasonography revealed a 1.7-cm suspicious left thyroid nodule and multiple enlarged left lateral neck nodes measuring up to 2.1 cm. Neck CT revealed a 1.7-cm left thyroid nodule and multiple suspicious lymph nodes in left levels II through IV. Fine-needle aspiration (FNA) showed papillary thyroid carcinoma (PTC). He underwent total thyroidectomy with bilateral central and left lateral neck dissection. Pathology showed PTC, tall cell variant, with minimal extrathyroidal extension, lymphovascular invasion, and perineural invasion. In the central neck, 4 of 15 nodes were involved, and 7 of 31 lateral nodes were involved, the largest of which was 4.6 cm, with extranodal extension. IHC for the BRAF V600E variant was positive.

> 大多數晚期 RAI-refractory DTC 病人在初次診斷時，病灶仍侷限於局部區域。病人 1 為 60 歲男性，於 2014 年因左頸腫塊就診。超音波顯示左側甲狀腺有一個 1.7 cm 的可疑結節，以及多顆腫大的左側頸側區淋巴結，最大達 2.1 cm。頸部 CT 顯示 1.7 cm 左側甲狀腺結節，以及左側 level II 至 IV 多顆可疑淋巴結。FNA 診斷為 papillary thyroid carcinoma (PTC)。病人接受 total thyroidectomy 併雙側 central 及左側 lateral neck dissection。病理為 PTC, tall cell variant，伴有輕微 extrathyroidal extension、lymphovascular invasion 與 perineural invasion。Central neck 15 顆淋巴結中 4 顆受侵犯，lateral neck 31 顆中 7 顆受侵犯，最大者 4.6 cm 並有 extranodal extension。BRAF V600E 的 IHC 呈陽性。

The patient then enrolled in the ASTRA trial (selumetinib vs placebo as pretreatment for adjuvant RAI in high-risk DTC).¹ He received 100 mCi RAI under thyrotropin alfa stimulation. Whole-body imaging 5 days later showed uptake in the bilateral thyroid bed and in the left lateral neck. TSH was kept suppressed, and serum thyroglobulin (Tg) remained less than 1 ng/mL (<1 μg/L).

> 病人隨後參加 ASTRA 試驗（於高風險 DTC 中比較 selumetinib 對比 placebo 作為 adjuvant RAI 前置治療）。¹ 他在 thyrotropin alfa 刺激下接受 100 mCi RAI。5 天後全身影像顯示雙側 thyroid bed 及左側頸側區有攝取。TSH 維持抑制，血清 thyroglobulin (Tg) 持續 <1 ng/mL（<1 μg/L）。

The patient developed recurrent disease in the left neck 2 years after thyroidectomy and underwent revision neck surgery. Nine months later, he had FNA-confirmed recurrent disease in the left neck; diagnostic RAI whole-body imaging showed no uptake. A second left revision neck surgery was performed. Pathology showed multiple deposits of tall-cell–variant PTC infiltrating fibroadipose and connective tissues. Next-generation sequencing from the second revision specimen confirmed the BRAF V600E variant, plus TERT promoter C228T and CIC E1366K comutations.

> 甲狀腺切除後 2 年，病人於左頸復發並再次接受頸部手術。9 個月後，FNA 再度確認左頸復發；診斷性 RAI 全身影像無攝取（顯示 RAI-refractory）。遂進行第二次左頸再手術。病理顯示多處 tall-cell–variant PTC 浸潤纖維脂肪與結締組織。第二次手術檢體的 next-generation sequencing 確認 BRAF V600E，並合併 TERT promoter C228T 與 CIC E1366K 共突變。

By May 2019, the patient developed locoregionally recurrent and distant metastatic disease: left cervical adenopathy up to 3.4 cm, scattered small lung nodules up to 1.4 cm, and a 3.0-cm gastrohepatic nodal conglomerate. The suppressed Tg concentration was 4.6 ng/mL (4.6 μg/L). He started systemic therapy with lenvatinib. He developed treatment-related hypertension requiring antihypertensive therapy and eventually dose reduction. After the first 2 cycles, he had a partial response (PR) per RECIST v1.1, with a 61% decrease in tumor measurements; Tg decreased to 2.7 ng/mL. A second dose reduction was required for treatment-related diarrhea. He remained responsive to lenvatinib for 48 months until oligoprogressive disease developed involving a thoracic vertebral body metastasis. Lenvatinib was held during stereotactic body radiotherapy and then resumed. Seven months later, in December 2023, lenvatinib was discontinued due to further progression with a mass in the left styloid parapharyngeal space.

> 至 2019 年 5 月，病人出現局部復發併遠端轉移：左頸淋巴結最大 3.4 cm、散在肺結節最大 1.4 cm，以及 3.0 cm 的 gastrohepatic 淋巴結團塊。抑制狀態下 Tg 為 4.6 ng/mL。他開始接受 lenvatinib 全身性治療，出現治療相關高血壓，需使用降壓藥並最終減量。前 2 個週期後依 RECIST v1.1 達 partial response (PR)，腫瘤測量值下降 61%；Tg 降至 2.7 ng/mL。之後因治療相關腹瀉再度減量。病人對 lenvatinib 維持反應達 48 個月，直到出現寡進展性病灶（一處胸椎椎體轉移）。於 stereotactic body radiotherapy 期間暫停 lenvatinib，之後再恢復。7 個月後（2023 年 12 月），因左側 styloid 咽旁間隙出現腫塊而停用 lenvatinib。

> 📊 圖：Case 1 病人 CT（2019 年 5 月與 7 月）—Panel A/D 左頸淋巴結、Panel B/E 肺轉移、Panel C/F gastrohepatic 淋巴結；治療後腫瘤明顯縮小（原書 Figure 1）。

In January 2024, second-line dabrafenib plus trametinib was initiated. By cycle 4, RECIST v1.1 met criteria for PR with a 31% decrease. During that time, Tg increased from 14 to 19 ng/mL. While on dabrafenib plus trametinib, the patient received a second 100 mCi dose of thyrotropin alfa–stimulated RAI; the stimulated Tg was 120 ng/mL, and the subsequent whole-body scan showed a solitary focus of uptake in the left neck. Two months later, suppressed Tg dropped to 6.1 ng/mL. A dose reduction was required for treatment-related pyrexia syndrome, but the regimen was otherwise well tolerated. He remained responsive for 22 months until multifocal thoracic spine metastases developed in November 2025. Dabrafenib plus trametinib was discontinued, stereotactic body radiotherapy was administered, and further systemic therapy (cabozantinib or clinical trials) is now under consideration.

> 2024 年 1 月啟動二線 dabrafenib 併 trametinib。至第 4 週期依 RECIST v1.1 達 PR，腫瘤下降 31%。期間 Tg 由 14 上升至 19 ng/mL。在使用 dabrafenib 併 trametinib 期間，病人接受第二次 100 mCi thyrotropin alfa 刺激的 RAI；刺激下 Tg 為 120 ng/mL，隨後全身掃描顯示左頸單一攝取灶（顯示 redifferentiation 使 RAI 攝取恢復）。2 個月後抑制狀態 Tg 降至 6.1 ng/mL。因治療相關 pyrexia syndrome（發熱症候群）需減量，其餘耐受良好。病人維持反應達 22 個月，直到 2025 年 11 月出現多發性胸椎轉移。停用 dabrafenib 併 trametinib，給予 stereotactic body radiotherapy，目前考慮進一步全身性治療（cabozantinib 或臨床試驗）。

### Case 2 — 放射線暴露後、RET fusion–positive PTC

A 16-year-old boy developed aplastic anemia, treated with high-dose chemotherapy and total-body irradiation followed by a matched, unrelated bone marrow transplant; he remained in remission. At age 23, he presented with a persistent dry cough, dyspnea on exertion, dysphagia, and a right neck mass. Neck MRI showed a diffusely enlarged thyroid gland, circumferential encasement of the trachea with loss of fat planes, severe tracheal narrowing, and bilateral multistation cervical adenopathy (largest a 3.6-cm node in right level 2). Core-needle biopsy showed PTC with columnar and focal hobnail features. BRAF V600E IHC was negative. PET-CT showed FDG-avid bilateral cervical and mediastinal adenopathy, diffuse uptake in a multinodular thyroid, and no uptake in a 3-mm lung nodule. Next-generation sequencing revealed a CCDC6-RET fusion. The multidisciplinary tumor board determined the tumor was unresectable.

> 一名 16 歲男孩罹患 aplastic anemia，接受高劑量化療與 total-body irradiation，之後進行配對非親屬 bone marrow transplant，此後持續緩解。23 歲時出現持續乾咳、運動時呼吸困難、吞嚥困難與右頸腫塊。頸部 MRI 顯示甲狀腺瀰漫腫大、環狀包繞氣管且脂肪界面消失、嚴重氣管狹窄，以及雙側多站頸部淋巴結腫大（最大為右側 level 2 的 3.6 cm 淋巴結）。粗針切片為 PTC，具 columnar 與局部 hobnail 特徵。BRAF V600E IHC 陰性。PET-CT 顯示雙側頸部與縱膈淋巴結 FDG 高攝取、多結節甲狀腺瀰漫攝取，3 mm 肺結節無攝取。Next-generation sequencing 發現 CCDC6-RET fusion。多專科腫瘤委員會判定腫瘤無法切除。

Systemic therapy was initiated with selpercatinib to achieve clinical benefit and, if a response occurred, to make the disease resectable. Selpercatinib was dose-reduced due to QTc prolongation, which resolved. Cough, dyspnea, and dysphagia resolved. After 2 cycles, tumor measurements decreased by 27% (RECIST v1.1); after 4 cycles, by 50%. At 8 months, the patient reported erectile dysfunction (a known selpercatinib adverse event), treated with tadalafil. Restaging neck CT showed significant improvement, with circumferential tracheal involvement no longer present; overall there was a 61% decrease in tumor measurements. Planning for staged hemithyroidectomies with bilateral central and lateral neck compartment dissection is underway, and postoperative RAI will be planned based on surgical outcomes, with consideration of pre-RAI selpercatinib for redifferentiation vs RAI alone.

> 以 selpercatinib 啟動全身性治療，目標為取得臨床效益，若有反應則使疾病轉為可切除。因 QTc 延長而減量，之後緩解。咳嗽、呼吸困難與吞嚥困難皆消退。2 個週期後腫瘤測量值下降 27%（RECIST v1.1）；4 個週期後下降 50%。第 8 個月時病人出現 erectile dysfunction（selpercatinib 已知副作用），以 tadalafil 治療。追蹤頸部 CT 顯示明顯改善，環狀氣管侵犯已消失；整體腫瘤下降 61%。目前正規劃分階段 hemithyroidectomy 併雙側 central 與 lateral 頸部區室廓清，術後 RAI 將依手術結果規劃，並考慮 RAI 前先以 selpercatinib 進行 redifferentiation，或單用 RAI。

## 🔬 背景與重要性 Background & Significance

Most deaths due specifically to thyroid cancer occur in patients with radioiodine (RAI)-refractory DTC. A decade ago, treatment options for advanced RAI-refractory DTC were limited to vascular endothelial growth factor receptor–targeting multikinase inhibitors (MKIs). MKIs have demonstrated efficacy in tumor shrinkage, progression-free survival, and, in some cases, overall survival, but their treatment-related adverse events can be impactful. Driver molecular alterations — including BRAF and RAS variants and RET/NTRK/ALK and ROS1 gene rearrangements — are often targetable with gene-specific systemic therapy. Decision-making regarding the best first-line therapy and optimal treatment sequence remains challenging.

> 甲狀腺癌特異性死亡多發生在 RAI-refractory DTC 病人身上。十年前，晚期 RAI-refractory DTC 的治療選項僅限於針對 vascular endothelial growth factor receptor 的 multikinase inhibitors (MKIs)。MKIs 在腫瘤縮小、progression-free survival，以及部分情況下 overall survival 皆有療效，但其治療相關不良反應可能相當顯著。Driver molecular alterations——包括 BRAF 與 RAS 變異，以及 RET/NTRK/ALK 與 ROS1 基因重排——常可用 gene-specific systemic therapy 標靶治療。然而，如何選擇最佳一線治療與最適治療順序，仍是臨床挑戰。

### Genotype-phenotype correlation（基因型-表現型相關性）

A rich genotype-phenotype correlation is observed across DTC. Driver alterations — pathogenic point variants, gene rearrangements, or fusions — are mutually exclusive. The BRAF V600E variant primarily gives rise to classic variant PTC as well as tall cell and hobnail variants, whereas RAS (N/H/K) pathogenic variants most frequently give rise to follicular thyroid cancer and follicular variant PTCs. Although oncogenic kinase fusions are less common than BRAF V600E in PTC, PTC harbors the highest frequency of oncogenic kinase fusions across all adult solid cancers. Fusions involving RET kinase are most common, followed by BRAF, NTRK 1 or 3, ALK, and ROS1, in decreasing frequency.

> DTC 呈現豐富的 genotype-phenotype correlation。驅動變異（致病性點突變、基因重排或 fusion）彼此互斥。BRAF V600E 主要產生 classic variant PTC，以及 tall cell 與 hobnail variants；RAS (N/H/K) 致病變異則最常導致 follicular thyroid cancer 與 follicular variant PTC。雖然致癌性 kinase fusion 在 PTC 中不如 BRAF V600E 常見，但 PTC 在所有成人固態腫瘤中具有最高頻率的致癌 kinase fusion。以 RET kinase fusion 最常見，其次依序為 BRAF、NTRK 1 或 3、ALK 與 ROS1。

Oncogenic kinase fusions are seen more frequently in pediatric and young adult patients and in thyroid cancers arising after ionizing radiation exposure, and give rise to characteristic histologic features — multinodular growth, prominent intratumoral fibrosis, lymphovascular invasion, extrathyroidal extension, and lymph node involvement. Kinase fusion–driven DTCs harbor more aggressive clinicopathological features and have worse clinical outcomes, particularly in older adults.

> 致癌 kinase fusion 較常見於兒童與年輕成人病人，以及曾暴露於 ionizing radiation 的甲狀腺癌，並呈現特徵性組織學表現——多結節生長、明顯瘤內纖維化、lymphovascular invasion、extrathyroidal extension 與淋巴結侵犯。Kinase fusion 驅動的 DTC 具有較侵襲性的臨床病理特徵，且預後較差，在年長成人尤然。

### Practice Gaps（實務落差）

- Molecular diagnostic testing of tumor tissue is indicated for every patient with advanced RAI-refractory DTC needing systemic therapy and should be performed whenever possible before starting first-line therapy — yet many patients still do not undergo testing despite its wide availability.
  - 對每一位需要 systemic therapy 的晚期 RAI-refractory DTC 病人，均應進行腫瘤組織分子檢測，且盡量在啟動一線治療前完成；然而儘管檢測已普及，許多病人仍未接受。
- There is significant interest in leveraging MAPK-pathway driver alterations to facilitate RAI treatment for RAI-refractory disease, yet gold-standard randomized phase III data are lacking.
  - 利用影響 MAPK pathway 的驅動變異來促進 RAI-refractory 疾病重新對 RAI 有反應（redifferentiation）備受關注，但仍缺乏黃金標準的隨機分派 phase III 數據。
- Highly effective gene-specific therapy targeting N/H/K RAS alterations — common in DTCs — is not yet available; a new generation of RAS-specific inhibitors is in development, and RAS-driven thyroid cancer studies are urgently needed.
  - 針對 DTC 中常見的 N/H/K RAS 變異，目前尚無高效的 gene-specific 治療；新一代 RAS-specific inhibitors 正在開發，亟需針對 RAS 驅動甲狀腺癌的研究。

## 🧭 診斷與評估 Diagnosis & Evaluation

- **Molecular testing is mandatory**：Tumor next-generation sequencing — particularly a platform with a robust method for detecting oncogenic kinase fusions — is critical for every patient with advanced RAI-refractory DTC before initiating systemic therapy, so that no patient harboring a targetable alteration is missed.
  - 分子檢測為必要步驟：腫瘤 next-generation sequencing——尤其是能可靠偵測致癌 kinase fusion 的平台——對每位晚期 RAI-refractory DTC 病人在啟動全身性治療前都至關重要，以免遺漏任何具有可標靶變異的病人。

- **IHC vs NGS**：In Case 1, BRAF V600E was first identified by IHC and later confirmed by NGS, which additionally revealed TERT promoter C228T and CIC comutations. In Case 2, BRAF V600E IHC was negative, and NGS uncovered a CCDC6-RET fusion — illustrating that a negative BRAF IHC does not exclude an actionable driver, and fusion-capable NGS is essential.
  - IHC 與 NGS：Case 1 先以 IHC 發現 BRAF V600E，之後由 NGS 確認，並額外發現 TERT promoter C228T 與 CIC 共突變。Case 2 的 BRAF V600E IHC 為陰性，NGS 才揭露 CCDC6-RET fusion——說明 BRAF IHC 陰性並不能排除可標靶驅動變異，能偵測 fusion 的 NGS 不可或缺。

- **Response & disease monitoring**：治療反應以 RECIST v1.1 評估腫瘤測量值變化；serum Tg（在 TSH 抑制或 thyrotropin alfa 刺激下）與 whole-body RAI scan 用於追蹤疾病活性與 redifferentiation 後的 RAI 攝取恢復。

- **判定 RAI-refractory**：Case 1 於復發時的診斷性 RAI whole-body imaging 無攝取，即符合 RAI-refractory 定義，據此轉向 systemic targeted therapy。

## 💊 治療與處置 Management

### BRAF V600E–positive、RAI-refractory PTC

- **First-line：lenvatinib**。Compared with dabrafenib ± trametinib, the evidence did not support replacing lenvatinib as standard first-line therapy for progressive BRAF variant–positive RAI-refractory PTC, regardless of genotype.
  - 一線首選為 lenvatinib。與 dabrafenib ± trametinib 相比，證據不支持以後者取代 lenvatinib 作為進展性 BRAF 變異陽性 RAI-refractory PTC 的標準一線治療（不論基因型）。
- **Second-line：dabrafenib plus trametinib**。以 tissue-agnostic FDA 核准（2023 年）為基礎，於 lenvatinib 或其他 MKI 進展後廣泛採用。
- **支持證據**：
  - Randomized phase II（n=53）：dabrafenib 單藥 ORR 35%、median PFS 10.7 months；dabrafenib 併 trametinib ORR 30%、median PFS 15.1 months。
  - International phase III（late 2025，先前接受 1–2 種 MKI）：dabrafenib 併 trametinib 對比 placebo，median PFS 12.8 months（95% CI, 10.2–21.2）vs 3.7 months（95% CI, 2.3–7.5），ORR 57.4%。
- **副作用處置示範（Case 1）**：lenvatinib 相關高血壓（需降壓藥並減量）與腹瀉（再度減量）；dabrafenib 併 trametinib 相關 pyrexia syndrome（需減量）。

### Redifferentiation（重新分化）

- BRAF V600E–driven PTC 具高度 MAPK signaling，導致 sodium–iodide symporter 與 TPO 等分化基因下調；阻斷 MAPK 可望恢復 RAI 攝取。
- **MERAIODE**（multicenter, phase II, single-arm，n=24，dabrafenib-trametinib 併 ¹³¹I）：6 個月時 RECIST v1.1 反應率 38%，顯示部分病人可能獲益。尚無 phase III 隨機對照數據。

### RET fusion–positive DTC

- **First-line：RET-specific inhibitor（selpercatinib 或 pralsetinib）優先於 MKI**。
- **LIBRETTO-001（selpercatinib）**：MKI-naïve RET fusion–positive DTC ORR 96%，PFS rate 於 2 與 4 年分別為 95% 與 66%；previously treated ORR 85%、median PFS 27.4 months。常見副作用（多為 grade 1–2）包括高血壓、肝轉胺酶上升、腹瀉、疲倦；30% 減量，僅 2% 因不良反應停藥。2020 年 FDA 首度核准 RET-specific inhibitor。
- **ARROW（pralsetinib，n=25，多數曾用 ≥1 MKI）**：ORR 91%、median PFS 25.4 months；安全性與 selpercatinib 相似，但骨髓抑制略多。2020 年獲 FDA 核准。

### NTRK fusion–positive DTC

- **First-line：TRK-specific inhibitor**，資料以 larotrectinib 為佳。
- **Larotrectinib**（3 項 phase I/II，甲狀腺癌 29 人：22 DTC、7 ATC）：DTC ORR 86%，PFS rate 於 1 與 2 年為 100% 與 84%；ATC 療效不佳（ORR 29%、median PFS 僅 2.2 months、median OS 8.8 months）。僅 7% 需減量，無人因不良反應停藥。2018 年成為 FDA 首個 tumor-agnostic 小分子抑制劑核准。
- **Entrectinib**（抑制 TRK A/B/C、ROS1、ALK；整合分析 121 人含 13 甲狀腺癌）：ORR 54%，median PFS 與 OS 皆 19.9 months。
- **Repotrectinib**（次世代 TRK A/B/C 與 ROS1 抑制劑，可克服 solvent front resistance）：kinase inhibitor–naïve ORR 59%、median PFS 30.3 months；曾接受 TRK inhibitor 者 ORR 48%、median PFS 7.4 months。

### RET fusion 個案手術策略（Case 2）

以 selpercatinib 作為 neoadjuvant/系統性治療使無法切除的腫瘤縮小、轉為可切除，規劃分階段 hemithyroidectomy 併雙側頸部區室廓清；術後 RAI 依手術結果規劃，並考慮 RAI 前先行 selpercatinib redifferentiation。

## 🧠 個案解析與臨床推理 Case Analysis & Clinical Reasoning

**Case 1 的核心邏輯**：本案是晚期 RAI-refractory DTC 的典型故事。PTC 為 DTC 最常見亞型，BRAF V600E 是 PTC 最常見的驅動變異。多數 BRAF V600E–positive PTC 病人長期預後良好，但一旦合併共突變（最常為 TERT promoter），較易出現復發性 RAI-refractory 疾病，且甲狀腺癌特異性死亡風險升高。Case 1 同時具 TERT promoter C228T 與 CIC E1366K 共突變，恰印證此高風險組合的侵襲性病程。

**治療順序（best sequence）**：依現有臨床試驗數據，進展性 BRAF V600E–positive RAI-refractory PTC 的最佳順序為 lenvatinib → dabrafenib + trametinib → cabozantinib。關鍵推理是：雖然 dabrafenib ± trametinib 有效，但其 phase II 療效未優於不分基因型皆有效的一線 lenvatinib；而 2025 年 phase III 於「先前接受 MKI 後」的族群強化了 dabrafenib 併 trametinib 的二線地位。

**Redifferentiation 的臨床機會**：Case 1 在 dabrafenib 併 trametinib 期間，刺激性 Tg 升至 120 ng/mL 並於全身掃描重現攝取灶，之後抑制性 Tg 下降，示範 MAPK 阻斷後 RAI 攝取的恢復。惟此策略仍缺 phase III 隨機對照證據，屬個別化考量。

**Case 2 的鑑別與推理**：年輕、曾接受 total-body irradiation、BRAF IHC 陰性——這些線索高度提示 kinase fusion 驅動。務必以能偵測 fusion 的 NGS 檢查，最終發現 CCDC6-RET fusion。Kinase fusion–positive DTC 好發於兒童與年輕成人及放射線暴露者，具多結節、瘤內纖維化、局部侵襲等特徵，且對 gene-specific therapy 反應極佳（selpercatinib ORR 高），可作為使不可切除腫瘤轉為可切除的策略。

**常見陷阱 Pitfalls**：
- 未在一線治療前做腫瘤分子檢測，錯失可標靶驅動變異——晚期 RAI-refractory DTC 中此類變異其實常見。
- 以 BRAF IHC 陰性即停止分子評估，遺漏 RET/NTRK 等 fusion。
- 忽略共突變（如 TERT promoter）對預後與復發風險的加乘影響。
- 將 DTC 的優異標靶療效外推至 ATC——larotrectinib 於 ATC 療效明顯較差。
- 標靶治療副作用（高血壓、腹瀉、QTc 延長、pyrexia、erectile dysfunction）需主動監測與劑量調整以維持治療。

## ⭐ 重點整理 Key Takeaways

- 每位需要 systemic therapy 的晚期 RAI-refractory DTC 病人，都應在啟動治療前接受 tumor molecular testing（含可偵測 kinase fusion 的 NGS）；可影響治療決策的 targetable driver alterations 其實常見。
- 進展性 BRAF V600E–positive RAI-refractory PTC，一線首選 lenvatinib，進展或不耐受後改用 dabrafenib plus trametinib；建議順序為 lenvatinib → dabrafenib + trametinib → cabozantinib。
- 2025 年 international phase III 顯示，先前接受 1–2 種 MKI 的 BRAF V600E–positive RAI-refractory PTC，dabrafenib plus trametinib median PFS 12.8 vs placebo 3.7 months、ORR 57.4%，鞏固其二線地位。
- RET- 或 NTRK fusion–positive RAI-refractory DTC，應以 RET- 或 TRK-specific gene therapy 作為一線治療（selpercatinib/pralsetinib、larotrectinib），療效優且耐受良好。
- Kinase fusion–driven DTC 好發於年輕族群與放射線暴露者，BRAF IHC 常為陰性，需依賴 fusion-capable NGS 才能辨識。
- BRAF V600E–positive PTC 合併 TERT promoter 等共突變者，復發性 RAI-refractory 疾病與癌症特異性死亡風險升高。
- MAPK 阻斷（如 dabrafenib-trametinib 或 RET/TRK inhibitor）具 redifferentiation 潛力以恢復 RAI 攝取（如 MERAIODE 6 個月反應率 38%），但仍缺 phase III 隨機對照證據。
- Gene-specific therapy 可使原本不可切除的 fusion–positive 腫瘤縮小、轉為可切除（如 Case 2 的 selpercatinib）。
