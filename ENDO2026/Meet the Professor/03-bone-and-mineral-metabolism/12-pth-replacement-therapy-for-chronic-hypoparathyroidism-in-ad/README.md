## 🩺 臨床情境 Clinical Scenario

本章附有兩則臨床案例，說明 PTH replacement therapy 在慢性 HypoPT 的實際應用。

### Case 1 — ADH1（*CASR* activating variant）

> A 35-year-old man (BMI = 27.5 kg/m²) with autosomal dominant hypocalcemia type 1 (ADH1) due to a likely pathogenic *CASR* variant (c.2482A>C; p.Thr828Pro) presents with persistent hypocalcemia, despite large doses of conventional therapy. Baseline evaluation shows hypocalcemia, hyperphosphatemia, low PTH level, hypercalciuria, hypomagnesemia, and hypokalemia. His course is complicated by frequent seizures, nephrocalcinosis, recurrent nephrolithiasis, and stage 3 chronic kidney disease.

一位 35 歲男性（BMI = 27.5 kg/m²），罹患 autosomal dominant hypocalcemia type 1 (ADH1)，肇因於一個 likely pathogenic 的 *CASR* 變異（c.2482A>C; p.Thr828Pro）。儘管使用高劑量 conventional therapy，仍持續 hypocalcemia。基礎評估顯示 hypocalcemia、hyperphosphatemia、低 PTH、hypercalciuria、hypomagnesemia 與 hypokalemia。病程並發頻繁 seizures、nephrocalcinosis、反覆 nephrolithiasis，以及 stage 3 chronic kidney disease。

> Despite treatment with calcium carbonate and calcitriol, biochemical control remains suboptimal. Palopegteriparatide is initiated at 18 mcg daily and is gradually titrated to 27 mcg daily after 6 months, reaching 60 mcg daily by month 52. By month 52, the patient has improved serum calcium, reduced urinary calcium excretion, and improved kidney function. He has had no kidney stones since starting palopegteriparatide.

儘管使用 calcium carbonate 與 calcitriol，生化控制仍不理想。開始使用 palopegteriparatide，起始劑量 18 mcg 每日，6 個月後逐步調升至 27 mcg 每日，到第 52 個月達到 60 mcg 每日。至第 52 個月時，病人血清 calcium 改善、尿鈣排泄減少、腎功能改善，且自開始 palopegteriparatide 後未再發生腎結石。

### Case 2 — Postsurgical HypoPT

> A 43-year-old woman with postsurgical HypoPT has been treated with conventional therapy requiring high doses of calcium supplements (600 mg of calcium citrate 4 times daily) and calcitriol (0.25 mcg 4 times weekly) for approximately 5 years. Despite this therapy, she remains symptomatic, with severe fatigue, headaches, cognitive dysfunction (brain fog), irritability, depressive symptoms, and paresthesias.

一位 43 歲女性，罹患 postsurgical HypoPT，接受 conventional therapy 約 5 年，需高劑量 calcium 補充（calcium citrate 600 mg 每日 4 次）與 calcitriol（0.25 mcg 每週 4 次）。儘管如此，仍持續有症狀，包括嚴重疲倦、頭痛、認知功能障礙（brain fog）、易怒、憂鬱症狀與 paresthesias。

> These symptoms necessitate frequent rescue calcium supplementation and are accompanied by marked fluctuations in serum calcium levels, ranging from hypocalcemia to hypercalcemia. She has been unable to continue working and is now on disability. At age 45 years, treatment with palopegteriparatide is initiated at 18 mcg daily and is gradually uptitrated to 27 mcg daily over 32 months with successful discontinuation of conventional therapy. She states that PTH therapy "has given me back my life," and she has been able to return to work.

這些症狀使她需要頻繁的緊急 calcium 補充，並伴隨血清 calcium 明顯波動，由 hypocalcemia 至 hypercalcemia。她已無法繼續工作，目前領取身心障礙補助。在 45 歲時開始 palopegteriparatide，起始 18 mcg 每日，於 32 個月內逐步調升至 27 mcg 每日，並成功停用 conventional therapy。她表示 PTH therapy「讓我重新拿回我的人生」，並得以重返職場。

## 🔬 背景與重要性 Background & Significance

> HypoPT is a rare endocrine disorder with a prevalence of 6.4 to 37 per 100,000. The diagnosis is confirmed by low serum corrected or ionized calcium, in the presence of low or inappropriately normal PTH levels.

HypoPT 是一種罕見的內分泌疾病，盛行率為每 100,000 人 6.4 至 37 例。診斷依據為血清 corrected calcium 或 ionized calcium 偏低，且合併偏低或 inappropriately normal 的 PTH 濃度。

> Chronic HypoPT is associated with multiple long-term complications, including renal complications (nephrolithiasis, nephrocalcinosis, and impaired kidney function), cardiovascular abnormalities (prolonged QT-interval, cardiac arrhythmias, and hypocalcemic heart failure), and neurologic manifestations (paresthesia, cognitive impairment, and seizures). Additional manifestations include cataracts, increased susceptibility to infections, and increased all-cause mortality.

慢性 HypoPT 與多種長期併發症相關，包括腎臟併發症（nephrolithiasis、nephrocalcinosis 與腎功能受損）、心血管異常（prolonged QT-interval、cardiac arrhythmias 與 hypocalcemic heart failure），以及神經學表現（paresthesia、認知功能障礙與 seizures）。其他表現尚包括 cataracts、感染易感性增加，以及 all-cause mortality 上升。

> The first-line treatment for chronic HypoPT is conventional therapy with active vitamin D and calcium supplements. Recently, the US FDA and the European Medicines Agency approved the long-acting PTH analogue, palopegteriparatide, as a therapeutic option for adults with chronic HypoPT for whom conventional therapy fails.

慢性 HypoPT 的第一線治療為 conventional therapy，即 active vitamin D 加上 calcium 補充。近期，US FDA 與 European Medicines Agency 核准了長效 PTH analogue——palopegteriparatide，作為 conventional therapy 治療失敗的成人慢性 HypoPT 病人的治療選項。

> Palopegteriparatide has been shown to correct hypocalcemia, sustain normocalcemia, normalize urinary calcium and serum phosphorus levels, and improve quality of life (QoL). Kidney function also improves dramatically with palopegteriparatide, allowing successful discontinuation of conventional therapy in most patients.

palopegteriparatide 已證實能矯正 hypocalcemia、維持 normocalcemia、使尿鈣與血清 phosphorus 恢復正常，並改善生活品質（QoL）。使用 palopegteriparatide 後腎功能也顯著改善，使多數病人得以成功停用 conventional therapy。

> The phase 2 clinical trial evaluated patient-reported outcomes using the HypoPT Patient Experience Scales (HPES) and the Short-Form Health Survey (SF-36v2) through week 110. Palopegteriparatide was associated with significant improvements in disease-specific symptoms, daily functioning and well-being, and general health-related QoL.

phase 2 clinical trial 以 HypoPT Patient Experience Scales (HPES) 與 Short-Form Health Survey (SF-36v2) 評估 patient-reported outcomes，追蹤至 week 110。palopegteriparatide 與疾病特異性症狀、日常功能與身心安適，以及一般 health-related QoL 的顯著改善相關。

> Furthermore, data from the phase 3 PaTHway trial on bone mineral density (BMD) showed that the mean BMD Z-scores decreased from the abnormally high values toward age- and sex-matched norms from baseline to week 52. The skeletal changes observed with palopegteriparatide are consistent with restoration of normal bone remodeling.

此外，phase 3 PaTHway trial 的 bone mineral density (BMD) 資料顯示，平均 BMD Z-scores 由異常偏高的數值，於 baseline 至 week 52 間朝向 age- 與 sex-matched 常模下降。palopegteriparatide 所觀察到的骨骼變化，與 normal bone remodeling 的恢復一致。

### Practice Gaps 臨床落差

- **Determining the reversibility of long-term complications of HypoPT with PTH replacement therapy.**
  釐清 PTH replacement therapy 能否逆轉 HypoPT 的長期併發症。
- **Limited understanding of fracture risk** in patients with chronic HypoPT among premenopausal and postmenopausal women and among men younger and older than 50 years.
  對慢性 HypoPT 病人骨折風險的了解有限，涵蓋停經前與停經後女性，以及小於與大於 50 歲的男性。
- **The effects of PTH replacement on neuropsychiatric and cognitive function** in patients with chronic HypoPT need further research.
  PTH replacement 對慢性 HypoPT 病人神經精神與認知功能的影響，仍需進一步研究。

## 💊 治療與處置 Management

### Conventional therapy 的角色與限制

> Conventional therapy with calcium supplementation and active vitamin D remains the first-line treatment for chronic HypoPT with a goal of maintaining serum-corrected calcium levels in the low-normal range or just below the normal reference range while normalizing serum phosphorus, magnesium, and urinary calcium levels.

以 calcium 補充加上 active vitamin D 的 conventional therapy 仍為慢性 HypoPT 的第一線治療，目標為將 serum-corrected calcium 維持於 low-normal 範圍或略低於正常參考範圍，同時使血清 phosphorus、magnesium 與尿鈣恢復正常。

> Wide fluctuations in serum calcium levels remain a limitation of conventional therapy, as oral calcium and calcitriol are associated with an increased risk of hypercalcemia. Patients often experience persistent hypocalcemic symptoms... The high pill burden associated with conventional therapy further compromises adherence and QoL.

血清 calcium 大幅波動仍是 conventional therapy 的限制，因為口服 calcium 與 calcitriol 會增加 hypercalcemia 風險。病人常有持續的 hypocalcemic 症狀，包括麻木、刺痛、肌肉痙攣及認知功能障礙，即使血清 calcium 已接近正常參考範圍仍可能存在。高服藥負擔（pill burden）進一步影響順從性與 QoL。

> Conventional therapy does not address the underlying PTH deficiency and cannot adequately prevent multisystem complications. Hyperphosphatemia may persist despite dietary modifications, phosphate binders, and reductions in active vitamin D because the absence of PTH impairs renal phosphate excretion.

conventional therapy 並未處理根本的 PTH 缺乏，也無法充分預防多系統併發症。由於缺乏 PTH 會損害腎臟排磷，即使調整飲食、使用 phosphate binders 並減少 active vitamin D，hyperphosphatemia 仍可能持續。

### 指引與典範轉移

> The 2022 international HypoPT guidelines recommend conventional therapy as first-line therapy (weak recommendation, low-quality evidence), but acknowledge that PTH replacement should be considered when conventional therapy proves unsatisfactory. This represents a paradigm shift in recognizing HypoPT as an endocrine deficiency disorder amenable to hormone replacement, similar to other classic endocrine diseases.

2022 international HypoPT guidelines 建議 conventional therapy 為第一線治療（weak recommendation, low-quality evidence），但也認可當 conventional therapy 不理想時應考慮 PTH replacement。這代表一項典範轉移：將 HypoPT 視為一種可用荷爾蒙替代的內分泌缺乏症，如同其他典型內分泌疾病。

> Palopegteriparatide is the only PTH replacement therapy currently approved for chronic HypoPT in adults. The 2025 best practice recommendations emphasize that palopegteriparatide is an important therapeutic advance and should be considered when conventional therapy is inadequate or not tolerated. The revised European Society of Endocrinology guidelines similarly recognize that PTH replacement therapy reduces pill burden, improves biochemical parameters, and may improve QoL.

palopegteriparatide 是目前唯一核准用於成人慢性 HypoPT 的 PTH replacement therapy。2025 best practice recommendations 強調 palopegteriparatide 是重要的治療進展，應在 conventional therapy 不足或無法耐受時考慮使用。修訂後的 European Society of Endocrinology guidelines 同樣認可 PTH replacement therapy 能降低 pill burden、改善生化參數，並可能改善 QoL。

### Palopegteriparatide 起始與劑量調整

> Palopegteriparatide is initiated at 18 mcg subcutaneously daily, with a 33% reduction in the active vitamin D dose and close monitoring of serum calcium adjusted for albumin.

palopegteriparatide 起始劑量為 18 mcg，皮下注射，每日一次，同時將 active vitamin D 劑量減少 33%，並密切監測 albumin-corrected serum calcium。

> Because palopegteriparatide has a long half-life of 60 hours, the dose is not adjusted before day 7.

因為 palopegteriparatide 半衰期長達 60 小時，劑量不會在 day 7 之前調整。

滴定原則（依來源）：

- 若血清 calcium 偏低：可增加 calcium 補充與/或 active vitamin D；若 hypocalcemia 於 day 7 之後仍持續，可將 palopegteriparatide 每次增加 3 mcg。
- 若血清 calcium 正常：可逐步減少 active vitamin D 與 calcium，目標是停用 active vitamin D，並將 calcium 補充降至每日 600 mg 或以下。
- 若血清 calcium 偏高：可減少或停用 active vitamin D，並逐步減量、停用 calcium 補充；若在無 calcium 與 active vitamin D 的情況下 hypercalcemia 仍持續，可每 7 天將 palopegteriparatide 減少 3 mcg。

> The minimum palopegteriparatide dosage is 6 mcg daily, and the maximum in clinical trials was 60 mcg daily.

palopegteriparatide 最低劑量為每日 6 mcg，臨床試驗中的最高劑量為每日 60 mcg。

**2025 Best Practice Recommendations 的轉換滴定架構**（Case 2 教學）

1. **若 albumin-corrected serum calcium 低於目標範圍**：每 7 天將 palopegteriparatide 增加 3 mcg；在 7 天之前則先增加 calcium 與 active vitamin D。
2. **若 albumin-corrected serum calcium 正常**：續用 palopegteriparatide 至 day 7；於 day 7 減少 active vitamin D 並將 palopegteriparatide 每日增加 3 mcg；若已未使用 active vitamin D，則將 calcium 每日減少 1500 mg 並將 palopegteriparatide 增加 3 mcg。
3. **若 albumin-corrected serum calcium 偏高**：停用或減少 active vitamin D；若已未使用 active vitamin D，則減少 calcium；若 active vitamin D 與 calcium 均已停用，則減少 palopegteriparatide。

> \* 註：因 palopegteriparatide 半衰期為 60 小時，劑量調整間隔不得短於每 7 天一次。

### 療效與安全性

> The safety profile of palopegteriparatide has been well characterized across multiple trials. Most treatment-emergent adverse events are mild or moderate and include injection-site reactions, nausea, headaches, and postural hypotension.

palopegteriparatide 的安全性在多項試驗中已被充分描述。多數 treatment-emergent adverse events 為輕度或中度，包括注射部位反應、噁心、頭痛與 postural hypotension。

> Data from phase 2 and phase 3 trials with palopegteriparatide demonstrated that more than 90% of individuals achieved independence from conventional therapy, and more than 80% met the multicomponent primary endpoint, with normalization of 24-hour urinary calcium excretion while maintaining normal serum calcium and phosphorus. There were clinically meaningful improvements in kidney function and health-related QoL. Palopegteriparatide was generally well-tolerated with no new safety signals identified.

phase 2 與 phase 3 試驗資料顯示，超過 90% 的病人達到 conventional therapy 的獨立性（不再需要），超過 80% 達到多成分 primary endpoint，即在維持正常血清 calcium 與 phosphorus 的同時，使 24-hour urinary calcium 排泄恢復正常。腎功能與 health-related QoL 亦有具臨床意義的改善。palopegteriparatide 整體耐受性良好，未發現新的安全性訊號。

### 替代選項

> If palopegteriparatide is unavailable, teriparatide can be used, often in combination with conventional therapy, in multiple daily doses or via a subcutaneous pump, because its half-life is only 1 hour. Teriparatide use in HypoPT is off-label and not approved for this indication.

若無法取得 palopegteriparatide，可使用 teriparatide，通常與 conventional therapy 併用，因其半衰期僅 1 小時，需每日多次給藥或以皮下 pump 給藥。teriparatide 用於 HypoPT 屬 off-label，並未核准此適應症。

## 🧠 個案解析與臨床推理 Case Analysis & Clinical Reasoning

**1. ADH1 的病生理定位**
Case 1 的核心在於辨識 activating *CASR* variant 造成的 ADH1。calcium-sensing receptor 過度活化，使身體「誤以為」血鈣偏高，於是抑制 PTH 分泌並增加尿鈣排泄。因此病人呈現 hypocalcemia、低/inappropriately normal PTH、hyperphosphatemia 與 hypercalciuria。臨床上最容易踩的陷阱，是看到 hypocalcemia 就直覺加大 conventional therapy 劑量；但在 ADH1，calcitriol 與 calcium 會進一步惡化 hypercalciuria，加速 nephrocalcinosis、nephrolithiasis 與腎功能惡化。這正是本案病人走向 stage 3 CKD 的機轉。

**2. 何時該由 conventional therapy 轉換到 PTH replacement？**
決策要點是「conventional therapy 不滿意（unsatisfactory）」。依來源，關鍵適應症包括：即使生化控制達標仍有持續性 hypocalcemic 症狀、無法維持目標血清 calcium、伴隨 nephrocalcinosis/nephrolithiasis 風險的 hypercalciuria、難治型 hyperphosphatemia，或影響 QoL 的過高 pill burden。Case 2 的教學題特別澄清：單純「病人偏好注射」或「輕度 hyperphosphatemia 但仍在參考範圍內（4.8 mg/dL，範圍 2.2–5.1）」並非最強的轉換理由，最強的指標是「adequate conventional therapy 劑量下仍持續 hypocalcemic 症狀」。

**3. 轉換時的用藥調整邏輯**
常見陷阱是「加藥」思維——擔心 hypocalcemia 而增加 calcium。正確方向相反：起始 palopegteriparatide 時應逐步「減少或停用」calcium 與 active vitamin D。試驗中達到 conventional therapy 獨立性的病人，每日僅需 ≤600 mg elemental calcium 且不需 active vitamin D。因 60 小時半衰期，任何劑量調整間隔不得短於 7 天，過早調整會造成血鈣震盪。

**4. 鑑別診斷思路**
HypoPT 的病因需區分 postsurgical（Case 2，最常見）與 nonsurgical（Case 1 的 genetic/ADH1）。生化上，activating *CASR* variant 造成的 ADH1 與一般 HypoPT 都會有低 PTH，但 ADH1 的 hypercalciuria 特別突出且對 conventional therapy 特別敏感，這使 PTH replacement 在此族群格外有價值——它能同時矯正 hypocalcemia 並降低尿鈣。相對地，pseudohypoparathyroidism 會呈現「高 PTH（阻抗）」，可與本組疾病區分。

**5. 決策要點**
治療目標並非把血鈣拉到正常中段，而是維持在 low-normal 或略低於參考範圍，以症狀緩解為導向，同時避免 hypercalcemia、hypercalciuria 與 hyperphosphatemia，並保護腎功能與 QoL。

## ⭐ 重點整理 Key Takeaways

- 對於 conventional therapy 治療失敗的成人慢性 HypoPT，應考慮 PTH replacement therapy（palopegteriparatide 為目前唯一核准者）。
- 治療目標：緩解 hypocalcemia 症狀，同時避免 hypercalcemia、hypercalciuria 與 hyperphosphatemia，將 serum-corrected calcium 維持於 low-normal 或略低於參考範圍。
- ADH1（activating *CASR* variant）的生化特徵為 hypocalcemia、低/inappropriately normal PTH、hyperphosphatemia 與 hypercalciuria；在此族群，conventional therapy 會惡化 hypercalciuria 並升高 nephrocalcinosis / nephrolithiasis 風險。
- Palopegteriparatide 起始 18 mcg SC 每日，同時減少 active vitamin D 33%；因半衰期 60 小時，劑量調整間隔不得短於 7 天，每次增/減 3 mcg（範圍 6–60 mcg 每日）。
- 轉換策略是「逐步減停」calcium 與 active vitamin D，而非加藥；達獨立者每日僅需 ≤600 mg elemental calcium 且不需 active vitamin D。
- 試驗顯示 >90% 病人達到 conventional therapy 獨立性、>80% 達到多成分 primary endpoint（24-h 尿鈣正常化並維持正常血清 calcium 與 phosphorus），並改善腎功能與 QoL。
- 最強的轉換適應症是「adequate conventional therapy 劑量下仍持續 hypocalcemic 症狀」，而非病人偏好或仍在參考範圍內的輕度異常。
- 若無 palopegteriparatide，可 off-label 使用短效 teriparatide（half-life 1 小時），需每日多次或皮下 pump 給藥。
