## 🩺 臨床情境 Clinical Scenario

本章附有三個臨床病例情境 (Clinical Case Vignettes)，用以說明如何在乳癌女性身上進行骨折風險評估與骨骼健康處置。

### Case 1
> A 38-year-old premenopausal woman with ER+, PR+, node-positive breast cancer receives chemotherapy followed by therapy with GnRH analogues and exemestane (aromatase inhibitor) for 5 years. Her BMD on DXA is –2.9 at the spine and –2.3 at the total hip.

一位 38 歲停經前女性，罹患 ER+、PR+、淋巴結陽性 (node-positive) 乳癌，接受化療後續以 GnRH analogues 合併 exemestane（一種 aromatase inhibitor）治療共 5 年。其 DXA 骨密度：脊椎 T-score –2.9、total hip –2.3。

- **問題一：此病人是否有較高的骨折風險？** 答案：**D) 高度骨折風險 (High increased risk of fracture)**。
  > The patient is at increased risk of fracture because she received chemotherapy, glucocorticoid treatment to reduce adverse effects, a GnRH analogue, and an aromatase inhibitor (exemestane).

  此病人骨折風險升高，因為她同時接受了化療、為減輕化療副作用而使用的 glucocorticoid、GnRH analogue，以及 aromatase inhibitor（exemestane）。

- **問題二：是否應建議治療？** 答案：**E) B、C、D 皆是**。
  > Calcium and vitamin D supplementation, along with resistance training, are the mainstay of osteoporosis treatment. According to international guidelines, pharmacological treatment is advised.

  補充 calcium 與 vitamin D、加上阻力訓練 (resistance training) 是 osteoporosis 治療的基礎；依國際指引，並應同時給予藥物治療 (pharmacological treatment)。

### Case 2
> A 57-year-old postmenopausal woman has ER+, PR+, node-negative breast cancer. Her mother had a hip fracture. She takes glucocorticoids, 7.5 mg daily, because of lupus and received an aromatase inhibitor for 5 to 8 years. Her BMD on DXA is –3.4 at the spine and –2.6 at the total hip.

一位 57 歲停經後女性，罹患 ER+、PR+、淋巴結陰性 (node-negative) 乳癌。母親曾有髖部骨折 (hip fracture)。她因 lupus 長期服用 glucocorticoids 每日 7.5 mg，並使用 aromatase inhibitor 5 至 8 年。DXA 骨密度：脊椎 T-score –3.4、total hip –2.6。

- **問題一：此病人是否有較高的骨折風險？** 答案：**D) 高度骨折風險**。
  > The patient is at increased risk of fracture because she receives high-dosage glucocorticoids to treat lupus, she has a parental history of osteoporosis, she received an aromatase inhibitor for 5 to 8 years, and she has very low BMD.

  此病人骨折風險升高，因為她使用高劑量 glucocorticoids 治療 lupus、有父母骨質疏鬆家族史、使用 aromatase inhibitor 5 至 8 年，且骨密度極低。

- **問題二：是否應建議藥物治療？** 答案：**E) B、C、D 皆是**（補充 calcium 與 vitamin D、阻力訓練，並開始 bisphosphonate 或 denosumab）。依國際指引，應給予藥物治療。

### Case 3
> A 62-year-old postmenopausal woman with ER++, PR++, 4-cm, node-positive breast cancer receives chemotherapy accompanied by glucocorticoid treatment to reduce adverse effects, followed by aromatase inhibitor therapy for 5 to 8 years.

一位 62 歲停經後女性，罹患 ER++、PR++、腫瘤 4 cm、淋巴結陽性乳癌，接受化療（合併 glucocorticoid 以減輕副作用），續以 aromatase inhibitor 治療 5 至 8 年。

- **問題一：此病人是否有較高的復發風險 (risk of recurrence)？** 答案：**D) 高度復發風險 (High increased risk of recurrence)**。此病人接受了化療、glucocorticoids，以及 aromatase inhibitor 5 至 8 年。

- **問題二：不論 BMD 為何，是否應建議以骨骼標靶治療 (bone-targeted treatment) 來預防疾病復發？** 答案：**E) B、C、D 皆是**。
  > Calcium and vitamin D supplementation are the mainstay of osteoporosis treatment. According to international guidelines, pharmacological treatment with denosumab or bisphosphonates is advised.

  補充 calcium 與 vitamin D 為基礎；依國際指引，應以 denosumab 或 bisphosphonates 進行藥物治療。

## 🔬 背景與重要性 Background & Significance

> Breast cancer is the most common malignant disease in women and the second leading cause of cancer-related death among women aged 40 to 55 years in the United States, with more than 40,000 women dying of this disease each year. In the United Kingdom, breast cancer has the fifth-highest incidence rate in Europe and the seventh-highest in the world (95.0 new cases per 100,000). Worldwide, breast cancer affects more than 1 in 10 women.

乳癌是女性最常見的惡性疾病，在美國 40 至 55 歲女性中為癌症相關死亡的第二大原因，每年有超過 40,000 名女性死於此病。在英國，乳癌發生率為歐洲第五高、全球第七高（每 10 萬人 95.0 例新診斷）。全球而言，每 10 位以上女性中就有 1 位罹患乳癌。

> Early diagnosis and improved treatment regimens have significantly increased survival, thereby increasing the potential for long-term adverse effects of cancer treatments, including bone loss and fractures.

早期診斷與治療方案的進步大幅提升存活率，也因此增加了癌症治療長期副作用（包括骨質流失與骨折）的發生機會。

> Skeletal homeostasis is maintained by coupled and balanced bone resorption and formation. Several local and systemic factors regulate these processes, including estrogen, a key regulator of bone resorption. Physiologic decreases in estrogen levels after menopause lead to an imbalance between bone resorption and formation, increasing the risk of osteoporosis (low bone mineral density [BMD]) and fractures.

骨骼恆定 (skeletal homeostasis) 靠著彼此偶聯且平衡的骨吸收 (bone resorption) 與骨形成 (bone formation) 來維持。多種局部與全身性因子調控此過程，其中 estrogen 是骨吸收的關鍵調節者。停經後 estrogen 生理性下降會使骨吸收與骨形成失衡，進而增加 osteoporosis（低骨密度 BMD）與骨折的風險。

> Breast cancer and its therapies can exacerbate this risk. Systemic therapies for breast cancer can interfere with bone turnover, either by affecting gonadal steroid hormone production or by inhibiting peripheral aromatization into estrogen. In addition, some therapies for breast cancer may directly affect bone formation. Regardless of the underlying mechanism, patients with breast cancer are at increased risk for cancer treatment–induced bone loss (CTIBL).

乳癌及其治療會進一步惡化此風險。乳癌的全身性治療可干擾骨代謝 (bone turnover)：或影響性腺類固醇荷爾蒙 (gonadal steroid hormone) 的生成，或抑制周邊芳香化 (peripheral aromatization) 成 estrogen。此外，部分乳癌治療可能直接影響骨形成。無論機轉為何，乳癌病人都面臨較高的癌症治療引起骨質流失 (cancer treatment–induced bone loss, CTIBL) 風險。

### Practice Gaps 臨床落差
> - A low percentage of women with breast cancer are identified as being at high risk for osteoporosis and fractures.
> - Prevention and treatment rates are low among women with breast cancer at high risk for fragility fractures.
> - There is limited awareness that adjuvant bisphosphonate treatment can, in addition to preventing osteoporosis, reduce the risk of bone recurrence.

- 僅有少數乳癌女性被辨識為 osteoporosis 與骨折的高風險族群。
- 在脆弱性骨折高風險的乳癌女性中，接受預防與治療的比例偏低。
- 對於 adjuvant bisphosphonate 治療除了預防 osteoporosis 外，還能降低骨骼復發 (bone recurrence) 風險這一點，臨床認知仍相當有限。

## 🧭 診斷與評估 Diagnosis & Evaluation

### 停經前女性的治療引起骨質疏鬆 Treatment-Induced Osteoporosis in Premenopausal Women

> Most breast malignancies are hormone responsive, and adjuvant endocrine therapy is routinely used to prevent breast cancer recurrence and death. Only a few studies have investigated the effects of chemotherapy in premenopausal women with hormone receptor–negative breast cancer, reporting a significantly increased fracture risk compared with that of control participants. Tamoxifen (± GnRH analogues and chemotherapy) is the treatment of choice in premenopausal women with hormone-responsive breast cancer.

多數乳癌屬荷爾蒙反應型 (hormone responsive)，因此常規使用輔助性內分泌治療 (adjuvant endocrine therapy) 以預防復發與死亡。少數研究探討化療對荷爾蒙受體陰性 (hormone receptor–negative) 停經前女性的影響，發現其骨折風險較對照組顯著升高。對於荷爾蒙反應型停經前乳癌，Tamoxifen（可合併 GnRH analogues 與化療）為首選治療。

> In contrast to outcomes in postmenopausal women, the use of tamoxifen in premenopausal women with breast cancer is associated with a significantly increased fracture risk. The combination of GnRH analogues with tamoxifen ... have a significantly increased risk of osteoporosis, although there are no available data on fracture risk.

與停經後女性不同，Tamoxifen 用於停經前乳癌女性會顯著增加骨折風險。相較於單用 Tamoxifen，合併 GnRH analogues 與 Tamoxifen 者發生 osteoporosis 的風險顯著較高，但目前尚無其骨折風險的資料。

### 停經後女性的治療引起骨質疏鬆 Treatment-Induced Osteoporosis in Postmenopausal Women

> In the past, tamoxifen was the treatment of choice ... and was found to preserve BMD in postmenopausal women, with fracture risk similar between postmenopausal tamoxifen users and nonusers.

過去 Tamoxifen 是荷爾蒙反應型停經後乳癌的首選，並被發現能維持停經後女性的 BMD；停經後使用者與未使用者的骨折風險相近。

> However, aromatase inhibitors have now replaced tamoxifen as the treatment of choice ... because of better efficacy and fewer serious adverse effects, such as uterine cancers and thromboembolic events. Because aromatase inhibitors prevent peripheral estrogen production, they suppress estrogen levels beyond those attained at natural menopause, thereby accelerating bone loss and increasing fracture risk.

然而，因療效更佳、且較少發生子宮癌 (uterine cancer)、血栓栓塞 (thromboembolic events) 等嚴重副作用，aromatase inhibitors 現已取代 Tamoxifen，成為多數停經後荷爾蒙反應型乳癌的首選。由於 aromatase inhibitors 阻斷周邊 estrogen 生成，會將 estrogen 壓低到低於自然停經的水準，進而加速骨質流失並增加骨折風險。

> A recent study reported that, compared with the general population, patients with breast cancer had fracture incidence rate ratios of 1.25 (95% CI, 1.23-1.28) and 1.18 (95% CI, 1.14-1.22) for hospitalization due to any bone fracture and hip fracture, respectively. These ratios remained significantly increased for 10 years. Women taking aromatase inhibitors were at higher risk of fracture than women taking tamoxifen (hazard ratio, 1.48; 95% CI, 0.98-2.22). Additionally, patients ... hospitalized for a bone fracture had a higher risk of death (hazard ratio, 1.83; 95% CI, 1.50-2.22).

一項近期研究指出，相較於一般族群，乳癌病人因任一骨折住院與因髖部骨折住院的發生率比 (incidence rate ratio) 分別為 1.25（95% CI, 1.23–1.28）與 1.18（95% CI, 1.14–1.22），且此升高在 10 年內持續存在。使用 aromatase inhibitors 者骨折風險高於使用 Tamoxifen 者（hazard ratio 1.48；95% CI, 0.98–2.22）。此外，因骨折住院的乳癌病人死亡風險亦較高（hazard ratio 1.83；95% CI, 1.50–2.22）。

### 停經前與停經後女性的治療相關骨折風險 Treatment-Related Fracture Risk

> A recent large-scale, case-control study showed a 2-fold higher fracture risk after chemotherapy than in healthy controls. Fracture risk with tamoxifen use depends on menopausal status. In premenopausal women ... tamoxifen use increased fracture risk 2-fold compared with healthy controls, whereas postmenopausal women had no increased fracture risk.

近期一項大型病例對照研究顯示，化療後骨折風險為健康對照組的 2 倍。Tamoxifen 相關骨折風險取決於停經狀態：停經前荷爾蒙敏感性乳癌女性使用 Tamoxifen 的骨折風險為健康對照組的 2 倍，而停經後女性則無骨折風險增加。

> Aromatase inhibitor–associated bone loss (AIBL) leads to a marked increase in bone resorption, with a 2- to 4-fold increase in bone loss compared with physiologic postmenopausal BMD loss. ... Randomized controlled trials including aromatase inhibitor therapy for 5 years suggest an increased absolute fracture risk of around 10%, indicating that 1 in 10 women will eventually fracture. ... In the latter, the fracture incidence in women with breast cancer on an aromatase inhibitor was reported to be 18% to 20% after 5 years of follow-up, indicating that in clinical practice, about 1 in 5 women will sustain an aromatase inhibitor–related fracture.

Aromatase inhibitor 相關骨質流失 (aromatase inhibitor–associated bone loss, AIBL) 會使骨吸收明顯上升，骨質流失速度為生理性停經後 BMD 流失的 2 至 4 倍。使用 aromatase inhibitor 治療 5 年的隨機對照試驗顯示，絕對骨折風險上升約 10%，意即每 10 位女性中約有 1 位最終會發生骨折。惟這些研究採嚴格納入與排除條件，未必反映常規臨床實務中未經篩選族群的骨折風險。在真實世界資料（含一項近期隨機對照試驗）中，使用 aromatase inhibitor 的乳癌女性追蹤 5 年後骨折發生率高達 18% 至 20%，代表臨床實務上約每 5 位女性就有 1 位會發生 aromatase inhibitor 相關骨折。

> After termination of aromatase inhibitor treatment, bone turnover normalizes and BMD can partially recover, but microarchitectural deterioration persists, leading to a persistently increased fracture risk. ... For those advocating an increased duration of aromatase inhibitor treatment for up to 10 years, a further increased fracture risk, in addition to the 2% to 3% per year, must be considered.

停用 aromatase inhibitor 後，骨代謝會恢復正常、BMD 可部分回升，但骨骼微結構 (microarchitecture) 的惡化持續存在，導致骨折風險長期偏高。近期對於是否將 aromatase inhibitor 療程延長至 10 年出現分歧證據；對於主張延長至 10 年者，除了每年 2% 至 3% 的既有骨質流失外，還須考量進一步增加的骨折風險。

### 骨折風險評估 Assessment of Osteoporosis-Related Fracture Risk

> Recently, an updated position statement from 7 international bone and cancer societies evaluated fracture risk and included treatment recommendations for AIBL. After a systematic literature review ... an evidence-based medicine approach was used to determine when to initiate antiresorptive therapy for AIBL, the appropriate antiresorptive therapy, and follow-up/monitoring procedures.

近期由 7 個國際骨骼與癌症學會提出的更新版立場聲明 (position statement) 評估了骨折風險，並納入 AIBL 的治療建議。透過系統性文獻回顧與實證醫學 (evidence-based medicine) 方法，確立何時應對 AIBL 啟動抗骨吸收治療 (antiresorptive therapy)、適當的抗骨吸收藥物選擇，以及追蹤與監測流程。

> In addition to aromatase inhibitor therapy, factors that increase fracture risk in women with breast cancer include a T-score less than –1.5, age older than 65 years, BMI less than 20 kg/m², family history of hip fracture, personal history of fragility fracture after age 50 years, oral corticosteroid use for more than 6 months, rheumatoid arthritis, and smoking.

除了 aromatase inhibitor 治療外，乳癌女性骨折風險上升的因子包括：T-score 低於 –1.5、年齡大於 65 歲、BMI 低於 20 kg/m²、髖部骨折家族史、50 歲後曾發生脆弱性骨折之個人史、口服 corticosteroid 使用超過 6 個月、rheumatoid arthritis，以及吸菸。

> Recent data suggest that BMD measurement alone should not be the sole criterion for determining fracture risk, and that an overall fracture risk assessment that combines risk factors provides a more accurate evaluation. Use of corticosteroids at a daily dose greater than 2.5 mg of prednisolone (or equivalent) for more than 3 months is an established risk factor ... When combined with chemotherapy regimens, oral corticosteroid doses are typically higher and may negatively affect bone health over a shorter period.

近期資料顯示，不應僅以 BMD 作為判定骨折風險的唯一標準；結合多項危險因子的整體骨折風險評估能提供更準確的評價。每日使用超過 2.5 mg prednisolone（或等效劑量）之 corticosteroids 超過 3 個月，是根據非惡性疾病資料所確立的危險因子。當與化療方案合併使用時，口服 corticosteroid 劑量通常更高，且可能在更短時間內對骨骼健康產生負面影響。

> Finally ... complete baseline laboratory assessments should include serum measurements of calcium, phosphate, 25-hydroxyvitamin D, C-reactive protein, alkaline phosphatase, TSH, and gamma-glutamyl transpeptidase; complete blood cell count; creatinine clearance; and protein electrophoresis (serum and/or urine).

為辨識並處理次發性 osteoporosis (secondary causes of osteoporosis)，完整的基線實驗室評估應包括血清 calcium、phosphate、25-hydroxyvitamin D、C-reactive protein、alkaline phosphatase、TSH、gamma-glutamyl transpeptidase；complete blood cell count；creatinine clearance；以及血清與（或）尿液的 protein electrophoresis。

## 💊 治療與處置 Management

### 生活型態、鈣與維生素 D Lifestyle, Calcium & Vitamin D

> All patients starting aromatase inhibitor therapy should be advised to engage in moderate exercise, including resistance and weight-bearing activities. Although weight-bearing exercise benefits BMD, reductions in fracture risk have not been demonstrated.

所有開始 aromatase inhibitor 治療的病人都應被建議進行中等強度運動，包括阻力運動與承重運動 (resistance and weight-bearing activities)。雖然承重運動有益於 BMD，但尚未證實可降低骨折風險。

> Regarding calcium and vitamin D, the International Osteoporosis Foundation recommends a daily intake of 1200 mg of calcium and 800 to 1000 international units of vitamin D for postmenopausal women. ... For these individuals at high risk of fracture, measurement of 25-hydroxyvitamin D is recommended, and high-dose vitamin D supplementation is given if deficient. For other postmenopausal women receiving aromatase inhibitor therapy, a daily dose of at least 800 (up to 2000) international units of vitamin D is recommended ... A recent meta-analysis has underscored that vitamin D alone or with calcium supplementation is ineffective for preventing fractures in women with breast cancer.

在 calcium 與 vitamin D 方面，International Osteoporosis Foundation 建議停經後女性每日攝取 calcium 1200 mg 與 vitamin D 800 至 1000 international units。年長者、或活動量與日曬較少者，可能需更高攝取量；對這些骨折高風險者，建議測量 25-hydroxyvitamin D，若缺乏則給予高劑量 vitamin D 補充。其他接受 aromatase inhibitor 的停經後女性，建議每日至少 800（最多 2000）international units 的 vitamin D 以維持充足濃度。惟近期一項統合分析強調：單獨補充 vitamin D、或 vitamin D 合併 calcium，對預防乳癌女性骨折並無效。

### BMD 監測與治療啟動 BMD Monitoring & Initiation

> For patients initiating aromatase inhibitor therapy, BMD measurement is advised. For patients with a T-score greater than –2.0 and no other fracture risk factors, BMD and risk status should be reassessed after 12 months. If no adjuvant antiresorptive therapy is initiated, an annual decrease in BMD of 5% to 10% should prompt investigation for secondary causes of bone loss, such as vitamin D deficiency, and initiation of antiresorptive treatment.

開始 aromatase inhibitor 治療者，建議測量 BMD。若 T-score 大於 –2.0 且無其他骨折危險因子，應於 12 個月後再評估 BMD 與風險狀態。若未啟動輔助性抗骨吸收治療，當每年 BMD 下降達 5% 至 10% 時，應查找次發性骨質流失原因（如 vitamin D 缺乏），並開始抗骨吸收治療以預防骨折。

> All patients initiating or receiving aromatase inhibitor therapy with any 2 of the following risk factors should receive antiresorptive therapy: T-score less than –1.5, age older than 65 years, BMI less than 20 kg/m², family history of hip fracture, personal history of fragility fracture after age 50 years, oral corticosteroid use for more than 6 months, and current or history of smoking. Any patient ... with a T-score less than –2.0 at the spine, total hip, or femoral neck should receive antiresorptive therapy irrespective of other risk factors.

所有開始或正在接受 aromatase inhibitor 治療、且符合下列任兩項危險因子者，皆應接受抗骨吸收治療：T-score 低於 –1.5、年齡大於 65 歲、BMI 低於 20 kg/m²、髖部骨折家族史、50 歲後之脆弱性骨折個人史、口服 corticosteroid 使用超過 6 個月、目前或曾經吸菸。任何在脊椎、total hip 或 femoral neck 的 T-score 低於 –2.0 者，不論其他危險因子，皆應接受抗骨吸收治療。

### 抗骨吸收藥物 Antiresorptive Agents

> Based on current evidence, subcutaneous denosumab (60 mg twice yearly; same dosage as for primary osteoporosis) and intravenous zoledronate (4 mg every 6 months; higher dosage than for primary osteoporosis) are the preferred agents for prevention and treatment of AIBL. Risedronate, 35 mg weekly, is the bisphosphonate with the strongest evidence for preventing AIBL.

依現有證據，皮下注射 denosumab（60 mg，每年 2 次；劑量與原發性 osteoporosis 相同）與靜脈注射 zoledronate（4 mg，每 6 個月一次；劑量高於原發性 osteoporosis）為預防與治療 AIBL 的首選藥物。口服 bisphosphonate 中以 Risedronate 35 mg 每週一次的預防 AIBL 證據最強。

> In all patients receiving oral bisphosphonate therapy, BMD should be monitored, and adherence assessed every 1 to 2 years. ... In case of poor adherence or BMD loss despite treatment, after 1 to 2 years, a switch to denosumab or intravenous bisphosphonate is recommended.

所有接受口服 bisphosphonate 者，應每 1 至 2 年監測 BMD 並評估用藥順從性 (adherence)；週期性檢測骨吸收標記 (bone resorption markers) 可作為方便、非侵入性的順從性評估。若順從性不佳、或治療下仍持續骨質流失，1 至 2 年後建議改用 denosumab 或靜脈注射 bisphosphonate。

> Patients receiving aromatase inhibitor therapy are at elevated risk of fracture for at least the duration of treatment. As a result, continuing antiresorptive therapy for as long as the patient is receiving an aromatase inhibitor (up to 5-10 years) is recommended. Currently, denosumab and zoledronate are the only antiresorptive agents with proven efficacy and safety in large, long-term, randomized controlled trials.

接受 aromatase inhibitor 治療者，至少在治療期間都處於骨折風險升高狀態。因此建議只要仍在使用 aromatase inhibitor（可達 5 至 10 年），就持續進行抗骨吸收治療。目前只有 denosumab 與 zoledronate 在大型、長期隨機對照試驗中被證實具療效與安全性；選藥時應一併考量各藥物的副作用特性與處置。

### 預防疾病復發：輔助性 bisphosphonate Disease Recurrence Prevention: Adjuvant Bisphosphonates

> More than 35 randomized clinical trials have investigated the use of adjuvant bisphosphonates in early breast cancer ... Overall, treatment with these agents reduces bone metastases and breast cancer deaths in postmenopausal women, beyond their effects on bone health.

超過 35 項隨機臨床試驗探討輔助性 bisphosphonates 用於早期乳癌，結果不一。整體而言，這類藥物在停經後女性中能減少骨轉移 (bone metastases) 與乳癌死亡，效益超越其對骨骼健康的作用。此顯著的存活效益已使其被納入主要腫瘤學專業機構（European Society of Medical Oncology、American Society of Clinical Oncology）及多份國際跨領域立場聲明的骨骼健康治療指引。

> Trials comparing agents both indirectly, as in the EBCTCG meta-analysis, and directly in one large randomized trial suggest that intravenous zoledronate, daily oral ibandronate, and daily oral clodronate are similarly effective. Most guidelines recommend intravenous zoledronate, 4 mg, be started as soon as possible during (neo)adjuvant chemotherapy and continued thereafter every 6 months for at least 3 years. ... Alternatively, the patient may choose between daily oral clodronate, 1600 mg daily, or oral ibandronate, 50 mg daily.

無論是 EBCTCG 統合分析的間接比較，或一項大型隨機試驗的直接比較，皆顯示靜脈注射 zoledronate、每日口服 ibandronate、每日口服 clodronate 療效相近。多數指引建議在（新）輔助性化療期間盡早開始靜脈注射 zoledronate 4 mg，之後每 6 個月一次，持續至少 3 年。部分臨床試驗使用的密集給藥方案被認為並非必要，且與較高的藥物相關顎骨壞死 (medication-related osteonecrosis of the jaw, MRONJ) 發生率相關。替代方案為每日口服 clodronate 1600 mg，或每日口服 ibandronate 50 mg。

> Although multiple studies have confirmed the efficacy of denosumab in preserving bone health, it cannot be recommended for preventing disease recurrence in postmenopausal women with breast cancer.

雖然多項研究證實 denosumab 能維持骨骼健康，但不建議將其用於預防停經後乳癌女性的疾病復發。

| 試驗 Trial | 族群 | 主要結果 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ABCSG-18（n=3420，停經後、荷爾蒙敏感早期乳癌、接受 aromatase inhibitor） | 復發風險相對較低 | 5 年時 disease-free survival 效益 2.1%（HR 0.83，中位追蹤 8 年）；對乳癌死亡率無顯著影響 |

兩項研究結果相互矛盾，可能的原因包括研究族群、主要目標、denosumab 劑量與給藥排程的差異。denosumab 並未被納入 ASCO–Ontario Health (Cancer Care Ontario) 或 ESMO 指引的建議治療。

## 🧠 個案解析與臨床推理 Case Analysis & Clinical Reasoning

**核心概念：兩個不同但重疊的臨床目標。** 乳癌女性的抗骨吸收治療服務於兩個目標——(1) 預防 AIBL / 骨折（骨骼健康），與 (2) 預防疾病復發（腫瘤學）。這兩者的藥物選擇邏輯不同，臨床上務必分清楚：
- 當**骨折風險**為主要考量時，denosumab 與 zoledronate 皆可，denosumab 為優先。
- 當**降低疾病復發**為優先時，建議 zoledronate（denosumab 在此目的上證據不足，D-CARE 為陰性結果）。

**AIBL 與一般停經後 osteoporosis 不同。** AIBL 的骨吸收上升更劇烈，骨質流失速度為生理性停經後的 2 至 4 倍，因此不能直接套用一般 osteoporosis 的治療門檻與監測頻率。值得注意的是，zoledronate 用於 AIBL 的劑量（4 mg 每 6 個月）高於原發性 osteoporosis，而 denosumab 劑量則相同（60 mg 每年 2 次）。

**風險評估不能只看 BMD。** 停經前女性即使 BMD 尚可，若合併化療引起的續發性停經 (secondary amenorrhea)、GnRH analogue、Tamoxifen 或 aromatase inhibitor，骨折風險仍顯著升高（Case 1）。因此臨床決策應採「整體風險因子組合」而非單一 T-score。任兩項危險因子、或任一部位 T-score ≤ –2.0，即為啟動藥物治療的門檻。

**Tamoxifen 的「矛盾 (paradox)」。** 同一藥物在不同停經狀態下對骨骼的作用相反：停經前使用 Tamoxifen 骨折風險加倍，停經後則因其部分 estrogen 促效作用而保護骨骼。這是 premenopausal 與 postmenopausal 病人推理上必須區分的關鍵點。

**鑑別診斷與陷阱 Differential diagnosis & pitfalls：**
- 啟動治療前務必排除次發性 osteoporosis：vitamin D 缺乏、副甲狀腺、甲狀腺（TSH）、腎功能 (creatinine clearance)、multiple myeloma（protein electrophoresis）等。BMD 每年下降 5%–10% 應觸發次發原因的查找。
- 陷阱一：僅補充 calcium 與 vitamin D 而不給藥。統合分析顯示，單靠 vitamin D（含合併 calcium）無法預防乳癌女性骨折——補充劑是基礎但非充分治療。
- 陷阱二：忽略化療常合併的 glucocorticoid 負擔。化療方案中的類固醇劑量通常較高，可在較短期間內損害骨骼（如 Case 1、Case 3）。
- 陷阱三：denosumab 停藥後的反彈性骨吸收與骨折風險——雖原文未展開，但選擇 denosumab 時須規劃銜接策略，並確保長期順從性。
- 陷阱四：zoledronate 過度密集給藥會提高 MRONJ 風險而無額外療效。

**療程長度的邏輯：** 只要病人仍在使用 aromatase inhibitor（可達 5–10 年），抗骨吸收治療就應持續。停藥後雖 BMD 部分回升，但骨微結構惡化持續，骨折風險長期偏高，因此追蹤不應在停用 aromatase inhibitor 後立即中止。

## ⭐ 重點整理 Key Takeaways

- 停經前乳癌女性若有化療引起的續發性停經 (chemotherapy-induced secondary amenorrhea)，或接受 GnRH/Tamoxifen 或 GnRH/aromatase inhibitor 治療，其 osteoporosis 與骨折風險升高。
- 停經後乳癌女性接受 aromatase inhibitor 內分泌治療者，osteoporosis 與骨折風險升高；AIBL 的骨質流失速度為生理性停經後的 2 至 4 倍。
- Tamoxifen 具停經狀態依賴的「矛盾」效應：停經前增加骨折風險（約 2 倍），停經後則保護骨骼。
- 風險評估應結合多項臨床危險因子而非僅看 BMD；符合任兩項危險因子、或 T-score ≤ –2.0，即應啟動 antiresorptive therapy。
- AIBL 的首選藥物為 denosumab（60 mg，每年 2 次）與 zoledronate（4 mg，每 6 個月）；口服選項中 Risedronate 35 mg 每週一次證據最強。
- 輔助性 bisphosphonate 不僅預防 osteoporosis 與骨折，還能在停經後女性降低骨轉移與乳癌死亡（降低復發）；denosumab 則不建議用於預防復發。
- 當降低復發為優先時選 zoledronate（4 mg，每 6 個月，至少 3 年）；當骨折風險為主要考量時選 denosumab。
- 單獨補充 vitamin D（含合併 calcium）無法預防乳癌女性骨折——為基礎措施，非充分治療。
