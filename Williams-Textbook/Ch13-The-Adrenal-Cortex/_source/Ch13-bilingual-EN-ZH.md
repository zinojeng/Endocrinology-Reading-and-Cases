## Key Points / 重點摘要

**EN:**
- The adrenal cortex produces three major classes of steroid hormones: mineralocorticoids (aldosterone from zona glomerulosa), glucocorticoids (cortisol from zona fasciculata), and adrenal androgens (DHEA/DHEAS from zona reticularis).
- Steroidogenesis is mediated by cytochrome P450 enzymes and hydroxysteroid dehydrogenases, with StAR protein mediating the rate-limiting transport of cholesterol across the mitochondrial membrane.
- Primary adrenocortical insufficiency (Addison disease) is characterized by low cortisol, high ACTH, hyperkalemia, hyponatremia, and hyperpigmentation; autoimmune adrenalitis (anti-21-hydroxylase antibodies) is the leading cause.
- Cushing syndrome diagnosis requires confirmation of hypercortisolemia (24h UFC, midnight salivary cortisol, 1mg DST) followed by ACTH determination to distinguish ACTH-dependent (Cushing disease, ectopic ACTH) from ACTH-independent (adrenal adenoma/carcinoma/hyperplasia) etiologies.
- Primary aldosteronism is screened using aldosterone-to-renin ratio (ARR >20-30 ng/dL per ng/mL/h), confirmed by suppression testing, and subtyped by adrenal vein sampling (AVS).
- Adrenal incidentalomas require hormonal evaluation (subclinical Cushing, PA, pheochromocytoma) and unenhanced CT attenuation imaging (<10 HU indicates benign adenoma).
- Congenital adrenal hyperplasia (CAH) is most commonly caused by 21-hydroxylase deficiency (CYP21A2 mutation), presenting as salt-wasting, simple virilizing, or nonclassic forms.

**中文：**
- 腎上腺皮質分泌三大類類固醇激素：鹽皮質素（由絲狀帶生成之醛固酮）、糖皮質素（由束狀帶生成之皮質醇）及腎上腺雄性素（由網狀帶生成之 DHEA/DHEAS）。
- 類固醇合成由細胞色素 P450 酶與羥基類固醇脫氫酶介導，其中 StAR 蛋白介導膽固醇跨線粒體膜的限速轉運。
- 原發性腎上腺皮質不全（Addison 病）以低皮質醇、高 ACTH、高血鉀、低血鈉及皮膚色素沉著為特徵；自體免疫性腎上腺炎（抗 21-羥化酶抗體）為最主要原因。
- 庫欣氏症候群診斷須先確認高皮質醇血症（24小時尿液游離皮質醇、午夜唾液皮質醇、1mg 德沙美松抑制試驗），再檢測 ACTH 以鑑別 ACTH 依賴型（庫欣氏病、異位 ACTH）與 ACTH 非依賴型（腎上腺腺瘤/皮質癌/增生）。
- 原發性醛固酮過多症（PA）利用醛固酮-腎素比值（ARR >20-30）進行篩檢，經確認試驗驗證後，由腎上腺靜脈採樣（AVS）進行分型。
- 腎上腺偶發瘤（incidentaloma）需要進行內分泌功能評估（亞臨床庫欣、PA、嗜鉻細胞瘤）與非對比 CT 衰減度影像評估（<10 HU 提示良性腺瘤）。
- 先天性腎上腺增生症（CAH）最常見原因為 21-羥化酶缺乏症（CYP21A2 基因突變），表現為失鹽型、單純男性化型或非典型型。

## 1. Historical Milestones & Anatomy / 歷史里程碑與解剖發育

**EN:**
The adrenal cortex originates from the intermediate mesoderm within the urogenital ridge. The adult adrenal cortex comprises three histologically and functionally distinct concentric zones: the outer zona glomerulosa (mineralocorticoids), the middle zona fasciculata (glucocorticoids), and the inner zona reticularis (adrenal androgens). Cortisol secretion is under the control of the hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal (HPA) axis via corticotropin-releasing hormone (CRH) and adrenocorticotropic hormone (ACTH), exhibiting a prominent circadian rhythm.

**中文：**
腎上腺皮質源自中胚層的中間中胚層（中間尿生殖脊）。成熟的腎上腺皮質包含三個在組織學與功能上截然不同的同心區域：外層的絲狀帶（zona glomerulosa，合成鹽皮質素）、中層的束狀帶（zona fasciculata，合成糖皮質素）及內層的網狀帶（zona reticularis，合成腎上腺雄性素）。皮質醇的分泌受下視丘-腦下垂體-腎上腺（HPA）軸經由 CRH 與 ACTH 的調控，並展現顯著的日夜節律。

## 2. Adrenal Steroids and Steroidogenesis / 腎上腺類固醇與類固醇合成

**EN:**
Steroidogenesis begins with cholesterol import into mitochondria by StAR (steroidogenic acute regulatory protein), followed by cleavage to pregnenolone by CYP11A1 (P450scc). Cortisol synthesis requires CYP17A1 (17α-hydroxylase/17,20-lyase), 3β-HSD, CYP21A2 (21-hydroxylase), and CYP11B1 (11β-hydroxylase). Aldosterone synthesis in the zona glomerulosa requires CYP21A2 and CYP11B2 (aldosterone synthase). In 11β-HSD2 deficiency or inhibition by liquorice (glycyrrhizic acid), cortisol acts on mineralocorticoid receptors, causing apparent mineralocorticoid excess (AME).

**中文：**
類固醇合成始於 StAR 蛋白質將膽固醇轉運至線粒體內膜，隨後由 CYP11A1（P450scc）側鏈裂解生成孕烯醇酮。皮質醇合成需要 CYP17A1（17α-羥化酶/17,20-裂解酶）、3β-HSD、CYP21A2（21-羥化酶）與 CYP11B1（11β-羥化酶）。絲狀帶中醛固酮的合成則需要 CYP21A2 與 CYP11B2（醛固酮合成酶）。在 11β-HSD2 缺乏或受甘草酸（glycyrrhizic acid）抑制時，皮質醇會大量活化鹽皮質素受體，導致表觀鹽皮質素過多症（AME）。

## 3. Primary Adrenocortical Insufficiency / 原發性腎上腺皮質不全

**EN:**
Primary adrenal insufficiency (Addison disease) results from destruction or dysfunction of the adrenal cortex. Autoimmune adrenalitis accounts for ~80–90% of cases in developed nations, associated with 21-hydroxylase autoantibodies and autoimmune polyendocrine syndromes (APS-1, APS-2). Clinical features include fatigue, weight loss, hyperpigmentation (due to elevated POMC/ACTH cleavage products acting on MC1R), hyponatremia, hyperkalemia, and postural hypotension. Diagnosis is confirmed by low morning serum cortisol (<3 μg/dL) or peak cortisol <18 μg/dL after a standard 250 μg ACTH stimulation test (cosyntropin test). Acute adrenal crisis is a life-threatening medical emergency requiring immediate IV hydrocortisone (100 mg bolus) and isotonic saline resuscitation.

**中文：**
原發性腎上腺皮質不全（Addison 病）導因於腎上腺皮質的破壞或功能障礙。在已開發國家中，自體免疫性腎上腺炎佔約 80–90% 的病例，常與抗 21-羥化酶自體抗體及自體免疫多腺體症候群（APS-1, APS-2）相關。臨床表現包括疲倦、體重減輕、皮膚色素沉著（因 POMC/ACTH 裂解產物作用於 MC1R 所致）、低血鈉、高血鉀與姿勢性低血壓。診斷由早晨清晨血清皮質醇低下（<3 μg/dL）或標準 250 μg ACTH 刺激試驗後峰值皮質醇 <18 μg/dL 確認。急性腎上腺危機（adrenal crisis）為致命急症，須立即給予靜脈水合皮質醇（100 mg 靜脈衝擊）及等滲透壓生理食鹽水復甦。

## 4. Cushing Syndrome / 庫欣氏症候群

**EN:**
Cushing syndrome encompasses clinical features resulting from chronic glucocorticoid excess. First-line screening tests include 24-hour urinary free cortisol (UFC > ULN), late-night salivary cortisol (>1.45 ng/mL or >4 nmol/L), and overnight 1-mg dexamethasone suppression test (DST cortisol >1.8 μg/dL). Once hypercortisolemia is established, plasma ACTH distinguishes ACTH-dependent (<10 pg/mL indicates ACTH-independent adrenal etiology; >20 pg/mL indicates ACTH-dependent etiology). High-dose (8 mg) DST and inferior petrosal sinus sampling (IPSS with CRH stimulation; central-to-peripheral ratio ≥2 baseline or ≥3 post-CRH) differentiate pituitary Cushing disease from ectopic ACTH secretion.

**中文：**
庫欣氏症候群包含長期糖皮質素過多引起的臨床表現。第一線篩檢試驗包括 24 小時尿液游離皮質醇（UFC > 上限）、深夜唾液皮質醇（>1.45 ng/mL）及過夜 1-mg 德沙美松抑制試驗（DST 後皮質醇 >1.8 μg/dL）。一旦確診高皮質醇血症，血漿 ACTH 測定可用於區分 ACTH 依賴型（<10 pg/mL 提示 ACTH 非依賴性腎上腺病因；>20 pg/mL 提示 ACTH 依賴型病因）。高劑量（8 mg）DST 與下岩靜脈竇採樣（IPSS 配合 CRH 刺激；中央對周邊比值基線 ≥2 或 CRH 後 ≥3）可用於鑑別腦下垂體庫欣氏病與異位 ACTH 分泌。

## 5. Primary Aldosteronism / 原發性醛固酮過多症

**EN:**
Primary aldosteronism (PA) is the most common cause of secondary hypertension. Screening is indicated in patients with resistant hypertension, severe hypertension, hypertension with hypokalemia, or adrenal incidentaloma, using the plasma aldosterone-to-renin ratio (ARR >20–30 ng/dL per ng/mL/h with plasma aldosterone >15 ng/dL). Confirmatory tests include oral sodium loading, saline infusion test (post-infusion aldosterone >10 ng/dL confirms PA; <5 ng/dL excludes), or fludrocortisone suppression test. Subtyping by adrenal vein sampling (AVS) with lateralization index >4:1 confirms unilateral aldosterone-producing adenoma (APA) amenable to laparoscopic adrenalectomy.

**中文：**
原發性醛固酮過多症（PA）是次發性高血壓最常見的原因。建議在抗藥性高血壓、嚴重高血壓、伴低血鉀的高血壓或腎上腺偶發瘤患者中，使用血漿醛固酮對腎素比值（ARR >20–30 且醛固酮 >15 ng/dL）進行篩檢。確認試驗包括口服鈉負荷試驗、靜脈食鹽水輸注試驗（輸注後醛固酮 >10 ng/dL 確診 PA；<5 ng/dL 排除）或氟氫可的松抑制試驗。利用腎上腺靜脈採樣（AVS）進行次分型，側化指數（lateralization index）>4:1 可確認單側醛固酮分泌腺瘤（APA），適用於腹腔鏡腎上腺切除術。

## 6. Adrenal Incidentaloma & Adrenocortical Carcinoma / 腎上腺偶發瘤與皮質癌

**EN:**
An adrenal incidentaloma is an unsuspected adrenal mass ≥1 cm detected on imaging performed for unrelated reasons. All incidentalomas require evaluation for autonomous hormone secretion (1-mg DST for subclinical Cushing, ARR for PA if hypertensive, 24h plasma/urinary metanephrines for pheochromocytoma). Malignancy risk is evaluated using unenhanced CT attenuation (<10 Hounsfield Units indicates lipid-rich benign adenoma; >20 HU or delayed contrast washout <50% suggests potential malignancy/ACC). Adrenocortical carcinoma (ACC) is a rare, aggressive tumor often presenting with mixed steroid excess (cortisol + androgens) and large size (>4 cm).

**中文：**
腎上腺偶發瘤（adrenal incidentaloma）是指因非腎上腺相關原因進行影像檢查時意外發現的 ≥1 cm 腎上腺腫塊。所有偶發瘤均須評估自主性激素分泌（1-mg DST 評估亞臨床庫欣、高血壓者檢測 ARR、24小時血/尿 metanephrines 排除嗜鉻細胞瘤）。惡性風險經由非對比 CT 衰減度評估（<10 HU 提示富含脂質之良性腺瘤；>20 HU 或對比劑洗退率 <50% 提示潛在惡性腫瘤/皮質癌）。腎上腺皮質癌（ACC）為罕見且高度侵襲性腫瘤，常表現為混合性類固醇過多（皮質醇 + 雄性素）及巨大腫塊（>4 cm）。

## 7. Congenital Adrenal Hyperplasia / 先天性腎上腺增生症

**EN:**
Congenital adrenal hyperplasia (CAH) refers to a group of autosomal recessive disorders caused by enzymatic defects in cortisol biosynthesis. 21-hydroxylase deficiency (CYP21A2) accounts for >90% of cases, leading to impaired cortisol/aldosterone synthesis and accumulation of 17-hydroxyprogesterone (17-OHP), which is shunted into androgen pathways. Clinical phenotypes include classic salt-wasting (SW, severe enzyme deficiency, neonatal salt crisis), classic simple virilizing (SV, ambiguous genitalia in 46,XX females), and nonclassic CAH (NC-CAH, late-onset hyperandrogenism). Newborn screening relies on elevated 17-OHP levels. Treatment requires glucocorticoid (hydrocortisone) and mineralocorticoid (fludrocortisone) replacement.

**中文：**
先天性腎上腺增生症（CAH）是一組因皮質醇生物合成酶缺陷導致的體體隱性遺傳疾病。21-羥化酶缺乏症（CYP21A2 基因突變）佔 90% 以上的病例，導致皮質醇/醛固酮合成受阻，並引起 17-羥孕酮（17-OHP）堆積並分流至雄性素合成途徑。臨床表型包括典型失鹽型（SW，嚴重酶缺乏，新生兒期鹽危機）、典型單純男性化型（SV，46,XX 女性外生殖器模糊）及非典型型（NC-CAH，遲發型雄性素過高）。新生兒篩檢依賴於血清 17-OHP 濃度升高。治療需要糖皮質素（水合皮質醇）與鹽皮質素（氟氫可的松）替代治療。
